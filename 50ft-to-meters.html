30. A 50ft rope is cut into two pieces. The length of one piece is 9 times the length of the other. what is the length of the longer piece?show solution in RESCA​


The length of the longer piece is 45 ft

[tex]\\[/tex]

Step-by-step explanation:

[tex]\\[/tex]

Given:

Let l be the total length, l = 50 ft

let x be the length of the shorter piece

Let 9x be the length of the longer piece

[tex]\\[/tex]

Required:

Length of the longer piece

[tex]\\[/tex]

Equation:

l = x + 9x

[tex]\\[/tex]

Solution/Calculation:Find the length of the shorter piece

Substitute the value of l which is 50 ft

l = x + 9x

50 ft = x + 9x

50 ft = 10x

x = [tex]\frac{50 ft}{10}[/tex]

x = 5 ft

[tex]\\[/tex]

Find the length of the longer piece

= 9x

= 9(5 ft)

= 45 ft

[tex]\\[/tex]

Answer:The length of the longer piece is 45 ft
