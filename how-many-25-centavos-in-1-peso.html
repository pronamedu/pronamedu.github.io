2. how many 25 centavos are there in 5 peso coins


Answer:

There are 20 pieces of 25 centavo coins needed to make a 5 peso coin.

Further Explanation:

The process of solving the problem becomes easier when we employ a mathematical equation that would work in a given situation.

Mathematical equation models the situation in a given problem in which would give a precise answer when it's solved.

Steps in Constructing a Mathematical Equation

1. Assume a variable for the unknown value.

2. Set up the equation based on the problem

3. Solve the unknown variable by applying the appropriate mathematical laws.

Let [tex]x[/tex] be the number of 25 centavos.

Remember that 25 centavos is [tex]0.25[/tex].

So the mathematical equation would be the one below.

                                         [tex]0.25x=5[/tex]

Steps in Solving the Equation

1. Divide both sides by 0.25

2. Simplify the left side.

3. Write a conclusion.

                                         [tex]\begin{aligned}0.25x&=5\\\frac{0.25x}{0.25}&=\frac{5}{0.25}\\x&=20\end{aligned}[/tex]

This means that there should be 20 pieces of 25 centavo coins to make 5 peso coins.
