27. 7. A box of milk is 875 cubic cm. 1 point What is the volume of the box in cubic meters?​


Answer :

7. A box of milk is 875 cubic cm.

What is the volume of the box in cubic meters?

Here are the basic conversation rules when converting cu.cm to cu.m and vice versa :

We multiply when converting from a larger unit to a smaller unit.We divide when converting from a smaller unit to a larger unit.

In converting cu.cm (cm³) to cu.m (m³) and vice versa, we can use the Conversation Rule:

1 m = 100 cm

Multiply both sides by itself three times

(1 m = 100 cm) 3

When multiplying both sides by 3, the answer will become 3m = 300cm but when multiplied both sides by itself three times it becomes

1m × 1m × 1m = 100cm × 100cm × 100cm thus

1m³ = 1,000,000cm³

To answer the problem above, we can apply the conversation rule:

→ Divide it by 1,000,000

875 ÷ 1,000,000 = ______ m³

[tex] \frac{875}{1000000} = 0.000875[/tex]

The equivalent volume of the box in cubic meters is 0.000875m³
