26. Find the GCF of 36, 60 and 72


Answer:

4

Step-by-step explanation:

The factors of 36 are 1, 2, 3, 4, 6, 9, 12, 18, and 36.

The factors of 60 are 1, 2, 3, 4, 5, 6, 10, 12, 15, 20, 30, and 60.

The factors of 72 are 1, 2, 3, 4, 6, 8, 9, 12, 18, 24, 36, and 72.

The common factors are 1, 2, 3, 4, 6, and 12. Therefore, the greatest common factor is 12.

NOTE: Since 36 is a factor of 72, the greatest common factor of 36, 60, and 72 is the same as the greatest common factor of 36 and 60.
