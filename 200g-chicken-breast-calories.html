1. What is a chicken’s airline breast? *a. A chicken breast with an attached first wing boneb. A chicken breast that is butterfliedc. A chicken breast that is debonedd. A chicken will cut into cubes​


Answer:

A.

Explanation:

Airline chicken is a food dish composed of a boneless chicken breast with the drumette attached. The breast is skin-on, and the first wing joint and tender are attached while the rest of the breast is boneless. The cut is also known as a frenched breast, due to the end of the wing bone getting trimmed.

18. Why the leg and thigh muscles have dark meat compared to breast muscle of chicken?​


Answer:

Explanation:

Chickens use their legs for walking. The higher activity of the leg muscles increases the muscles' need for oxygen. The darker color of more active muscles comes from a chemical compound in the muscle called myoglobin, which is important for oxygen transport.
