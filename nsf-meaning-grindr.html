6. Bank Reconciliation ABC Trading's records at the end January, 2022 showed the following: Cash balance per Bank Statement P81,000 Cash balance per General Ledger P77,700 Bank service charge P1,200 Outstanding checks as of January 31 P18,000 NSF check returned by the bank P1,680 Cash receipts of P15,600 recorded in the books in January does not appear in the bank statement. A credit memo in the amount of P3,780 represents a customer's note collected by the bank. Required: a. Prepare Bank Reconciliation Statement. b. Prepare Adjusting Journal Entries.


Her is the solution for a. Prepare Bank Reconciliation Statement:

Bank Reconciliation Statement As of January 31, 2022

Cash balance per Bank Statement: P81,000

Add: Outstanding checks: P18,000

Less: NSF check returned by the back: (1,680)

Adjusted cash balance per Bank Statement: P97,320

Less: Cash balance per General Ledger (77,700)

Add: Cash receipts not appearing in the bank statement 15,600

Add: Credit memo from customer's note collected by the bank 3,780

Adjusted cash balance per General Ledger: P41,800

The adjusted cash balance per the bank statement is P97,320 and the adjusted cash balance per the general ledger is P41,800.

Here's the solution for b. Prepare Adjusting Journal Entries:

Adjusting Journal Entries

Debit Credit

Cash: 77,700

Bank Service Charge: 1,200

Outstanding checks: 18,000

NSF check returned by the bank: 1,680

Cash Receipts: 15,600

Credit Memo from customer's note collected by the bank: 3,780

Bank: 97,320

The first entry debits Cash and Bank Service Charge for the difference between the cash balance per the bank statement and the cash balance per the general ledger, and credits Bank for the same amount.

The second entry debits Outstanding checks and NSF check returned by the bank for the difference between the adjusted cash balance per the bank statement and the adjusted cash balance per the general ledger, and credits Cash Receipts and Credit Memo from customer's note collected by the bank for the same amount[tex].[/tex]
