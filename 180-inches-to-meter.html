12. find the lengths of sides of a square table whose diagonal is 180 inches​


Problem:Find the lengths of sides of a square table whose diagonal is 180 inches​

Given:

diagonal = 180 inches

Required:

length of the side of a square table

Solution:

the characteristic of the square is that all of its 4 sides are equal

by using Pythagorean theorem,

Let x be the side of the square

c² = a² + b²

180² = x² + x²

32400 = 2x²

16200 = x²

x = √16200

x = 127.28 inches

Answer:

length of the side of a square table = 127.28 inches

14. find the length of the side of square table whose diagonal is 180 inches​


In the given problem, apply your knowledge in algebra and basic mathematics specially in solving word problem about plane figures.

Given Statement

Find the length of the side of square table whose diagonal is 180 inches.

Formula

Remember, the solving the length of a diagonal of the square and rectangle is the same to the formula of pythagorean theorem.

c² = a² + b² → d² = l² + w² → d² = s² + s² or d² = 2s²

Solution

d² = 2s²

d = 180

180² = 2s²

32400 = 2s²

s² = 16200

s = √16200

s = 90√2

Answer

The side of the square table is 90√2 inches or 127.28 inches.

26. Calculate the average speed in meter seconds if. A cheetah runs 180 meters in 10 seconds.

[tex]\green{••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••}[/tex]

PROBLEM

Calculate the average speed in meter seconds if. A cheetah runs 180 meters in 10 seconds.

[tex]\green{••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••}[/tex]

[tex] \sf Speed(s) = \frac{Distance(d)}{Time(t)} [/tex]

[tex] \sf s = \frac{180 \: meters}{10 \: seconds} [/tex]

[tex] \sf s = 18 \: m/s[/tex]
