6. An airplane travels 4000m in 16 seconds on a heading of 35º What is its velocity?Show your work​


Answer:

1. velocity = displacement/elapsed time

2. therefore, the velocity = 4000/16 m/s = 250 m/s

3. since velocity is a vector, we must include the direction with its speed of 250 m/s

4. hence, the velocity = 250 m/s, 35° north of east, assuming that it is traveling in the positive x direction

11. katie traveled southwest a distance of 4000m on a bicycle in 5400s. what is her velocity?​


Answer:

v = 0.74 m/s

Step-by-step explanation:

It is given that,

Distance covered by Katie, d = 4000 m

Time taken by Katie, t = 5400 s

Let v is the velocity of Katie. It can be calculated suing the definition as the total distance covered divided by total time taken. mathematically, it is given by :

v = 0.74 m/s (in south west direction)

So, the velocity of Katie is 0.74 m/s
