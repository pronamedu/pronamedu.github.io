29. Reflect on your own communicative style? justify vour answer (Write in paragraphform with at least 50 words)​


Answer:

1.) Passive

Individuals who use the passive communication style often act indifferently, yielding to others. Passive communicators often display a lack of eye contact, poor body posture and an inability to say “no.”

2.) Aggressive

It’s often apparent when someone communicates in an aggressive manner. You’ll hear it. You’ll see it. You may even feel it.

3.) Assertive

Thought to be the most effective form of communication, the assertive communication style features an open communication link while not being overbearing. Assertive communicators can express their own needs, desires, ideas and feelings, while also considering the needs of others.

4.) Passive-Aggressive

Passive-aggressive communication style users appear passive on the surface, but within he or she may feel powerless or stuck, building up a resentment that leads to seething or acting out in subtle, indirect or secret ways.

>How to Become an Assertive Communicator<

>>Understanding how others communicate can be key to getting your message across to them. In order to develop a more assertive communication style, here are a few tips to keep in mind:

* Take ownership (use “I” statements)

* Maintain eye contact

* Learn to say “no”

* Voice your needs and desires confidently
