Step-by-step explanation:

Let 4 be 3 8/8 because you were subtracting a mixed fraction and the denominator of the fraction is 8.

So start subtracting with the fraction,

8/8 - 5/8 = 3/8

Then subtract 1 from your whole number 3

3 - 1 = 2

Then combine your whole number and your fraction

So the answer is 2 3/8
