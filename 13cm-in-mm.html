Problem:

If a rectangle has a length of 13cm less than five times its width and its perimeter is 82cm, what are the dimensions?

a. L=42cm and W = 13cm

b. L=42cm and W = 9cm

c. L=32cm and W = 13cm

d. L=32cm and W = 9cm

Given:

a rectangle has a length of 13cm less than five times its width:

L = 5W - 13

perimeter is 82cm

P = 82

Solution:

P = 2L + 2W

82 = 2(5W - 13) + 2W

82 = 10W - 26 + 2W

108 = 12W

W = 9 cm

L = 5W - 13

L = 5(9) - 13

L = 45 - 13

L = 32 cm

Ans. D

Step-by-step explanation:

A = 2 (1 + √2) s²

A = 2 (1 + √2) 13²

A = 2 (1 + 1.41) 169

A = 2 (2.41) 169

A = 814.58

A = 815 cm²

Answer:

816 cm²

Step-by-step explanation:

The formula to find the area of a octagon is 2 (1 + √2)a² where "a" is the length of the side.

We are given that the side is 13 cm long, therefore a = 13 cm.

Find the area:

We find the area by substituting a = 13 into the formula, 2 (1 + √2)a²

Area = 2 (1 + √2)a²

Area = 2 (1 + √2)(13)²

Area = 816 cm²
