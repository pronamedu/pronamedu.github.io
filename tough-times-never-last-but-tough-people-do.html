5. What do you do when life get tough on you?​


Answer:

1. Understand that things will change

2. Hardships are necessary

3. Look from a positive point of view

4. There are no failures, only lessons

5. Focus on what you can control

Explanation:

1.Understand that things will change- One of the most important ideas when going through hard times in life is to understand that things will never stay the same forever. If you are facing tough times right now, don’t worry because things will never stay the same.2.Hardship are necessary- Yes, difficult times, challenges, setbacks, failures, and defeats are a necessary step in life. Regardless of whom you are or what you want to accomplish in life, these are the common things that you need to go through. It is the norm of life.3.Look from a positive point of view- This may be easier said than done. But the saying, “Every cloud has a silver lining” is true. You can’t see the positive in the situation because you haven’t found it yet.4.There are no failures, only lessons-  From what you have learned above, there are no failures, only lessons. Even if you can’t find the opportunity hidden within the problem, it is alright as long as you treat the failure as feedback and fail forward.5.Focus on what you can control- Sometimes, situations are beyond our control and no matter what we do, we cannot change anything about it.
