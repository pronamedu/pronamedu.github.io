6. If 200 is divided in the ratio 1:2:5, what is the middle number? A. 25 B. 50 C. 75 D. 125


Answer:

B. 50 is the middle number

Step-by-step explanation:

Let x be the 1st number

2x be the 2nd number

5x be the 3rd number

And equate the following:

200 = x + 2x +5x

200 = 8x

25 = x

And then the numbers would be:

First number: x = 25

2nd number: 2x = 2(25)

                           = 50

3rd number: 5x = 5(25)

                          = 125

Therefore the middle number is 50.

19. Divide 5x⁴ + 20x³ -30x² + 125 by x+5​


The quotient is 5x³ + 5x² - 10x + 25 with a remainder of 0.

To see this, we can use polynomial long division:

5x⁴    | / (x+5)

5x³    | - 5x⁴

______ |

-25x³  |

______ |

20x³   | / (x+5)

20x³   | + 25x³

______ |

-5x²   |

______ |

-30x²  | / (x+5)

-30x²  | + 50x²

______ |

20x    |

______ |

125    | / (x+5)

125    | + 100

______ |

0

So the quotient is 5x³ + 5x² - 10x + 25 and the remainder is 0, giving us the final equation:

5x⁴ + 20x³ -30x² + 125 = (x+5)(5x³ + 5x² - 10x + 25) + 0
