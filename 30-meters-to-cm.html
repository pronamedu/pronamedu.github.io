To get the ratio in the problem shown:

First, since a single meter is 100 centimeters, we need to multiply 100 and 2.

100 x 2 = 200.

Second, arrange the ratio structure, which would be 30:200, but we still need to make it lowest term, so to do that, divide 30 and 200 by 10, which gives us 3:20.

Therefore, the ratio of measurements of 30 cm to 2 m is 3:20.
