Direct speech is used when we want to repeat or describe what someone said.

Examples:  

Ramon said, “I like to travel the world.”

I said, “We are studying for the final exam.”

Indirect speech is used when we want to report what someone said without quotation marks and without using exactly the same words.

Examples:

Ramon said that he liked to travel the world.
