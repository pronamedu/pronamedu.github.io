28. A picture inside a frame is 2 inches longer than it is wide. the pictureis in a frame that has a width of 3 inches on each side of the picture.If the area of the picture including the frame is 195 square inches find the dimension of the frame.​


Answer:

The dimensions of the frame are 15 in x 13 in

Step-by-step explanation:

Let

x ----> the length of the picture

y ----> the width of the picture

we know that

x=2+yx=2+y -----> equation A

The area of the picture, including the frame is

A=(x+6)(y+6)A=(x+6)(y+6)

A=195\ in^2A=195 in2

so

195=(x+6)(y+6)195=(x+6)(y+6) ----> equation B

substitute equation A in equation B

195=(2+y+6)(y+6)195=(2+y+6)(y+6)

solve for y

\begin{gathered}195=(y+8)(y+6)\\y^2+6y+8y+48=195\\\\y^2+14y-147=0\end{gathered}195=(y+8)(y+6)y2+6y+8y+48=195y2+14y−147=0

solve the quadratic equation by graphing

The solution is y=7 in

see the attached figure

Find the value of x

x=2+7=9\ inx=2+7=9 in

Find the dimensions of the frame

x+6=9+6=15\ inx+6=9+6=15 in

y+6=7+6=13\ iny+6=7+6=13 in

therefore

The dimensions of the frame are 15 in x 13 in
