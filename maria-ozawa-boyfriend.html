Si Vice Admiral Ozawa Jisaburō ay ipinanganak sa kanayunan ng Koyu County, Miyazaki prefecture sa isla ng Kyūshū, Japan. Nagtapos si Ozawa sa ika-37 klase ng Imperial Japanese Naval Academy noong Nobyembre 19, 1909, na inilagay ang ika-45 sa isang klase ng 179 na kadete. Ginawa niya ang kanyang midshipman service sa mga cruiser na Soya at Kasuga at battleship Mikasa, at inatasan ng isang ensign noong 15 Disyembre 1910. Si Ozawa ay isa sa mga nangungunang tagapagtaguyod ng naval aviation sa Imperial Japanese Navy. Si Ozawa ang kauna-unahang mataas na opisyal na nagrekomenda na ang mga hukbong carrier ng sasakyang panghimpapawid ng Japan ay organisahin sa isang air fleet upang sila ay makapagsanay at lumaban nang sama-sama. Matapos ang pag-atake sa Pearl Harbor, naging responsable si Ozawa sa mga operasyong pandagat ng Japan sa South China Sea bilang Commander in Chief ng Southern Expeditionary Fleet na sumasaklaw sa pagsalakay sa Malaya. Noong unang bahagi ng 1942 (Enero hanggang Marso), ang kanyang armada ay kasangkot sa mga pagsalakay sa Java at Sumatra.

Noong Marso–Abril 1942 pinamunuan niya ang lubos na matagumpay na commerce raiding detachment sa panahon ng pandarambong sa Indian Ocean.

Noong Nobyembre 1942, si Ozawa ay hinirang na Commander in Chief ,ng IJN 3rd Fleet, na pinaalis si Admiral Chūichi Nagumo bilang kumander ng mga hukbong carrier ng Japan. Gayunpaman, ginampanan ni Ozawa ang kanyang tungkulin nang matalino at propesyonal hanggang sa katapusan, kahit na tinapos ng kanyang fleet ang kanyang karera sa pakikipaglaban sa Pilipinas bilang kaunti lamang sa isang puwersa ng pain, walang laman ang mga flight deck dahil sa kakulangan ng mga eroplano at piloto. Noong 29 Mayo 1945, tinanggap niya ang isang posisyon sa Imperial Japanese Navy General Staff, at naging huling CINC ng Imperial Japanese Navy hanggang sa katapusan ng digmaan noong Setyembre 1945. 5. . Sandra and her boyfriend had a fight. She slapped her boyfriend at an angle of 400 and landed 0.31 m away from her. With what velocity did Sandra slap her boyfriend if the contact time is 0.99 seconds?


To solve for the Velocity or Initial Velocity that Sandra had to slap her boyfriend, first you must look at the given.  

The given is an angle of 40 degrees so that will be your theta, next, you have the range or the distance of Sandra's Boyfriend from Sandra after the slap which is 0.31m, after that, the contact time or total time of 0.99s.  

With all of this, what's missing to complete the information is the Velocity which is what's being asked.

To find what is asked, you must look at the given which is:

Range (R) = 0.31m

Total time or Contact Time (Tt) = 0.99s

Theta (θ) = 40°

In the formulas used in Projectile Motion at an Angle, the formula that has range is R = VxTt. So we have to use formula and derive from it. R = VxTt will become R/Tt = Vx because we are trying to find the Initial Velocity of the slap. So it is 0.31m/0.99s = Vx and the Vx is equal to 0.31m/s, but we are not done yet. That is the Horizontal Velocity but we are trying to find the Initial Velocity.

Formula:

[tex]Vx = \frac{R}{Tt}[/tex]

Solution:

[tex]Vx =\frac{0.31}{0.99}[/tex]

[tex]Vx = 0.31[/tex]

Final Answer:

[tex]Vx = 0.31 m/s[/tex]

To find the Initial Velocity, we must use another formula. The formula that we need to use next is Vx = Vi cos(Theta) and derive the needed formula that we need. It will become Vx/cos(Theta) = Vi because Vi is missing and we have a given of Vx and Theta. The solution is now 0.31m/s divided by cosine 40. By doing that, the answer will become Vi = 0.4m/s.

Formula:

[tex]Vi = \frac{Vx}{cos}[/tex](_θ)

Solution:

[tex]Vi = \frac{0.31}{cos40}[/tex]

[tex]vi = 0.4[/tex]

Final Answer:

[tex]vi = 0.4 m/s[/tex]

*In using significant figures in problems like this, the number of significant figures that you will use in your final answer is the least number of significant figures in the given. In this problem, both given have one significant figure, therefore, we will also use one in the given.

The INITIAL VELOCITY of Sandra's slap to her boyfriend is 0.4m/s
