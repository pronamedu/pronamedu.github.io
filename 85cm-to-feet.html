9. now lets solve this word problem the tablecloth of a reading table in your home has a length of 85cm and width of 65cm you want to decorate the tablecloth by putting lace around it for the edging of the tablecloth how long of lace is needed? A-G-O-N-S-A-​


Answer:

240 cm of lace

Step-by-step explanation:

To find the length of lace needed to go around the tablecloth, we need to add the length of all four sides of the tablecloth and then add some extra length for overlap and tying off the ends. Assuming that we want to add 5 cm of lace on each side for overlap and tying off, the calculation is as follows:

Length of lace needed =

(length of tablecloth + width of tablecloth + 5 cm + 5 cm) x 2

= (85 cm + 65 cm + 5 cm + 5 cm) x 2

= 240 cm

Therefore, we need 240 cm of lace to go around the tablecloth.
