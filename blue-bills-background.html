25. A bowl has 4 red balls , 5 blue balls, and 3 yellow balls, If a ball is picked randomly, Find the probability of that, a.) It is a blue ball. b.) it is not a red ball. c.) it is a red ball or yellow ball. d.) it is a blue ball or red ball.​


Answer:

a) 5/12 or 41.67%

b) ⅓ or 33.33%

c) 7/12 or 58.33%

d) ¾ or 75%

Step-by-step explanation:

Given:

4 red balls, 5 blue balls, 3 yellow balls

Total number of balls:

[tex] = 4 + 5 + 3 \\ = 9 + 3 \\ = 12[/tex]

To find the probability in fraction form, use the total as a denominator, and the no. balls of that color as the numerator.

To find it in percent form, divide the no. balls of that color by the total, then multiply by 100.

a.

[tex] \frac{5}{12} \ or \\ =5 \div 12 \times 100\\ = 0.4167 \times 100 \\ = 41.67\%[/tex]

b.

[tex] = 12 - (5 + 3) \\ = 12 -8 \\ = 4[/tex]

[tex] = \frac{4}{12} \\ = \frac{1}{3} \: or \\ = 4 \div 12 \times 100\\ = 0.3333 \times 100 \\ = 33.33\%[/tex]

c.

[tex] = 4 + 3 \\ = 7[/tex]

[tex] \frac{7}{12} \: or \\ = 7 \div 12 \times 100 \\ = 0.5833 \times 100 \\ = 58.33\%[/tex]

d.

[tex] = 5 + 4 \\ = 9[/tex]

[tex] = \frac{9}{12} \\ = \frac{3}{4} \: or \\ =3 \div 4 \times 100 \\ = 0.75 \times 100\\ = 75\%[/tex]
