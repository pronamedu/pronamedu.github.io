4. 27. If z varies jointly as x and y and z=60 when x=5 and y=6, find z when x=7 and y=6.A.60B. 74C. 84D. 64​


JOINT VARIATION

Problem:

» If z varies jointly as x and y and z=60 when x=5 and y=6, find z when x=7 and y=6.

A.60B. 74C. 84D. 64

Answer:The value of z (unknown value) is 84.

— — — — — — — — — —

Step-by-step explanation:

- Write the equation of variation first and substitute the given values of z, x and y to solve for the constant proportionality or constant of variation (k).

[tex] \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \tt z=kxy\\ \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \tt \: \: \: \:\: 60= k(5)(6) \\ \: \: \: \ \: \: \: \: \tt \: \: \: 60 = k(30) \\ \: \tt \frac{60}{30} = k \\ \: \: \: \: \: \tt \blue{ 2 } = k\\ [/tex]

Since, the constant proportionality or constant of variation (k) is 2. Hence, the equation of variation

is [tex]\tt \purple{z=2xy}.[/tex]

Then, find z when x=7 and y=6.

[tex] \: \: \: \: \tt {z=2xy} \\ \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \tt z =(2)(7)(6) \\ \tt \: \: \: \: z= \underline{ \boxed{ \color{hotpink}{ \tt84}}}[/tex]

Hence, the value of z is 84.
