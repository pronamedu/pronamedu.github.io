22. what is specific message does mother earth is crying try to convey ​


Answer:

Take care of the environment

Explanation:

Because of this, many plants and animals are becoming extinct or in danger to become extinct every single year. We have to take care for the environment or else we will create the same mistakes.

This neglect has caused many effects. When more trees are cut, there is a higher probability of higher floods when typhoons come. Due to global warming, polar bears have a higher chance of being extinct and the ice in Antarctica is melting. We have to realize these effects as soon as possible or else these situations will become worse. Let us prevent Mother Earth from crying. If we do this, both humans and the environment will benefit.
