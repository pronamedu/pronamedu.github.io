2. famous folk dances in bicol????


Here are some of the famous folk dances in Bicol Region:
1. Pantomina - this is performed during wedding events in the Bicol Region.

2. Bicolano Cariñosa - this is performed during festivals and gatherings. It uses two handkerchiefs and do the hide and seek movement.

3. Sinakiki - this is performed during fiestas. This dance shows the actions of a rooster in courting.

4. Paseo de Bicol - Paseo means walking, this dance originates from Oas, Albay. Teenager performed his dance around a kapilya while the adults pray inside to remove bad luck.

5. Pandanggo Rinconada - from Nabua, Camarines Sur. Performed by young and old people during Christmas Season. This is the favorite on the Rinconada District.

12. A festival dance performance that potrays the classical battle of epic heroes in bicol region​.


Answer:

Ibalong Festival

The Ibalong Festival is a non-religious festival in Legazpi City, Albay, Philippines based on the Ibalong Epic, and is held in August. The festival celebrates the epic story Ibalong who was accompanied by three legendary heroes, namely Baltog, Handyong, and Bantong.

B. Lubi Dance - Sayaw “Lubi-Lubi” (coconut dance) is an indigenous fun dance (street parade) utilizing the City's Abundant coco plants and materials. It is a celebration of peace, love and unity among the Gingoognons every May to pay homage to the local patroness Sta. Rita de Cascia on her annual feast day.

B. LUBI-LUBI DANCE

Explanation:

 Lubi-lubi is a Bikol dance a combination of simple folk and social steps (literally, Lubi means coconut. A game called Lubi-Lubi is played with coconut shells). According to the Bikol this dance originated in Leyte and Samar where it is still dance like the balitaw.

In the Bikol regions, whenever there is a ballroom dancing the Lubi-Lubi is often introduced to break monotony it is also popular in Albay and Sorsogon.
