The sea turtle spend his entire life in the water but how long can he survive?

What we do know is that sea turtles live a long time (some can live up to 50 years or more) and have similar lifespans to humans.

Now that they are fast disappearing, how can we save them?

Reduce marine debris that may entangle or be accidentally eaten by sea turtles.Participate in coastal clean-ups and reduce plastic use to keep our beaches and ocean clean.

Why is classification of species important in the study of biodiversity?

Because it provides basic understanding about the components of biodiversity which is necessary for effective decision-making about conservation and sustainable use.

22. a lion can live for 40 years a whale can live 2.2 times 5hat of a lion a turtle can live 1.8 that of a whale how long can turtle live​


Answer:

If a lion can live for 40 years, and a whale can live 2.2 times that of a lion, then the whale can live for:

2.2 * 40 = 88 years

If a turtle can live 1.8 times that of a whale, then the turtle can live for:

1.8 * 88 = 158.4 years

Therefore, the turtle can live for 158.4 years.
