28. amy can type 420 words in 4 minutes what is the ratio of words to minutes​


Step-by-step explanation:

If she can type 420 words in 4 minutes then that would be [tex] 420:4 [/tex]. But we know that we can also express this in fraction form, such that we get:

[tex] \frac{420}{4} [/tex]

If we reduce it to lowest term, then we get:

[tex] \frac{420 \div 4}{4 \div 4} = \frac{105}{1} [/tex]

Which means that she can type 105 words per minute, or

[tex] \boxed{ 105 \text{words}:1 \text{minute} } [/tex]
