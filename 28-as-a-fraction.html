18. Change fraction to a percent 4-28​


Answer:

To change a fraction to a percent, divide the numerator (the top number) by the denominator (the bottom number) and then multiply the result by 100%.

For example, to change the fraction 4-28 to a percent, you would divide 4 by 28 to get 0.14285714285714288. Then you would multiply that number by 100% to get 14.285714285714288%.

So the fraction 4-28 expressed as a percent is 14.285714285714288%.
