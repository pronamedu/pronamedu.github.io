20. The perimeter of a rectangular concrete slab is 82 feet and its area is 364 sq feet. What is the length of the longer side of the slab


perimeter = 82 ft

2l + 2w = 82

l + w = 41

l = 41 - w

area = 364 ft²

lw = 364

(41 - w)(w) = 364

41w - w² = 364

0 = w² - 41w + 364

0 = (w - 13)(w - 28)

w = 13, 28

The longer side of the slab is 28 feet long.

24. 13. Two diagonals divide a square carpet into 4 congruent triangles. The baseof each triangle is 5 feet and the height is 2.5 feet. What is the area of theentire carpet?A. 18 sq. ft.B. 20 sq. ft. C. 25 sq. ft. D. 27 sq. ft.​


Answer:

Without bothering about the 4 triangles, you can easily get total area as 25 sq. ft.

How?

The shape of the carpet is square, the side is 5ft, so the area is simply 5*5 = 25 sq. ft.

But if you didn't notice this, let's compute it using the triangles;

Area of each triangle = ½bh

where

b = 5 ft

h = 2.5 ft

So,

Area of each triangle = ½bh

Area of each triangle = ½(5)(2.5)

Area of each triangle = 6.25 sq. ft.

So total Area = Area of each triangle x 4

Total Area = Area of each triangle x 4

Total Area = 6.25 x 4

Total Area = 25 sq. ft.
