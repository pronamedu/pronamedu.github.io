10. w varies jointly with the square root of v and x cubed. If w = 160 when v = 25 ad x =2, find k.


Answer:

The constant of variation (k) is 4.

Step-by-step explanation:

Mathematical Equation:

[tex]w=x^3k\sqrt{v}[/tex]

For the constant of variation (k):

Given:   [tex]w=160[/tex],   [tex]v=25[/tex],   [tex]x=2[/tex]

Find:   [tex]k=?[/tex]

Formula:   [tex]w=x^3k\sqrt{v}[/tex]

Solution:

[tex]w=x^3k\sqrt{v}\\160=(2)^3\sqrt{25}k\\160=(8)(5)k\\160=40k\\\frac{160}{40}=\frac{40k}{40}\\\boxed{k=4}[/tex]
