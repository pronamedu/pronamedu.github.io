Answer:

1.2 MB + 1024 KB = 2 MB + 1 MB = 3 MB

250 GB * 1024 MB/GB = 256000 MB

To divide it into 2 equal parts, we need to divide 256000 MB by 2:

256000 MB / 2 = 128000 MB
