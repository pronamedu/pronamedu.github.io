x² + 6x - 40 = 0

x² - 4x + 10x - 40 = 0

x(x - 4) + 10(x - 4) = 0

(x - 4)(x + 10)

x cannot be negative

Therefore,

x = 4

width of the rug = 4 ft

length of the rug = 4 + 6 = 10 ft

Assuming all figures are rectangles, the formula for the area of a rectangle is:

area = length x width

For the 15ft x 20ft rectangle, the area would be:

15ft x 20ft = 300 square feet

For the 20ft x 40ft rectangle, the area would be:

20ft x 40ft = 800 square feet

For the second 20ft x 20ft rectangle, the area would be:

20ft x 20ft = 400 square feet

Now we can add up the areas of all three rectangles to get the composite area:

300 + 800 + 400 = 1500 square feet

Therefore, the composite area of the figures with lengths of 15ft, 20ft, 40ft, and 20ft is 1500 square feet
