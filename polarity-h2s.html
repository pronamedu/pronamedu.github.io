26. Baance the following using the Law of Conservation: 1.)Fe + H2 SO4➡️FeSO4 + H2 O 2.)Fe(OH)3+H2 SO4➡️ Fe(SO4)3+H2 O


Good Day....

In balancing the chemical equation, we need to follow the law of conservation of mass. The mass of the reactant is equal to the mass of the product. Which means, the number of atoms in the reactant is equal to the number of atoms in the product.

1.)   Fe + H2SO4 ➡️ FeSO4 + H2O ( this is a single displacement, Fe will replace H and the product will be FeSO4 + H2 not FeSO4 + H2O)

               Fe + H2SO4 ➡️ FeSO4 + H2 (this equation is already balanced)

                     Fe  = 1                 Fe = 1

                     H  =   2                 H = 2

                     SO4 = 1                 SO4 = 1

2.)         2 Fe(OH)3+ 3 H2SO4➡️  Fe2(SO4)3+ 6 H2O

                           Fe = 2                                Fe = 2

                            O = 6                                O = 6

                            H = 12                               H = 12

                            SO4 = 3                            SO4 = 3
