Answer:

Coterminal angles of a given angle θ may be obtained by either adding or subtracting a multiple of 360° or 2π radians. Coterminal of θ = θ + 360° × k if θ is given in degrees, Coterminal of θ = θ + 2π × k if θ is given in radians. Two angles are coterminal if the difference between them is a multiple of 360° or 2π.

15 + 18 + 21 + 24 + 27 + 30 + 33 + 36 + 39 + 42 + 45 + 48 = 378

The sum of all multiples of 3 from 15 to 48 is 378.

Arithmetic Sequence Sum of the Arithmetic Sequence

The sum of all the multiples of 3 from 15 to 48 is 378.

Solution:

Arithmetic Sequence Formula: S_n = n/2[2a₁ + (n – 1) d]

Given: a₁ = 15

           d = 3

           n = 12

Find the sum of all the multiples of 3 from 15 to 48.

S_n = n/2[2a₁ + (n – 1) d]

S₁₂  = 12/2[2(15) + (12 – 1) 3]

S₁₂  = 6[30 + (11) 3]

S₁₂  = 6[30 + 33]

S₁₂  = 6[63]

S₁₂  = 6[63]

S₁₂  = 378

    2. Therefore, the sum of all the multiples of 3 from 15 to 48 is 378.

Definition:

       An arithmetic sequence is a sequence of numbers such that the difference of any two consecutive terms of the sequence is a constant. While an arithmetic series is the sum of the terms of the arithmetic sequence.
