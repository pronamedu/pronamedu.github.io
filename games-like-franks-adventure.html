17. frank had a score of -700 points in a video game. On each of the next 3 plays, he gained 400 points. Then what was his score?


Answer:

His score was 500 points.

Step-by-step explanation:

-700 + 400 = -300

-300 + 400 = 100

100 + 400 = 500

His score was 500 points.
