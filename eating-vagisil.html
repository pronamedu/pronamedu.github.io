10. Can cats eat fish bones?


Answer:

yes

Explanation:

Cats can eat fish bones, but they shouldn't. It doesn't matter if the bones are cooked or uncooked. They can be a choking hazard, cause internal impaction in the digestive tract, and do damage internally. If the cat tries to regurgitate the bones, they may cause further damage to the throat.
