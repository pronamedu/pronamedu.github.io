24. An athlete can run covering the 200 yards in 18 seconds. How much time would it take him more or less in 150 meter dash?​


Answer:

The correct answer is 14.765 seconds.

Step-by-step explanation:

Given:

distance (d) = 200 yards

time (t) = 18 seconds

new distance (dn) = 150 meters (164.042yards)

Solution:

Formula: Speed = distance / time (s=d/t)

s = 200yards/ 18sec

s = 11.11 yards/sec

s = d/t

11.11 yards/sec = 164.042yards/ t

t = 164.042yards / 11.11 yards/sec

t = 14.765 seconds
