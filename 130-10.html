So, 130 is the 33rd term.

24. If the measures of the three angles of a quadrilateral measures 120° , 130°, and 10° then the measure of the fourth angle is​


Answer:

100°

Step-by-step explanation:

a + b + c + d = 360°

120° + 130° + 10° + d = 360°

260° + d = 360°

d = 360° - 260°

d = 100°
