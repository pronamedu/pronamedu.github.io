140 + 2b = 230
2b = 230 - 140

the formula for finding the area of the rectangle is length x breadth, thus,

20. The perimeter of a rectangle is 230 cm. If the length of the rectangle is 70 cm, find its breadth and area


Answer:

breadth = 45 cm

area = 3150 square centimeters

Step-by-step explanation:

The perimeter of a rectangle is equal to twice the width and length,

Perimeter = 2 * (length + width)

Width = (Perimeter / 2) - length

Width = (230 / 2) - 70

= 115 - 70

= 45 cm

We then compute the area as follows,

Area = length * width

Area = 70 * 45

= 3150 square centimeters

[tex]230=(2w+3)w\\230=2w^{2}+3w\\0=2w^{2}+3w-230\\0=(2w+23)(w-10)[/tex]

[tex]l=2w+3\\l=2(10)+3\\l=20+3\\l=23\: cm[/tex]
