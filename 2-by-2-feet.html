8. The area of rectangular garden is (x^2 + 3x - 54) square feet.If the length is (x + 9) feet,find a polynomial that represents the width.(w= A/L)A.x^2 + 6 feetB.x^2 - 6 feetC.x + 6 feetD.x - 6 feet​


Answer:

d. x - 6 feet

Step-by-step explanation:

Given:

A = (x² + 3x - 54) square feetL = (x + 9) feet

Formula:

w = A/L

Solution:

[tex]\sf w = \frac{A}{L}[/tex]

[tex]\sf w = \frac{x^2+3x-54}{x+9}[/tex]

[tex]\sf Let \: f(x) = x^2 + 3x - 54[/tex]

[tex]\textsf{Assume }\sf f(x) = (x+9)(x+\alpha); \: for \: some \: number \: \alpha.[/tex]

[tex]\sf It \: follows, \begin{cases} \sf \alpha + 9 = 3\\ \sf 9\alpha = -54 \end{cases} \implies \alpha = -6[/tex]

[tex]\sf \therefore x^2 + 3x - 54 = (x+9)(x-6)[/tex]

[tex]\sf Going \: back \: to \: our \: original \: equation,[/tex]

[tex]\sf w = \frac{x^2+3x-54}{x+9}[/tex]

[tex]\sf w = \frac{\cancel{(x+9)}(x-6)}{\cancel{x+9}}[/tex]

[tex]\sf w = x-6[/tex]

[tex]\textsf{Hence, we select option D.}[/tex]

[tex]\textsf{Check:}[/tex]

15. On a set of blueprints, a 12-foot wall is represented by a 6-inch line segment. What scale is used in the blueprints? 6 feet : 2 inches 2 feet : 2 inches 6 feet : 1 inch 2 feet : 1 inch


Answer:

2 feet : 1 inch

Step-by-step explanation:

A ratio can be treated like a fraction, in which we can cancel the common factors.

12 feet : 6 inches

=> 6 * 2 feet : 6 inches

=> 2 feet : 1 inch

Therefore, the scale used in the blueprints is 2 feet : 1 inch.
