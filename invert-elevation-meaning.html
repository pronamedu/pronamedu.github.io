11. You are on a hiking trail that leads to the top of a mountain. At 1 P.M . you reach an elevation of 4400 feet. At 4 P.M. you reach an elevation of 5600 feet. What is your mean hourly change in elevation?


Arithmetic Sequence:

An = A1 (n-1) d

A1 = 4400

An = 5600

n = 4

d = ?


Solve for d, common difference

5600 = 4400 (4-1) d

1200 = 3d

400 = d or d = 400


Answer: 400 ft. is the mean hourly change in elevation

15. In technical drawing data, what is the elevation drawing means? Explain briefly,​


Answer:

As buildings are rarely simple rectangular shapes in plan, an elevation drawing is a first angle projection that shows all parts of the building as seen from a particular direction with the perspective flattened. Generally, elevations are produced for four directional views, for example, north, south, east, west.

30. The elevator has a rated capacity of 0.6 ton (1ton=2,000lbs.). If at one time, the elevator carries 12 passengers consisting of 3 children with a mean weight of 45 lbs, 4 male with a mean weight of 130lbs and 5 female with a mean weight of 122lbs. Is the elevator overloaded? By how much? Show your computation.​


If the elevator has a rated capacity of 0.6 tons and a ton is equivalent to 2,000 lbs, the twelve passengers cannot ride on it at the same time without overloading it. The average total capacity that will be consumed by the twelve is 1,265 lbs. This is 65 lbs more than the rated capacity of 1200 lbs.

How was it computed?

Solving for the value of 0.6 tons in lbs:

Considering that 1 ton= 2,000 lbs,

0.6 ton x [tex]\frac{2000lbs}{1 ton}[/tex] = 1200 lbs

Solving for the total average capacity of the 12 passengers:

3 children x 45 lbs= 135 lbs

4 male x 130 lbs= 520 lbs

5 female x 122 lbs= 610 lbs

Adding the products:

135 + 520 + 610 = 1, 265 lbs

The rated capacity (1,200 lbs) is less than the actual total average capacity (1,265 lbs) of the people who want to ride the elevator.
