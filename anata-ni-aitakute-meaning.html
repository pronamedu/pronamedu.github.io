4. Hontōni daisukidayoWatashi wa anata o koroshitai anata o aishiteimasu​


Answer:

I l*ve you

When you translate English into Japanese, there are many ways to express this. For instance, “I l*ve you.” The conventional translation is, “Watashi wa anata o aishite imasu.” However, few Japanese actually use this expression. ... “Suki” means “like” and “l*ve.” Many Japanese mean “l*ve” when they use “suki” for a person.
