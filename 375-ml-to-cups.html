2. What is the percent in volume of 94 ml of ethyl alcohol present in 375 ml of beer?


SOLUTION:

Step 1: List the given values.

[tex]\begin{aligned} & V_{\text{solute}} = \text{94 mL} \\ & V_{\text{solution}} = \text{375 mL} \end{aligned}[/tex]

Step 2: Calculate the percent by volume.

[tex]\begin{aligned} \%(v/v) & = \frac{V_{\text{solute}}}{V_{\text{solution}}} \times 100\% \\ & = \frac{\text{94 mL}}{\text{375 mL}} \times 100\% \\ & = 25.067\% \\ & \approx \boxed{25\%} \end{aligned}[/tex]

Hence, the percent by volume of ethyl alcohol is 25%.

6. if i make a solution by adding water to 75ml of ethanol until the total volume of the solution is 375 ml what is the percent by volume of ethanol in the solution


Answer:

20% v/v

Explanation:

The percent by volume (% v/v)

The percent by volume of a solution is calculated by dividing the volume of the solute by the total volume of the solution multiplied by 100. The percent by volume formula is:

% v/v = [tex]\frac{volume of solute (mL)}{total volume of solution ( mL)} x 100[/tex]

In this problem, we are given the volume of solute which is 75mL of ethanol and the total volume of the solution which is 375 mL.

Substitute all the given values to the % v/v formula. Hence,

% v/v = [tex]\frac{volume of solute (mL)}{total volume of solution ( mL)} x 100[/tex]

%v/v = [tex]\frac{75 mL of ethanol}{375 mL of solution} x 100[/tex]

%v/v = 20%

Therefore, the percent by volume of ethanol in the solution is 20%.

29. 5. The density of ethylene glycol (antifreeze, HOCH2CH2OH) is 1.09 g/mL. What is the concentration of ethylene glycol in % by volume if you have 30.7 grams of ethylene glycol mixed with 375 mL of water?​


✏️CONCENTRATION ETHYLENE GLYCOL

[tex]\red{•••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••}[/tex]

ANSWER

We can use the density of ethylene glycol to convert its mass to volume.

Density of ethylene glycol = 1.09 g/mL

Volume of ethylene glycol=mass/density[tex]\\=\frac{ \rm30.7 \: g}{ \rm1.09 \: g/ ml } = \rm28.18ml[/tex]

Total volume of solution = volume of water + volume of ethylene glycol= 375 mL + 28.18 mL= 403.18 mL

[tex]\red{•••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••}[/tex]

Now we can calculate the concentration of ethylene glycol in % by volume:

Concentration of ethylene glycol = (volume of ethylene glycol / total volume of solution) x 100%[tex]\\ =\frac{ \rm \: 28.18ml}{ \rm403.18 \: ml} \times 100[/tex]

[tex] 6.99%[/tex]

Therefore, the concentration of ethylene glycol in % by volume is 6.99%.
