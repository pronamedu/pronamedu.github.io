17. The mean of four numbers is 71.5. If three of the numbers are 58, 76, and 88, what is the value of the fourth number?


Answer:

The value of the fourth number is 64.

Step-by-step explanation:

To find the fourth number, we are going to follow the formula in finding the mean.

What is the mean?

The mean is the average of the given numbers. To find the mean, we need to add up all the numbers, then divide by how many numbers there are.

sum divided by the count

Solution:

Let x be the fourth number.

(58 + 76 + 88 + x) ÷ 4 = 71.5

(222 + x) ÷ 4 = 71.5

[(222 + x) ÷ 4] (4) = 71.5 (4)

222 + x = 286

x = 286 - 222

x = 64

Final Answer:

64

Checking:

(58 + 76 + 88 + 64) ÷ 4 = 71.5

286 ÷ 4 = 71.5

71.5 = 71.5 ✔
