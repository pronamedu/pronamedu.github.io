21. hazel has 3 yd of rope. how many 10 inches of smaller ropes can be cut from it?


In the given problem, you need to apply your knowledge and skills in alegbra and basic math especially in converting of measurements and solving word problem.

Given:

10 inches

3 yards

Problem:

How many smaller rope can be cut?

Solution:

Remember: You cannot perform mathematical operations if two or more given are in different units.

Step 1: Convert yards to Inches

1 yd = 36 in

3 yd × 36 in/yd

108 inches

Step 2: Solve the problem

Just divide the lenght of each smaller pieces of rope to the total length of the rope

108 inches ÷ 10 inches

10.8

Final Answer:

10 pieces of smaller rope
