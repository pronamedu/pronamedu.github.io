10. Julio can paint a small treehouse in 6 hrs, while Augusto can do the same job in 8 hrs. If they work together, how long will it take them to do the job?


Julio can paint a small treehouse in 6 hrs

=> 1 hour = 1/6 of the job


Augusto can do the same job in 8 hrs

=> 1 hour = 1/8 of the job


Together:

1 hour = 1/6 + 1/8 = 7/24 of the job


7/24 of the job = 1 hour

1/24 of the job = 1 ÷ 7 = 1/7 of the hour

24/24 of the job = 1/7 x 24 = 3.43 hours


Answer: it takes 3.43 hours to do the job together.
