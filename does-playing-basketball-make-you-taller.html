Mark saw Joe and Sara, who are both taller than 6 feet, playing basketball while wearing bright sneakers, Joe invited his friend Jim, who is heavy-set at 193 pounds and about 5½ feet tall, to join them for soccer the next morning. After playing soccer, all had to finish homework that was due the next day, a Tuesday. On which day did Mark see Joe and Sara play basketball while wearing white sneakers?


Answer:

Mark saw Joe and Sara playing basketball while wearing bright sneakers on a day that is not specified in the given information. Therefore, we cannot determine the specific day on which this occurred.

However, we do know that the next morning after Joe and Sara played basketball, they played soccer with Jim. And since the homework was due the next day, which was a Tuesday, we can work backward to determine the day on which they played soccer.

If the homework was due on Tuesday, then the day they played soccer must have been either Monday or Tuesday. But since they played soccer "the next morning" after playing basketball, we can eliminate Tuesday as a possibility because they would not have had time to finish their homework if they played soccer on Tuesday morning.

Therefore, the only possible day on which they played soccer is Monday. So, Mark saw Joe and Sara playing basketball while wearing bright sneakers on a day that was either Sunday or Monday.
