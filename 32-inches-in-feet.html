9. The acceleration of an object due to gravity is 32 feet per second squared. What is acceleration due to gravity in inches per second squared?


Answer:

384 inches per second squared

Step-by-step explanation:

Given: 32 feet per second squared

You can convert the feet to inches

1 ft (feet) = 12 in (inches) (from english conversion units)

32 feet per second squared to ___ inches per second squared

[tex]32\frac{ft}{s^{2}}(\frac{12 inches}{1 ft} ) = 384 \frac{in}{s^{2} }[/tex]

Answer: 384 in/ s^2
