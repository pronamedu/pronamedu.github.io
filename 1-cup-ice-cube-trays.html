19. Pour exact same amount of the liquids in the ice cube tray Milk Orange juice Ice tea Water


When compared in accordance to freezing time, milk will freeze 2% faster than water due to density differences. Milk is a lot denser than water, thus makes it easier for molecules to compact and become solid.

Pure orange juice contains sugar called fructose, which is an addition to just water. This makes it a lot harder to freeze since the sugar molecules don't stick easily, thus make it harder to freeze.

Iced tea is filled with powder and sugar. There are a lot of extra constituents in iced tea, especially sugar molecules. This means iced tea is  slower than water when frozen at 0 degrees Celsius or 32 degrees Fahrenheit.

Water is easy to freeze in terms of freezing time when compared to orange juice and iced tea since it only requires the normal temperature to freeze, which is basically 0 degrees Celsius or 32 degrees Fahrenheit.
