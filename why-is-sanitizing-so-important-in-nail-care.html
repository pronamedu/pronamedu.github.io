1. Why is sanitizing so important in nail care


Answer:

Importance of Sanitizing Nailcare Tools

Sanitation is so important in nailcare especially in nail salons. Sanitizing nailcare tools should be a habit because these tools are shared among a number of people. By proper sanitation, it prevents the spread of fungal and bacterial diseases from one person to the other person. It kills the leftover pathogen on the tools and ensures the safety of using the tools.

Nailcare Tool Maintenance

Here are some tips to ensure that nailcare tools are safe to use from time to time.

Clean clippers, glass files, and cuticle nippers regularly. Wipe and wash with soap and water. Disinfect by rubbing with an alcohol-soaked cotton ball.

Don’t share emery boards because they are made of paper. They can’t be properly sanitized. Replace them when the grit begins to wear down or filing your nails no longer produces smooth results.

Store tools properly. Place dry, disinfected implements in an airtight container. This will keep them germ-free.

Nail Care Tools And Equipment And Their Uses

Here are some of the nail care tools and equipments.

Foot Files

A foot file is built from a metal and sandpaper. You will use these on a regular basis. Foot files keep your feet soft and smooth.

Callous Remover

Callous remover is a one of nail care tools and equipment based on nail caring. It is designed to strop off calluses and corns.

Pusher

It is a long iron made equipment which is used for nails. It is a tool which can be used to push back and loosen the nails.

Cuticle Nipper

It is a small tool which is used to trim or cut back the cuticles present on your fingernails. The tool is made from a durable metal such as stainless steel. At the end of it is a metal jaw with sharp edges intended to cut away excessive skin around the cuticle.

Foot Scrubs

High-quality foot scrubs help enhance the pedicure experience by exfoliating and revitalizing the skin on the feet and legs.

Nail Files

For shaping toenails prior to polishing.

Foot Spa Basin

This is a large rectangular container used for soaking and bathing your feet.

Lotion

To soften skin and use for foot and leg massage.

Cuticle Nail Pusher

Keep those pesky cuticles under control.

Toe Ropes

These are great for keeping toenails separate so polish doesn't stick or get smudged.
