15. what is the definition of Shi mariamman​


Answer:

Māri (/mɒrı/, /maari/), also known as Mariamman ( /mɒrı əˈmʌn/) and Mariaai, spelt also Maariamma, or simply Amman or Aatha ("mother") is the South Indian Hindu goddess of rain. She is the main South Indian mother goddess, predominant in the rural areas of Tamil Nadu and Karnataka.
