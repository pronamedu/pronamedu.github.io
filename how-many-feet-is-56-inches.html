If all the 16 polka dots are mounted in a wall 8 feet by 7 feet, howmany square feet is not covered by the 16 polka dots?A. 3.14 ftB. 5.76 ftC. 50.24 ftD. 56 fe​


Answer:

9. B 19.81 in

Area of Triangle

A = 1/2bh

A = 1/2(5 in)(4 in)

A = 1/2(20 in²)

A = 10 in²

Area of semi circle

A = 3.14 (r)²/2

A = 3.14(2.5 in)² /2

A = 3.14(6.25 in²/2

A = 9.81 in²

Composite Area

A = 10 in² + 9.81 in²

A = 19.81 in²

10. B 5.76 ft²

Area of 16 circle

A = 3.14(12 in)²×16

A = 452.16 in² × 16

A = 7234.56 in²

Convert 7234.56 in² to ft²

7,234.56 in² = 50.24 ft²

Area of a wall

A = 8ft × 7 ft

A = 56 ft²

Area not covered by polka dots

A = 56 ft² – 50.24 ft²

A = 5.76 ft²
