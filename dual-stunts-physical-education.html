Topic: Basketball - Health and Fitness

Objectives:

- To develop the fundamental skills and knowledge of basketball

- To understand the importance of health and fitness in playing basketball

- To learn the principles of sportsmanship and teamwork in playing basketball

- To understand the importance of maintaining proper sanitation and hygiene in sports

Activities:

1. Warm-up exercises - Jogging, stretching, and other exercises to prepare the body for physical activity

2. Introduction to basketball - Basic basketball skills and techniques such as dribbling, passing, shooting, and defense.

3. Basketball drills - Individual and team-based basketball drills to improve skill and coordination.

4. Health and Fitness theory - Discussions on the importance of maintaining proper health and fitness in playing basketball, including topics such as nutrition, hydration, and injury prevention.

5. Safety in sports - Discussions and demonstrations on how to play basketball safely and how to avoid common sports-related injuries.

6. Teamwork and sportsmanship - Discussions and activities on the importance of teamwork and sportsmanship in basketball and in life.

7. Sanitation and hygiene - Discussions on the importance of maintaining proper sanitation and hygiene in sports, including proper cleaning and disinfecting of sports equipment, and personal hygiene practices.

Assessment:

- Assessment of basketball skills and techniques through observation and participation in basketball drills and games.

- Written assessment on health and fitness, safety in sports, and sanitation and hygiene.

- Participation and cooperation in team activities and discussions.
