10. A rectangular garden has a perimeter of 180 feet. The north side of the garden is 40 feet. What is the length of the east side of the garden?


Answer:

50 feet

Step-by-step explanation:

The north side of the garden is the width of the garden, while the east side of the garden is the length of the garden. So the width of the garden is 40 feet.

P = 2(L+W)

Given,

W = 40P = 180

Substitute the given value of P and W.

180 = 2(L + 40)

180 = 2L + 80

180 - 80 = 2L

100/2 = L

50 = L

∴ The length of the east side of the garden is 50 feet.

21. Mr. Bert is selling a rectangular plot of land that has a width 8 feet less than its length. The plot has an area of 180 square feet. How wide is the plot of land?


Answer:

10ft

Step-by-step explanation:

The area of a rectangle is defined as

A = length * width OR  

  = base * height OR  

 = depth * width

We don't know the exact length and width, but we do have some details about it. Let's set the length as x.

If length = x, then width (which is 8 feet less than its length) is x -8:

l = x

w = (x-8)

A = 180 ft^2

We can then solve for x given the following

A = length * width

180 ft^2 = x * (x-8)

180 ft^2 = x^2 - 8x

x^2 - 8x -180 = 0

(x + 10) * (x-18) = 0

From the above, we can see that x can either be -10ft or 18ft. However, we can not have a negative length. Therefore, x = 18ft.

The length is 18ft, but the plot of land is 10 ft wide.

27. In Roundhouse kick, the pivot is 180 degrees, while the 45 degrees’ kick is pivoting its feet to 90 degrees. Determine we’re are the targets of each kick stated.


Both attacks hit the opponent with their feet. The difference is that the roundhouse kick goes for the head of the opponent. Usually, a 45-degree kick goes for the lower body, but in Taekwondo sparring, a 45-degree kick goes for the middle of the body, most likely around the ribs.

Further explanation:Roundhouse kick

Some instructors say that to do a roundhouse kick, you should turn 90 degrees, bend the knee of the leg you're kicking with, and then kick with the knee at a right angle to the target. Others do a roundhouse kick by turning 180 degrees and pointing their knee toward the target. This makes it hard for the kicker to see the target because they have to look over their shoulder. But the 180-degree pivot gives off the most power. Others turn 160 degrees, which lets the kicker bring their knee up and still get a lot of power, and lets them see the goal better.

For a hook kick, the pivot point is 180 degrees, so the kicking leg can go straight toward the side of the target and still hook like a whip.

Some instructors say that side kicks should begin with a 90-degree turn, which is a quick knee-kok followed by a side kick. In traditional Taekwondo, kicks are done by turning 180 degrees, which makes them look like back kicks. This means that the kicker has to be able to see the goal behind them. Sliding side-kicks require a 180-degree pivot. A 160-degree turn is used in modern Taekwondo so that you can see the target better. Again, the 180-degree turn gives the most powerful kick.

For spinning hook and back kicks, you have to turn 180 degrees. Anything less will make the knee that isn't moving twist in a way that isn't natural.

There are many reasons why it's important to know how to use different kick pivots. If you don't do it right, you lose power, lose your balance, can't see your target, and may hurt the joints in your stationary leg.

30. a rectangular patio has area 180 square feet. The widthof the patio is three feet less than the length. Find the length and width​


Answer:

Length = 15

Width = 12

Step-by-step explanation:

Let:

Length be x and Width be y

First Equation: xy = 80

2nd Equation: y = x-3

Substitute:

x(x-3) = 180

Solve for x:

x(x-3) = 180x^2 - 3x = 180x^2 - 3x - 180 = 0 (transpose)(x+12)(x-15) = 0 (factor)x = -12, x = 15 Since there is no negative measurement, x = 15

Solve for y:

y = x-3y = 15 - 3y = 12
