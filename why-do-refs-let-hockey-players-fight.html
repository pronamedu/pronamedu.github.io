25. 3. In a class there are: 8 students who play football and hockey, 7 students who do not play football or hockey, 13 students who play hockey, 19 students who play football. How many students are there in the class? ven diagram ​


To solve this problem, we can use a Venn diagram to visualize the information given. Let's draw a Venn diagram with two circles representing football and hockey, and label the regions as follows:

- A: students who play football and hockey

- B: students who play hockey but not football

- C: students who play football but not hockey

- D: students who do not play football or hockey

We can then fill in the numbers given in the problem:

- A = 8

- B = 13 - 8 = 5

- C = 19 - 8 = 11

- D = 7

To find the total number of students in the class, we need to add up the numbers in all four regions:

- Total = A + B + C + D

- Total = 8 + 5 + 11 + 7

- Total = 31

Therefore, there are 31 students in the class. Here is the Venn diagram:
