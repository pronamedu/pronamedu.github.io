24. What is acronym TLE? What is TLE and its importance? What is TLE subject all about? Why do we need to study TLE? How can TLE help us?


TLE SubjectWhat is the Acronym TLE?

The acronym of TLE stands For Technology and Livelihood Education. This subject falls under the Technical-Vocational-Livelihood track specialization and can be taken between Grades nine to Grade twelve.

Technology and Livelihood Education Subject and Its Importance

Technology and Livelihood Education Subject is a subject course offered to students in the secondary education curriculum in the Philippines. The scope of its learning is wide and some examples of the things we can learn in TLE subjects are Home Economics, Industrial arts, and many more. Its importance lies in the knowledge and skills that we learn that could help us be more productive person that achieves a productive output.

What is Technology and Livelihood Education Subject All About?

TLE subject is all about teaching us the basic knowledge of technology and livelihood. It gives us an idea and teaches us skills that can be used in hands-on work opportunities. The knowledge that we learn in this subject can be used to make a decent living. The scope subjects of TLE are Home Economics, Entrepreneurship, Agri-Fishery Arts, Information and Communication Technology, and Industrial Arts.

Why Do We Need To Study TLE?

It is important to study TLE because, through this subject course, we gain an understanding of the general living of an individual. We develop skills that can be used in the present and could even be your source of income in the future. Through TLE we grasp a sense of independence and self-reliance. We learn the application of skills combined with the technological advancement that we have in the present day.

How Can TLE Help Us?We get to develop hands-on skills that can be used in daily living.We may gain a source of income by creating a type of business we learn on the subject.We learn the basic skills of living such as cooking.We gain an understanding of entrepreneurship.
