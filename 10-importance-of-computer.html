24. what is the importance of learning the different computer troubleshooting techniques (10)​


The importance of learning different computer troubleshooting techniques is that it allows individuals to quickly and effectively diagnose and solve problems with their computer systems. These techniques can range from simple checks, like restarting a computer or checking connections, to more advanced methods, like using system logs or diagnostic software. By learning these techniques, individuals can save time and money by being able to resolve issues on their own, rather than having to take their computer to a professional for repair. In addition, having knowledge of troubleshooting techniques can also be useful in a professional setting, such as IT support or computer repair.
