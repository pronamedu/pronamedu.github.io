12. examples of these kinds of mixtures are What common exc found in the environment?​


Answer:

A Homogeneous Mixture is a mixture in which the composition is uniform throughout the mixture.

Homogeneous Mixture Found in the Environment:
- Rain
- Steel
- PURE Air
- Salt Water
- Natural Gas

A Heterogeneous Mixture is a mixture in which the composition is not uniform throughout the mixture.

Heterogenous Mixture Found in the Environment:
- Soil (With Living Organisms or Materials)
- Rocks (In Different Types, Colors, and Shapes)
- Sand (With Water)
- River Water
- Smog (Gas mixed with a liquid and a solid)
