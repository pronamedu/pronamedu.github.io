1. 4 ft to 50 cm in ratio? ​


Answer:

To convert 4 feet to centimeters, you can use the conversion factor that 1 foot is equal to 30.48 centimeters. Using this conversion factor, you can set up the following proportion:

4 feet : 50 centimeters :: 1 foot : x centimeters

Solving for x, you get:

x = (50 centimeters) * (1 foot / 4 feet)

= 12.7 centimeters

So, 4 feet is equal to approximately 12.7 centimeters.

You can also use this conversion factor to convert other measurements from feet to centimeters. For example, to convert 8 feet to centimeters, you can set up the proportion 8 feet : x centimeters :: 1 foot : 30.48 centimeters, and solve for x to get x = 243.84 centimeters. To convert 6 feet to centimeters, you can set up the proportion 6 feet : x centimeters :: 1 foot : 30.48 centimeters, and solve for x to get x = 182.88 centimeters.
