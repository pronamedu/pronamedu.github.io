17. 5. It is a type of dimensioning that requires all dimensions be lettered betweenguide lines that are parallel to the bottom edge of the drawing paper.A. Aligned MethodC. Overall DimensionB. Location DimensionD. Uni-directional Method​


Dimensioning

It refers to the method of forming or shaping something to particular measurements. They use marks such as diagrams to show dimensions.

Question: It is a type of dimensioning that requires all dimensions to be lettered between guidelines that are parallel to the bottom edge of the drawing paper.

Answer: A. Aligned Method

Here is the explanation for each dimensioning method mentioned in the choices above.

Aligned Method

Aligned methods use directions written in two forms from the whole drawing. It is written upward and on the right side. They can be read from the bottom and right sides of the illustration or drawing. One of the key advantages of the aligned method in terms of dimensions is that it can be written in the horizontal style and it is easier to write.

Overall Dimension

In various parts of a drawing, the baseline is used to write the dimension. Afterward, the dimensions are written in a parallel style to the baseline. The smallest object is written to the side of the object while on the other hand, the biggest is written on the external side of the object.

Location Dimension

In this method, the location or distance relationship of one feature of an object with respect to the other one. It also shows the location of each feature within an object and view. It tells every viewer where the edges are inside an object view.

Uni-directional Method

It literally means that all the dimensions are interpreted or read in the same direction. This is the most used method in dimensioning. This whole dimension is written in the same direction in the whole drawing.

The two most used methods of placing dimensions are the aligned system method and the unidirectional system. These dimensioning standards are widely used because their benefits and methods are easy to understand.
