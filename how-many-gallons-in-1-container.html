15. When an oil tank is 7/12 full, it contains 5 1/4 gallons. How many gallons does it hold when full?


Let x be the amount in full (in gallons) of an oil tank.

Convert mixed fraction to improper fraction:

5 ¹/₄ =  ²¹/₄

[tex] \frac{7}{12} (x) = \frac{21}{4} [/tex]

x = [tex] (\frac{21}{4})( \frac{12}{7} )[/tex]

x = 9 gallons

Answer:  The oil tank can hold 9 gallons when full.
