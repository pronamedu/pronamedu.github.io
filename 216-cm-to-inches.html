8. Find the side measure of a cube with the volume of 216 cubic cm. s = ____ cm.​


Answer:

s = 6 cm

Step-by-step explanation:

Please remember that the formula in getting the volume of a cube is V= s³.

Given:

V = 216 cm³

substitute the given to the formula.

V = s³

216 cm³ = s³

in order to get the s, first you need to put all the equations to cube root.

[tex]\sqrt[3]{216cm^{3} = s^{3} }\\[/tex]

all of the cube roots will be cancelled.

therefore,

6 cm = s
