5. A rectangle measures 10 meters by 600 centimeters. Find the area of the rectangle in square meters.


Answer:

Area of the rectangle in square meters is 60 square meters.

Step-by-step explanation:

In order to solve for the area of the rectangle in square meters, convert first the given measurement to meters such that 600 centimeters = _____meters. Since 1 meter is equivalent to 100 centimeters then 600 meters must be equal to 6 meters. Using this dimension, we can now solve for the area of the rectangle such that A = lw, A = 10 meters x 6 meters, A = 60 square meters.
