9. The degree measure of a semicircle is_____?A. 180°B. 200°C. 260°D. 360°​


Answer:

A. 180°

Step-by-step explanation:

a circle has 360° while semicircle means half circle so the degree of a semicircle is 180°

Answer:

A

Explanation:

A semicircle is half of a circle. A central angle which is subtended by a semicircle has a measure of 180°. A circle is associated with a complete rotation, which is 360°. A semi-circle is associated with half of a rotation which is 180°.

150 is the 75% of 200

0.6(540)= 324
324 is the 60% of 540

Out of a hundred is the meaning of the word per cent. A relative figure reflecting hundredth parts of any quantity is called a percentage.
