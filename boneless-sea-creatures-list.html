25. why is the ugliest sea creature is the manatee


Answer:

Manatees

Saying that manatees are the ugliest sea creature is an unpopular opinion. People often see them as sweet looking, cuddly and cute creatures.

Manatees are also called as sea cows. They have a stubbly stout and flat tails. They are slow-moving mammals found on shallow coastal areas.

They only feed on sea grasses, algaes and mangrove leaves, making them herbivores. Manatees belong in the order of Serinia and their closest living relatives are Elephants.

29. is there a sea creatures in deep see now or before​


Answer:

Yes

Explanation:

Organisms that dwell below the ocean's photic zone are referred to as deep sea creatures. Hundreds of bars of pressure, minimal amounts of oxygen, very little food, no sunshine, and continual, severe cold are all circumstances that these organisms must exist under. The majority of species must rely on food that falls from the sky.

These species dwell in harsh conditions, such as the abyssal or hadal zones, which are hundreds of meters below the surface and nearly devoid of light.
