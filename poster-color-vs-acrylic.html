22. make a simple comparison between pink color vs red color​


Answer:

Pink color is a lighter shade of red color. While red is a strong, bold and passionate color, pink is perceived as a gentle, nurturing and romantic color. Red is often associated with excitement, energy, and intensity whereas pink is linked with sweetness, tenderness, and love. Red can grab attention and evoke a sense of urgency while pink can provide a calming and soothing effect. Red is commonly used for warning signs or alerts, while pink is predominantly used for feminine products or romantic gestures.
