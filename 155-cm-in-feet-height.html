5. The heights of Susan, Ada, and Rhea were 155 cm, 158 cm, and 161 cm, respectively. What is the height of Tricia if the mean of all the four girls is 158 cm?​


Mean

The measure of central tendency which is computed through adding the given values and dividing the sum by the number of values. It is most sensitive to extreme values. It is often called the average. The mean, together with the median and the mode are called measures of central tendency. Each of these measures describes a different indication of the typical or central value in the distribution.

Answer:

158 cm

Solutions:

Asked: The height of Tricia.

Given: 155 cm - height of Susan

           158 cm - height of Ada

           161 cm - height of Rhea

           158 cm - mean

Operation: finding the mean

Number Sentence: Mean = [tex]\frac{x_1 + x_2 + x_3 + x_4}{n}[/tex]

Solution:        Mean = [tex]\frac{x_1 + x_2 + x_3 + x_4}{n}[/tex]

                   158 cm = [tex]\frac{155 + 158 + 161 + x_4}{4}[/tex]

155 + 158 + 161 + x₄ = (158)(4)

                 474 + x₄ = 632

                           x₄ = 632 - 474

                           x₄ = 158

Answer: Therefore, Tricia is 158 cm.
