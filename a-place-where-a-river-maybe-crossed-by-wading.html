Places on Earth can be located by using geographical coordinates, which are based on latitude and longitude. Together, latitude and longitude form a grid system that allows any place on Earth to be identified with a unique set of coordinates. With the help of modern technology, such as GPS or global positioning system, the accurate location of a place can be determined quickly and easily.
