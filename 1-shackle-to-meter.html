6. an outline is a tool not a shackle what is the explanation behind it ​


Answer:

A shackle is a u-shaped, load-bearing connecting device designed to be used with a removable pin while an outline is an organizational tool used by authors in their academic and professional writing. It is a skeleton, a foundation of an entire writing piece, produced to structurize main ideas into a list of contents.
