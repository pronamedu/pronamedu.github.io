26. Solve the following 1. What is the measure of each angle of a regular six-sided polygon? 2. How many diagonals can be drawn in a heptagon? 3. Each exterior angle of a regular polygon is 18 0 . How many sides has the polygon?


Answer:

1. What is the measure of each angle of a regular six-sided polygon?

Interior Sum = (6 - 2) × 180°

= 4 × 180°

= 720°

each of interior angle = 720°/6

= 120°

the sum of the exterior angle is 360°

each exterior angle = 360°/6

= 60°

2. How many diagonals can be drawn in a heptagon?

regular heptagon has 7-sides

number of diagonals = n(n - 3)/2

n = 7

number of diagonals = 7(7-3)/2

= 7(4)/2

= 28/2

= 14

therefore, a regular heptagon has 14 diagonals

3. Each exterior angle of a regular polygon is 18° . How many sides has the polygon?

the sum of the exterior angle is 360°

the number of sides of regular polygon = 360°/18°

= 20

therefore, the number of sides of a polygon is 20 when each exterior angle is 18°
