3. Mr. Brock has a fixed semi-annual salary of Php50,555.75. How much is his bi-monthly salary rate?


Answer:

≈ Php 16,851.92

Explanation and computation:

"Semi-" means half and "annual" means year, so Mr Brock's semi-annual salary of Php50,555.75 is also his six-month salary rate.

Solving for the Mr. Brock's bi-monthly salary rate by dividing his semi-annual salary by 3.

[tex]\frac{50,555.75}{6\:mos}/3\approx16,851.92/2\:mos[/tex]
