9. A merry-go-round moves in a circle at a constant speed. Is the merry-go-round accelerating? Explain your answer.


Good Day

Problem:

A merry-go-round moves in a circle at a constant speed. Is the merry-go-round accelerating? Explain your answer.

Merry go round is accelerating even the speed is constant.

Acceleration is the rate of change in velocity over a certain period of time.

When the speed is constant, it doesn't mean that its velocity is constant. Speed is a scalar quantity it doesn't have direction unlike velocity which is a vector quantity and it has a direction. In merry go round, the velocity (direction) is always changing even if its speed is constant which means it is accelerating.

Only at a constant velocity you have a zero acceleration.
