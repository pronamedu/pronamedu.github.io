24. Endurance is the ability to withstand and recover from physical and psychic fatigue. Describe which types of resistance and cite any sport in which the use of resistance is a priority?​


Answer:

There are four types of resistance: localized muscular resistance, strength resistance, aerobic resistance and anaerobic resistance. Endurance is a priority in marathons.

What types of resistors?

Endurance is a physical capacity that allows the individual to sustain a given effort for a longer time, maintaining quality.

Resistance types are:Localized muscular resistance: allows the individual to sustain heavy loads for a longer time. Ex: crossfit training;

Strength resistance: corresponds to resistance to heavy loads, without leading the body to fatigue;

Aerobic resistance: occurs when the individual can perform a certain activity for a long period. Ex: 10 km run, marathon, etc.;

Anaerobic resistance: allows intense effort, but without accumulating lactic acid. For example: strength training.

In marathons, 42 km races, there is a predominance of resistance because it allows the individual to run long distances without getting tired.
