8. The sum of Millie's and Martie's ages is 65.Three times Millies age is 15 years less than twice Marthy's age.What are their ages?​


Answer:

Millie is 23 years old and Martie is 42 years old.

Step-by-step explanation:

Let x & y be the ages of Millie and Martie respectively. (Assuming Martie and Marthy are the same person, lol)

Equation 1:

[tex]x + y = 65[/tex]

Equation 2:

[tex]3x = 2y - 15[/tex]

Solving the simultaneous equations above, we get x = 23 and y = 42.

Therefore, Millie is 23 years old and Martie is 42 years old.

a.) Kilo

symbol: K

Definitions: also a kilogramme[1]) is the base unit of mass in the International System of Units (SI), the metric system. It is a widely used measure in science, engineering and commerce worldwide, and is often simply called a kilo colloquially. It means 'one thousand grams'.

b.) Centi

Symbol: cm

Definitions: a unit prefix in the metric system denoting a factor of one hundredth.

c.) mega

symbol: M

dfns: a unit prefix in metric systems of units denoting a factor of one million (106 or 1000000).

d.)deci

symbol: d

dfns: a decimal unit prefix in the metric system denoting a factor of one tenth.

e. Milli

symbol: m/ mm

dfns: A prefix that means “one thousandth,” as in millimeter, one thousandth of a meter.

f. Micro

symbol: μm / mc

dfns: a gauge that measures small distances or thicknesses between its two faces, one of which can be moved away from or toward the other by turning a screw with a fine thread.

g. Nano

symbol: n/ nm

dfns: are used to measure wavelengths of light and distances between atoms in molecules.

h. pico

symbol: P

dfns: (used in units of measurement) denoting a factor of 10−12.
