Last Christmas, my godmother gave me (pink, three, beautiful) dresses.2. My mother bought (silver, secondhand, a) car.3. Jimmy owns (interesting, a lot of) books.4. During our summer vacation, we stayed in (cute, little, a) cottage.5. My sister ate (yellow, two) mangoes last night.​


Answer:

Directions: The following sentences are composed of series of adjectives  which are not arranged properly. Arrange the adjectives inside the parenthesis  in correct order.

1. Last Christmas, my godmother gave me beautiful, three, pink dresses.

beautiful = opinion three = numberpink = color

2. My mother bought a silver secondhand car.

a = determinersilver = colorsecondhand = origin

3. Jimmy owns a lot of interesting books.

a = determinerlot of = sizeinteresting = opinion

4. During our summer vacation, we stayed in a cute little cottage.

a = determinercute = opinionlittle = size

5. My sister ate two yellow mangoes last night.​

two = numberyellow = color

ORDER OF ADJECTIVESDeterminer - a, an, the, this, that, these, and those.Opinion - beautiful, terrible, great, horrible, difficult, silly.Size - large, big, tiny, small, gigantic, miniature,Age - young, old, new, ancient.Shape - round, square, triangular, rectangular, oval.Color - pink, yellow, purple, blue, black, red.Origin - Chinese, Korean, French, American, Filipino, Greek, Hebrew.Material - leather, cotton, silk, cloth, wooden, metal.Purpose - writing, cooking, washing, running.Noun - car, dress, book, pencil, comb.
