4. _____ allows people to make voice calls through the Internet. a. VoIP b. Online Chat c. Videoconferencing d. Wiki


Letter A

VoIP or Voice Over IP is the one that allows people to make voice calls through the internet. It is the transmission of voice and other contents like multimedia (videos and pictures) over IP or internet protocol networks. Some VoIP allows people to call others using the same technology (VoIP) while others allow people to call someone who is using a telephone number, whether it is mobile, long distance or even long distance number.

VoIP serves as a converter in the network. In order for the information to be transmitted, VoIP changes the data into digital data. The use of another device is needed to bring back the information to its standard form (e.i., voice which is an analog data).

5. Ang tawag sa nagtratrabaho sa call centers ay CSR ano Ang ibig Sabihin Ng R​


Answer: Representative

Explanation: What is a Customer Service Representative (CSR) in a Call Centre? A Customer Service Representative is a person employed to answer the customer, either by phone or by instant messaging, social media or by email. Customer service representatives may also be called contact centre advisors or call centre agents.

An agent who manages both incoming and outgoing customer calls is also referred to as a blended agent. Other names for a call center agent include customer service representative (CSR), contact center agent, telephone sales or service representative (TSR), attendant, associate, operator, account executive or team member.
