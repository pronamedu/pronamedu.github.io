6. A rocket ship is travelling at an average speed of 1.75 × 104 miles per hour. How many miles will the rocket ship travel in 1.2 × 102 hours?


Answer:

2.1 * 10⁶ miles

Step-by-step explanation:

We use the formula for distance with regards rate and time,

Distance = Rate * Time

Substitute the given,

Distance = (1.75 * 10⁴) * (1.2 * 10²)

Distance = 2.1 * 10⁶

Therefore, the rocket ship will travel 2.1 * 10⁶ miles.

14. An airplane flies 70 miles in 102 minutes. What is its average speed in miles per hour?


Answer:

We can use the formula for average speed:

average speed = total distance / total time

In this case, the total distance is 70 miles and the total time is 102 minutes. To find the average speed in miles per hour, we need to convert the time to hours:

102 minutes = 102/60 hours = 1.7 hours (rounded to one decimal place)

Now we can plug in the values:

average speed = 70 miles / 1.7 hours

Using a calculator or long division, we get:

average speed = 41.18 miles per hour (rounded to two decimal places)

Therefore, the average speed of the airplane is 41.18 miles per hour.
