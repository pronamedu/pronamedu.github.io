6. The cost of transporting 30 sacks of rice for 115 kilometers in a truck is 1,500.00. What will be the cost of transporting 35 sacks for 125 kilometers?​


Answer: It will cost 1902.17

[tex]{}[/tex]

Step-by-step explanation:

The cost of transporting 30 sacks of rice for 115 kilometers in a truck is 1,500.00

[tex]{}[/tex]

Let c be the total cost

Let n be the number of sacks

Let d be the distance

[tex]{}[/tex]

If number of sacks increases, cost increases If the distance increases, cost increases

[tex]{}[/tex]

Cost varies jointly with number of sacks and the distance

[tex]{}[/tex]

c ∝ nd

c = knd

[tex]{}[/tex]

Where k is the constant of variation

[tex]{}[/tex]

cost of transporting 30 sacks of rice for 115 kilometers in a truck is 1,500.

[tex]{}[/tex]

When n = 30, d = 115, c = 1500

1500 = k(30)(115)

[tex]\sf\large{k\:=\: \frac{1500}{30\:×\:115}}[/tex]

[tex]\sf\large{k\:=\: \frac{50}{115}}[/tex]

[tex]\sf\large{k\:=\: \frac{10}{23}}[/tex]

[tex]\sf\large{c\:=\: \frac{10nd}{23}}[/tex]

[tex]{}[/tex]

When n = 35; d = 125

[tex]\sf\large{c\:=\: \frac{10(35)(125)}{23}}[/tex]

[tex]\sf\large{c\:=\: \frac{43750}{23}}[/tex]

c = 1902.17
