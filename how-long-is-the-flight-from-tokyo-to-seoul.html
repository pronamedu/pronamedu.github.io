6. A direct flight from Manila to Tokyo leaves Manila at 12:50 am and arrives in Tokyo at 6:10 am on the same day. If Japan time is one hour ahead of Philippine time, how many hours does it take to fly from Manila to Tokyo?


Answer:

4 hours 20 mins


Step-by-step explanation:

Tokyo is one hour ahead of Manila

6.10 am in Manila = 5.10 in Tokyo


Find the duration from 12.50am to 5.10 am

12.50am to 4:50 am = 4 hours

4:50 am to 5:00 am = 10 mins

5:00 am to 5:10 am = 10 mins

Total duration = 4 hours + 10 mins + 10 mins = 4 hours 20 mins


Answer: 4 hours 20 mins
