Answer:

Jose Rizal, also known as Jose Protacio Rizal Mercado y Alonso Realonda, was the national hero of the Philippines. His mother, Teodora Alonso Realonda, was a highly educated woman from a well-respected family in the Philippines. She was able to teach her children, including Jose Rizal, to read and write at a young age and instilled in them a love for learning and education. Teodora Alonso Realonda was known to be a strong and independent woman, which greatly influenced her son Rizal throughout his life. She was able to provide him a strong foundation on his education that paved the way for his future achievements.

10. Jose Rizal Father's Side ​


Jose Rizal Family tree that can be traced from his Father

Jose Rizal’s father is Don Francisco Engracio Rizal Mercado, who became ‘tiniente gobernadorcillo’ (lieutenant governor) in Calamba and was thus nicknamed ‘Tiniente Kiko’.

Francisco’s great grandfather is Domingo Lam-co, a learned pro-poor or ‘maka-masa’ Chinese immigrant businessman who married a sophisticated Chinese mestiza of Manila named Ines de la Rosa. One of their two children, Francisco (also), resided in Biñan and married Bernarda Monicha.

Answer:

Jose Rizal's father's side of the family included his father, Francisco Mercado Rizal, and his father's parents, Juan and Cirila Alejandro. Francisco Mercado Rizal was the son of a wealthy Chinese merchant from the town of Binan. He married Teodora Alonzo, Jose Rizal's mother, and the two had 11 children, including Jose. Juan and Cirila Alejandro were the parents of Francisco Mercado Rizal, and the couple had five children.
