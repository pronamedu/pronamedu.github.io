8. 2.If Set A = MICHAEL and Set B = JORDAN, what is A⋂ B?


Answer:

{}

Explanation:

"∩" is the intersection of two sets. It means all elements/items that are in both of the sets.

For example, if:

A = {1, 2, 3}

B = {4, 6, 2, 1}

A∩B will be {1, 2} because 1 and 2 are items that are in both sets A and B.

In the problem:

A = {MICHAEL}

B = {JORDAN}

A∩B will be {} because there are no same items in sets A and B.
