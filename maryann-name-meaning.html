Answer:

3.6m

Step-by-step explanation:

arrangement is

Maryann -- Pole -- Shiela

total distance (M to S) = 6.85m

dist. of M to P = 3.25m

dist. of P to S = total distance - dist. of M to P

= 6.85 - 3.25 = 3.6

Answer:

Step-by-step explanation:

To solve this problem, we can use the concept of proportions. Let x be the number of ropes that are 70cm long, and y be the number of ropes that are 40cm long. We can then set up the following equation:

70x + 40y = 18400 (1)

where 70x represents the total length of the ropes that are 70cm long, and 40y represents the total length of the ropes that are 40cm long.

We also know that there are a total of 34 ropes, so we have:

x + y = 34 (2)

We can then solve for x by substituting (2) into (1) and simplifying:

70x + 40(34-x) = 18400

70x + 1360 - 40x = 18400

30x = 17040

x = 568

Therefore, Edith cut 568 ropes that are 70cm long.

Answer: 568 ropes

Let's use algebra to solve this problem. Let x be the weight of Ruth in kg. Then, Lilia weighs x + 9 kg, and Maryann weighs x + 9 + 7 = x + 16 kg. The total weight of the three is 127kg, so we have:

x + (x + 9) + (x + 16) = 127

Simplifying and solving for x, we get:

3x + 25 = 127

3x = 102

x = 34

Therefore, Ruth weighs 34 kg, Lilia weighs 43 kg, and Maryann weighs 50 kg.
