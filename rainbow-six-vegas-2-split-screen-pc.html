20. white light is a mixture of colors that can be split into visible spectrum by __a. rainbowb. prisonc. waved. prism​


d. Prism

Explanation:

A prism spreads white light into its various component wavelengths, or colours. When white light is spread apart by a prism or a diffraction grating, the colours of the visible spectrum appear. The colours vary according to their wavelengths.

Another Explanation:

White light is a mixture of all colors of light. This mixture can be split into different colors using a transparent block called a prism. Prisms are specially shaped so that light passing through them bends. Some colors bend more than others as they pass through the prism, so they split apart.
