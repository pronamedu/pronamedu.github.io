24. what is the volume of isopropyl alcohol in a 1 400 ml rubbing alcohol containing 70% isopropyl alcohol by volume? A. 70 ML B.140 ML C.980 ML D.1 400 ML (please kailangan ko na ito)


SOLUTION:

[tex]\text{volume of solute} = \dfrac{\text{percent by volume}}{100\%} \times \text{volume of solution}[/tex]

[tex]\text{volume of solute} = \dfrac{70\%}{100\%} \times \text{1,400 mL}[/tex]

[tex]\boxed{\text{volume of solute = 980 mL}}[/tex]

ANSWER:

C. 980 mL
