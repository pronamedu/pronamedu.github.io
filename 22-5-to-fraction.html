22. Reduce the fraction to its lowest form: 22/50 *22/505/101/211/25​


Answer:

The simplest form of  

22 /50  is   11 /25

Step-by-step explanation:

Steps to simplifying fractions

Find the GCD (or HCF) of numerator and denominator

GCD of 22 and 50 is 2

Divide both the numerator and denominator by the GCD

22/50 ÷ 2 /2

Reduced fraction:  

11 /25

Therefore, 22/50 simplified to lowest terms is 11/25.
