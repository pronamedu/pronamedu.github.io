9. what is the volume of water in a 500ml bottle of rubbing alcohol that has a concentration of 70% by volume


Answer:

150 mL

Explanation:

If there is 70% alcohol, then there is 30% water.

The 70% means that 70% of the 500mL is alcohol

[tex] \implies 500\times 70\% [/tex]

[tex] = 500\times \frac{7}{10}[/tex]

[tex]=\frac{500\times7}{10}[/tex]

[tex]=\frac{50\times10\times7}{10}[/tex]

[tex]=50(7)=\bold{350}[/tex]

Therefore, the volume of alcohol is 350mL

If there is 500mL then the volume of water is

[tex] \implies 500-350 mL[/tex]

[tex] =\fbox{150 mL } [/tex]

percent by volume = 8%

volume of solution = 500 mL

Required:
