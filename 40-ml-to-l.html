To convert from °C to Kelvin, add 273.15 to the temperature in °C.

At 30 °C and 3 atm, the initial volume of the gas can be calculated as:

P1V1 = P2V2, where P1 and V1 are the initial pressure and volume, and P2 and V2 are the final pressure and volume.
