19. the sun is shining brightly in the meadow lets go out a.persuade B.inform C.entertain​


Answer:

A. Persuade

The sentence "the sun is shining brightly in the meadow, let's go out" is an example of persuasion. The speaker is suggesting or encouraging the listener to go out and enjoy the sunny weather in the meadow. The sentence is not informative, as it does not provide any new information or facts. It also is not entertaining, as it does not aim to entertain or amuse the listener.
