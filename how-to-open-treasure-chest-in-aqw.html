Answer:

The length of the treasure chest with an area less than 16ft² should be less than 8ft and its width should be less than 2ft.

Explanation:

w (w + 6) < 16

w² + 6w < 16

w² + 6w - 16 < 0

w² + 8w - 2w - 16 < 0

w (w + 8) - 2 (w + 8) < 0

(w + 8)(w - 2) < 0

w + 8 < 0 , w - 2 > 0

w₁ < - 8 , w₂ > 2

If you're asking about its greatest possible dimension/s with an area less than 16ft²:

Length < w + 6 , Width < w₂

Length < 2 + 6 , Width < 2

Length < 8 , Width < 2

Therefore, the length of the treasure chest with an area less than 16ft² should be less than 8ft and its width should be less than 2ft.

Bonus:

If you're asking about its greatest possible dimension/s with an area of 16ft²:
