I have had a very busy day. It was so untidy. I have fixed

my bedroom. I have  finished my assignment. I had two

assignments in English and Math. English is my favorite

subject at school. I have helped my Mom. She wanted me

to make lunch for my uncle and Aunt. I have talked to

my friend on the telephone, too. Aunt. I have not visited

my grandparents. I missed them very much, .​

Explanation:

The present perfect tense refers to an action or state that either occurred at an indefinite time in the past (e.g., we have talked before) or began in the past and continued to the present time (e.g., he has grown impatient over the last hour). This tense is formed by have/has + the past participle.
