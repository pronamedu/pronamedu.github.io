4. Differentiate the different kinds of forms:Binary form-Temary form-Rondo form-​


Answer:

Binary form is a musical form in 2 related sections, both of which are usually repeated.

Ternary form, sometimes called song form is a three-part musical form consisting of an opening section, a following section and then a repetition of the first.

Rondo form is an instrumental musical form introduced in the Classical period.

Binary form is A-B and Ternary form is A-B-A then Rondo form is

A-B-A-C-A-D-A.

Each new section contrasts in some way to what has gone before it.
