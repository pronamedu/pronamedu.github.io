This increase of samsung phone users will also add up to the demand of phone cases. As an effect, its price has the tendency to increase applying the law of demand and supply. This indicates that when an item has a high demand in the market, there is a possibility for its price to increase.

Answer:

1. Yes, Samsung's participation in a sporting event can contribute to the enhancement of its brand image. By associating itself with a popular and widely followed sporting event, Samsung can reach out to a large audience and build a positive perception of its brand. Furthermore, by sponsoring or endorsing athletes or teams, Samsung can create emotional connections with its target audience and improve brand loyalty.

2. To build its brand image as a global brand, Samsung must use a multi-faceted strategy that includes a strong focus on innovation, product quality, customer service, and marketing. Samsung must also focus on building a strong corporate culture and reputation for ethical business practices. Additionally, Samsung should consider partnerships and collaborations with other global brands, as well as targeted marketing campaigns in key markets around the world.

3. Samsung must take into account several factors if it wants to undertake globally brand building initiatives, including cultural differences, market trends, consumer preferences, and regulatory requirements in different countries. Samsung must also ensure that its brand message and marketing strategies are relevant and resonate with local audiences. Additionally, Samsung must have a strong understanding of its competitors and their strengths and weaknesses in different markets.

4. Whether Samsung needs to rebrand to capture other segments of the market depends

on several factors, including the current brand perception, market trends, and competitive landscape. Rebranding can be a complex and costly process, and it may not always be necessary or advisable. Instead, Samsung may choose to develop new products, expand into new markets, or launch targeted marketing campaigns to reach new segments of the market while maintaining its existing brand image.
