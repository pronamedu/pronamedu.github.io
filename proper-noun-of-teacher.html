24. common nouns or proper nouns.1.teacher 2.colgate 3.restaurant4.pasig river 5.day​


Common nouns  and proper nouns

Answer:

teacher : common noun colgate : proper noun restaurant : common noun pasig river : proper noun day​ : common noun

Explanation:

A proper noun is a specific name for a particular person, place, or thing and is always capitalized. Common examples of proper nouns include people's names, geographical locations, and brand names.

A common noun is a word that refers to a person, place, thing, or idea. The word "common" simply means that the noun is not specific. Common nouns are not capitalized unless they are the first word in a sentence.

For example, the word "city" is a common noun. It refers to any city, such as New York City, Los Angeles, or Paris.
