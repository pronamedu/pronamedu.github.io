2. What are the Classification ofSpoon broom ToothbrushLampChairAirconBallpenLight bulbWheel chairGadget​


Answer:

spoon

A spoon is a utensil consisting of a small shallow bowl (also known as a head), oval or round, at the end of a handle

broom

A broom is a cleaning tool consisting of usually stiff fibers (often made of materials such as plastic, hair, or corn husks) attached to, and roughly parallel to, a cylindrical handle, the broomstick.

Toothbrush

Toothbrushes are classified as soft, medium, or hard based on the diameter of these bristles. The diameter ranges for these classifications are 0.16 to 0.22 mm for soft, 0.23 to 0.29 mm for medium, and 0.30 mm and above for hard.

Lamp

Electric lamps may be classified into three different groups: (1) Arc lamps; (2) Filament lamps; (3) Gas discharge lamps in which discharge may take place in sodium vapour or mercury vapour or neon gas.

50 gadgets cost P82000, 100 gadgets cost P157000

f(x)=1500x+7000

f(50)=1500(50)+7000

f(50)=75000+7000

f(50)=82000

f(x)=1500x+7000

f(100)=150000+7000
