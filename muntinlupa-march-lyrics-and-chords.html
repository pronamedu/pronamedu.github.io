6. What is meaning of the logo in muntinlupa


Muntinlupa City’s seal, which was designed by the son of the Philippine National Artist Fernando Amorsolo – Manuel Amorsolo, has the following meaning:

 

1.   Philippine Eagle – symbolizes the city’s desire to soar and the fulfilment of its vision to become a premier city.

2.   Bamboo – symbolizes the people’s resilience and ability to easily cope up.

3.   The words “Lakas, Talino at Buhay” – literally pertain to the values of the citizens.

4.   Nine stars – symbolizes the nine barangays that comprises the city.

29. what is the meaning of muntinlupa?​


Answer:

Muntinlupa is an old term, which refers to a village now known as the Poblacion. As early as 1800, the Catholic priest Father Joaquin de Zuniga chronicled it as La Poblacion que Sigue se Llama Muntinlupa. As early as 1601, Muntinlupa was under the religious supervision of the Augustinians.
