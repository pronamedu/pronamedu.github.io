25. Mang Juan bought 60 crates of chicos. Each crate contained 150 chicos. He repacked them into bags of 6 chicos each. How many bags of chicos did he make?​


25 each bag
150 divided 6 =25

Answer:

Mang Juan made 1500 bags of chicos.

Step-by-step explanation:

Given:

Number of crates of Chicos: 60

1 crate = 150 chicos

1 bag = 6 chicos

Question: How many bags of chicos did he make?

Step:

Multiply the number of crates by the number of chicos per crate.

60 × 150 = 9000

Therefore there are 9000 chicos.

Then divide the total number of chicos by the number of chicos in each bag.

9000 ÷ 6 = 1500

Therefore, Mang Juan made 1500 bags of chicos.
