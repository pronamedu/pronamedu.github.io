22. slogan tungkol sa karapatan ng kababaihan, lagyan ng repleksyon ang slogan


Answer:

Every women should be respected and recognized

Explanation:

the statement implies that the rights of every women should be recognized and respected just like how the rights of men are recognized. Also, everyone, regardless of their gender, should be treated fairly and be given the same opportunities for them to showcase their capabilities and talents in contributing to the society.  Hence, let's be fair to everyone and promote women's empowerment.
