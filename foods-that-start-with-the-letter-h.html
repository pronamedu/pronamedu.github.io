1. What do you call the food thay contains 75% simple sugar and 25% water. The answer is said to start in letter H​


Answer:

Honey

Explanation:

Honey contains around 75% simple sugar and 25% water.

To make one pound of honey, the bees in the colony must visit 2 million flowers, fly over 55,000 miles and will be the lifetime work of approximately 768 bees.

A single honeybee will only produce approximately 1/12 teaspoon of honey in her lifetime.
