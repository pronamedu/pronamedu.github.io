1. Nikki poured 3/2 cup of condensed milk equally into 4 plastic cups .how much condensed milk does each cup have


Answer:

3/8 each cup.

Step-by-step explanation:

3/2 divided by 4.

3 x 1 3

---------- = -----

2 x 4 8

Given:

[tex]\frac{3}{2}[/tex] cup of condensed milk

4 plastic cups

The condensed milk was poured equally so we need to divide the number of plastic cups to the number of condensed milk.

[tex]\frac{3}{2}[/tex] ÷ 4

Turn 4 into fractions : [tex]\frac{4}{1}[/tex]

[tex]\frac{3}{2}[/tex] ÷ [tex]\frac{4}{1}[/tex]

In dividing fractions, the 2nd term/given must be reciprocal and turn the division into multiplication

What and how to get reciprocal : https://brainly.ph/question/1894764

[tex]\frac{3}{2}[/tex] × [tex]\frac{1}4}[/tex]

Multiply numerator by numerator, denominator by denominator

3 × 1 = 3

2 × 4 = 8

Answer: [tex]\frac{3}{8}[/tex] condensed milk in each cup

Checking:

condensed milk in each cup × plastic cups

[tex]\frac{3}{8}[/tex] × 4 = [tex]\frac{12}{8}[/tex] or [tex]\frac{3}{2}[/tex]
