18. You look at a local newspaper to post your advertisement. You find that it is going to cost you $50 for the first 200 words. Anything over 200 words will be an additional $0.05 per word. Write a piecewise function to determine how you would calculate the cost of your advertisement. If your advertisement was exactly 250 words, how much would it cost? Calculate how much YOUR advertisement would cost, if you used 87 words


Answer:

If the advertisement has 250 words, its cost is $52.50.

The cost for 80 words is $50.

Step-by-step explanation:

Number of words: x

Piecewise function:

f(x) = 50 ; x ≤ 200

f(x) = 50 + 0.05x ; x > 200

As one function:

f(x) = 50 + 0.05 (x - 200)  where x > 200

(a) If x = 250, calculate the cost:

f(250) = 50 + 0.05 (250 - 200)

f(250) = 50 + 0.05(50)

f(250) = 50 + 2.50

f(250) = $52.50

If the advertisement has 250 words, its cost is $52.50.

(b) If x = 80 words, calculate the cost:

Since 80 ≤ 200, then:

f(x) = 50  where  x ≤ 200

f(80) = 50

The cost for 80 words is $50.

25. TYPES OF ADVERTISING 1. Paid Advertising DESCRIBE HOW YOU USE IT COST​


Paid advertising refers to any form of advertising where the advertiser pays to have their message or product promoted to a specific audience. There are many different types of paid advertising, including online advertising (such as display ads, search engine ads, social media ads, and video ads), print advertising (such as ads in newspapers, magazines, and brochures), and broadcast advertising (such as TV and radio ads).

If you want to use paid advertising to promote your product or service, the cost will depend on a variety of factors, such as the platform you use, the type of ad you create, the targeting options you choose, and the competition for ad space.
