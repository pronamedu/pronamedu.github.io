7. Bert has $8.80 in pennies and nickels. If there are twice as many nickels as pennies, how many pennies does Bert have? How many nickels? (ANS: 80 pennies, 160 nickels)


Answer:

80 pennies, 160 nickels

Step-by-step explanation:

First, we need to determine the equivalent amount of a penny and a nickel.

A penny worths 1 cent, while a nickel worths 5 cents. Thus, one nickel is equivalent to 5 pennies.

Now, let us assume that x is the number of pennies.

pennies = x

nickels = 5(2x)

Next, let us form an equation. Since the total amount of the pennies and nickels is $8.80, we will convert it into cents. One dollar is equivalent to 100 cents. Therefore, the total amount of pennies and nickels is 880 cents.

Then, the equation should be as follows:

x + 5(2x) = 880

x + 10x = 880

11x = 880

11x = 880

11 11

x = 80

Therefore, there are 80 pennies.

Finally, let us determine the number of nickels.

5(2x) = 5*2*80 = 800 cents

We will convert it into nickels by dividing it into 5.

800/5 = 160

Therefore, there are 160 nickels.
