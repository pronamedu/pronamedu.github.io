13. Character in finding dory​


Answer:

Dory: a blue tang fish with short-term memory loss

Marlin: a clownfish and Dory's friend from the first movie, "Finding Nemo"

Nemo: a young clownfish and Marlin's son

Hank: a "septopus" (an octopus missing one of its eight tentacles) who helps Dory navigate the Marine Life Institute

Destiny: a near-sighted whale shark who becomes Dory's friend
