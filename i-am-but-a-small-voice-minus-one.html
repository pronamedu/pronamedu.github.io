1. I am thinking of a number minus 12 equals 60. What is the number I am thinking of?​


Answer:

x = 72

Step-by-step explanation:

Let x be the number

x - 12 = 60

Transpose 12 and change its sign

x = 60 + 12

Add the numbers

x = 72

Checking:

x - 12 = 60

72 - 12 = 60

4n/4=72/4

n=18

The number is 18.
