23. slogan about the power of unity during this pandemic explain it briefly ​


Answer:

If we unite, we can overcome everything. If we are divided, we will lose even before we started.

Explanation:

If people are united there is more chance for us of surviving this pandemic. For example, if all of us will observe health protocols strictly, the virus will not spread easily. However, if there are those who still believe that the virus is fake, and do not wear facemasks, then the virus can spread easier. Hence, in order to win against the pandemic, it is important that we cooperate with each other.
