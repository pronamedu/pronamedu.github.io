30. find the area hectare of the following 600 m x 600 m​


Answer:

36 ha

Step-by-step explanation:

Area of Square formula

A = a²

Where A is the area and a is the side length.

Given: a = 600m (this is a square since both sides has 600 m)

Plug it in on the formula

A = (600 m)²

A = 360,000 m²

Hectare (ha) is 10,000 m².

Hence, we can divide the 360,000 m² to 10,000 to form it on ha.

Therefore, the area is 36 ha.
