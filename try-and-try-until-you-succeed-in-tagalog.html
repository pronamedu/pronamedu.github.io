9. If you try to fail and succeed, then which have you done?


Answer:

It is quite complicated to reach any conclusion in this case because if you see it like "you wanted to fail at something and eventually got failed, it means that you have successfully completed your task". Hurray, you are successful.

But in a different aspect, your aim was to fail at something and you have successfully completed your task, which means that you have failed to get failed. Bad luck buddy, you are unsuccessful.

Let's understand this with an example:

You have decided to fail in your exam and eventually get failed. Since your actual aim was to fail in the exam and you have successfully achieved the feat, which means that you are successful because you have successfully completed what you had decided before exams.

Conclusion:

The simplest answer to this question is "You have successfully failed".
