13. the product of 25 and 6 is how much greater than the sum of 25 and 6??​


• Problem:

The product of 25 and 6 is how much greater than the sum of 25 and 6?

• Solution:

You need to solve first the product of 25 and 6. This means that you need to multiply 25 and 6.

[tex] \tt25 \times 6 = 150[/tex]

Then solve for the second one, sum of 25 and 6. You need to add both of the given quantities since sum is the result in addition.

[tex] \tt25 + 6 = 31[/tex]

To answer the question, you need to subtract the first answer to the second one.

[tex] \tt150 - 31 = 119[/tex]

• Answer:

The product of 25 and 6 is 119 greater than the sum of 25 and 6.
