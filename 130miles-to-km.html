Answer:

B. 20 m/min

Step-by-step explanation:

The formula of speed is:

[tex]s = \frac{d}{t} [/tex]

Thus:

[tex]s = \frac{400}{20}[/tex]

[tex]s = 20[/tex]

Since the distance is in meter or m and the time measures in seconds.

Therefore, the car travels with the speed of [tex]\boxed{\tt{20m/min}}[/tex]

25. if a car is driving 30miles every 6mins how long is the distance​


Answer:

3 miles

Step-by-step explanation:

The formula for the distance is
Distance = speed x  time
speed = 30 miles ( This should be mph or miles per hour)

time = 6 mins
But you need to convert 6 mins into hour
6 mins = 0.1 hour

Now substitute the values
Distance = 30 miles (mph) x 0.1 hour
Distance = 30 miles (mph) x 0.1
Distance = 3 miles
