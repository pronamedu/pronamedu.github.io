21. Find the common factors for at least first 3 possible multiples of each numberusing listing method.1) 25 and 752) 35 and 453) 21 and 56​


Answer:

The common factors for at least the first 3 possible multiples of each number are the following;  

1. 25 and 75 is 75

.

2. 35 and 45 is 315

.

3. 21 and 65 is 168

.

Step-by-step explanation:

The meaning of multiple is the product after one number is multiplied by another. For example, we are supposed to find the first seven multiples of 14, then the answer should be 14, 28, 42, 56, 70, 84, and 98.

Different Types of Common Factors Greatest Common FactorLeast Common Factor or Least Common Multiple

The solutions can be presented in two methods. Multiples of 14

           [tex]\begin{aligned}14&=14\\14+14&=28\\28+14&=42\\42+14&=56\\56+14&=70\\70+14&=84\\84+14&=98\end{aligned}[/tex]          [tex]\begin{aligned}14\times 1&=14\\14 \times2 &= 28\\14 \times 3 &=42\\14 \times 4&=56\\14 \times 5 &=70\\14\times 6 &=84\\14 \times 7&=98\end{aligned}[/tex]

To solve the problem, we just need to list at least the first three multiples of the given two numbers and choose the common multiple/factors.

1) 25 and 75      

[tex]\begin{array}{ccccc} 25 &\rightarrow&25,&50,&\boxed{75}\\75&\rightarrow&\boxed{75},&50,&225\end{array}[/tex] From the given multiples, this clearly shows that the common multiple/factor found in 25 and 75 is 75.

2) 35 and 45

[tex]\begin{array}{ccccccccccc} 35 &\rightarrow&35,&70,&105,&140, &175,& 210,&245,& 280,& \boxed{315}\\45&\rightarrow&45,&90,&135, &180, &225, &270, &\boxed{315}\end{array}[/tex] This clearly shows that the common multiple/factor is 315.

3) 21 and 56

[tex]\begin{array}{cccccccccc} 21 &\rightarrow&21,&42,&63, &84, &105, &126, &147, &\boxed{168}\\56&\rightarrow&56,&112,&\boxed{168}\end{array}[/tex] We are able to find a common factor, in this case, it's 168.
