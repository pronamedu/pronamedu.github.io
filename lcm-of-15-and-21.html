10. Which of the following is incorrect? a. The LCM of 12 and 16 is 48. b. The LCM of 15 and 30 is 15. c. The LCM of 12 and 18 is 36. d. The LCM of 15 and 21 is 105.


Answer and Explanation:

Which of the following is incorrect?

A. The LCM of 12 and 16 is 48. ✔️

B. The LCM of 15 and 30 is 15.

C. The LCM of 12 and 18 is 36. ✔️

D. The LCM of 15 and 21 is 105. ✔️

The LCM ( Least Common Multiple ) of 15 and 30 is 30.

Explanation:

To find the LCM of the pairs, we need to multiply the two digits to find the Least Common Multiple until we find the same digit.

Example:

Find the least common multiple of 10 and 30

×1 10 ×1 30✔️

×2 20 ×2 60

×3 30✔️ ×3 90

The LCM is 30

[tex]\large\bold{Least \: Common \: Multiple}[/tex]

[tex]___________________________________[/tex]

[tex]\huge{{\underline{{\sf{Question}}}}}[/tex]

Which of the following is incorrect?

[tex]\huge{{\underline{{\sf{Choices}}}}}[/tex]

a. The LCM of 12 and 16 is 48.

b. The LCM of 15 and 30 is 15.

c. The LCM of 12 and 18 is 36.

d. The LCM of 15 and 21 is 105.

[tex]\large\sf{Further \: Explanation} \: \downarrow[/tex]

Least Common Multiple or LCM is the smallest value that can be divide by the two numbers that being addressed.

[tex]\huge\mathfrak{Solution}[/tex]

[tex]\sf{Least \: Common \: Multiple \: of \: 12 \: and \: 1 6}[/tex]

[tex]\sf{Multiple \: of \: 12 \downarrow}[/tex]

[tex]\rm{12 = 12,24,36,\red{48},60}[/tex]

[tex]\sf{Multiple \: of \: 16 \downarrow}[/tex]

[tex]\rm{16 =16,32,\red{48},64}[/tex]

The LCM of 12 & 16 is 48. Letter A is correct.

[tex]\sf{Least \: Common \: Multiple \: of \: 30 \: and \: 15}[/tex]

[tex]\sf{Multiple \: of \: 15 \downarrow}[/tex]

[tex]\rm{15 = 15,\red{30},45,60}[/tex]

[tex]\sf{Multiple \: of \: 30 \downarrow}[/tex]

[tex]\rm{30 = \red{30},60,90,120}[/tex]

The LCM of 15 & 30 is 30. Letter B is incorrect.

[tex]\sf{Least \: Common \: Multiple \: of \: 12 \: and \: 18}[/tex]

[tex]\sf{Multiple \: of \: 12 \downarrow}[/tex]

[tex]\rm{12 = 12,24,\red{36},48,60}[/tex]

[tex]\sf {Multiple \: of \: 18 \downarrow}[/tex]

[tex]\rm{18 = 18,\red{36},54,70}[/tex]

The LCM of 12 & 18 is 36. Letter C is correct.

[tex]\sf{Least \: Common \: Multiple \: of \: 15 \: and \: 21}[/tex]

[tex]\sf{Multiple \: of \: 15 \downarrow}[/tex]

[tex]\rm{15 = 15,30,45,60,75,90,\red{105},120}[/tex]

[tex]\sf{Multiple \: of \: 21 \downarrow}[/tex]

[tex]\rm{21 = 21,42,63,84,\red{105},126}[/tex]

The LCM of 15 & 21 is 105. Letter D is correct.

Hence, the Option B is incorrect.
