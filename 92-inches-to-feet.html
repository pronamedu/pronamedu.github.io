V = khb where k is a constant

rectangle

length = 12 ft

width = 6 ft

triangle

b = 4 ft

h = 5 ft

A = A_rectangle + A_triangle

= (l × w) + (1/2bh)

= 72 ft² + 10 ft²

A = 82 ft²
