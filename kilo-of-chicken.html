price of 3 kilograms of chicken and 2 kilograms of is rupees 600.

price price of 2 kilograms of chicken and 4 kilograms of face is rupees 700.

To find,

The price of 1 kilogram of chicken and 1 kilogram of fish.

Solution,

Let, price of 1 kg of chicken = Rs x

Price of 1 kg of fish = Rs y

Price of 3kg chicken = Rs 3x

Price of 2kg fish = Rs 2y

Price of 2kg chicken = Rs 2x

Price of 4kg fish = Rs 4y

According to the question,

3x+2y = 600

2×(3x+2y) = 2×600

6x+4y = 1200 (1)

2x+4y = 700 (2)

By, subtracting (2) from (1), we get that,

6x+4y-2x-4y = 1200-700

4x = 500

x = 125

Putting the value of x in (2), we get that,

2×(125) + 4y = 700

250+4y = 700

4y = 700-250

4y = 450

y = 112.50

Hence, price of 1kg chicken is Rs. 125 and 1kg fish is 112.50

29. One kilo of chicken is P115.00. How much is 2 3/5 kilos of chicken?​


To solve this problem, we can use multiplication and addition.

First, we need to find the cost of one kilo of chicken:

1 kilo of chicken = P115.00

Next, we need to find the cost of 2 3/5 kilos of chicken:

2 3/5 kilos = 2 + 3/5 kilos (we can add the whole number and the fraction separately)

2 + 3/5 = 13/5 (we can convert the mixed number to an improper fraction by multiplying the whole number by the denominator and adding the numerator)

So, 2 3/5 kilos can be written as 13/5 kilos.

To find the cost of 13/5 kilos of chicken, we can multiply the cost of one kilo by 13/5:

Cost of 13/5 kilos of chicken = P115.00/kilo x 13/5 kilos

Multiplying, we get:

Cost of 13/5 kilos of chicken = P299.80

Therefore, 2 3/5 kilos of chicken costs P299.80.
