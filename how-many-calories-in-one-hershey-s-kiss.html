10. there are 225 candies in a box. the candies are hershey's, goya and muncher in terms of brands. if 20% of the candies are hershey's, 1/15 of the candies are goya. how many candies are munchers?​


Answer:

165 munchers

Step-by-step explanation:

Jusy add the number of hersheys and goyas then subtract it to the total number of candies in the box to know how many munchers are there.

Getting the number of hersheys.

[tex]225 \times 20\% \\ = 225 \times 0.2 \\ = 45[/tex]

Getting the number of goyas.

[tex]225 \times \frac{1}{15} \\ = \frac{225}{15} \\ = 15[/tex]

Now, let's get the number of munchers.

[tex]225 - (45 + 15) \\ = 225 - 60 \\ = 165[/tex]

Therefore, there are 165 candies that are muncher in the box.
