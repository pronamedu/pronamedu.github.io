2. Jin had 50% fewer erasers than Dwayne. After Dwayne gave 15 of his erasers to Jin, Jin had 40% fewer erasers than Dwayne. How many erasers did Jin have at first?​


Answer:

Jin had 50% erasers than Dwayne. So:

Jin's erasers = 50% of Dwayne's erasers.

Dwayne gave 15 erasers to jin, then he had 40% erasers. So:

Jin's erasers - 15 = 40% of Dwayne's erasers.

Now let jin's erasers be x and Dwayne's erasers be y. Therefore we can write the above statements as:

x = 50% × y

=> x = 0.5y ___(1)

x + 15 = 40% × y

=> x - 15 = 0.4y

=> x = 0.4y + 15.___(2)

From (1) and (2):

0.5y = 0.4y + 15.

0.1y = + 15.

y = 150.

Now, x = 50% × y

= 50% × 150

= 75.

Conversion of Quarts to Cups:

There are 8 cups in 2 quarts. To convert quarts to cups, consider the amount of cups in a quart. In the English system of measurement 1 quart is equivalent to 4 cups. Using this equivalent we can now convert 2 quarts to cups.

Solutions:

2 quarts = ______cups

Given: 1 quart = 4 cups

2 quarts x 4 cups = 8 cups

                 1 quart

Therefore, there are 8 cups in 2 quarts.

Given: 1 quart = 4 cups

10 cups x 1 quart = 10 quarts = 2.5 quarts or 2 1/2 quarts

Therefore, 2.5  or 2 1/2 quarts of sugar were used by Melissa for her baking class.
