Scott Joplin composed the "Maple Leaf Rag" (copyright registered on September 18, 1899)[1], an early ragtime musical work for piano. Despite the demise of ragtime following Joplin's death in 1917, the "Maple Leaf Rag" was recorded by a number of well-known performers.
