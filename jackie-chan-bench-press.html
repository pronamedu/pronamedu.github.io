Question:

Identify if the activity is anaerobic or aerobic exercise.

bench press

Answer:

anaerobic exercise

Bench press is an example of physical exercise which strengthen the muscles such as: Pectoralis major in your chest, deltoids muscle in the shoulder, arm muscle like triceps and biceps, it also strengthen the latissimus dorsi muscle. Usually, if you just started doing the exercise, you will experience muscle pain on the target muscles due to anaerobic metabolism on those muscle which produces lactate (lactic acid) to stimulate the pain receptor and causes muscle pain that is why it is considered as anaerobic exercise.

Anaerobic exercises demands more oxygen exceeds the oxygen available in the organ. When you organs or muscles do some activity without oxygen, it produces lactate which stimulate the pain receptor to cause muscle pain. This exercise is only short-lasting but the intensity of the activity is high.
