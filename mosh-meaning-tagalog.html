PROBLEM

Moshe saves 25,000.00 in the bank. If this is 90% of her monthly salary, how much is her monthly salary?

ANSWER 22,500

FORMULA

[tex]percentage \: = \: \frac{n \times 100}{y} [/tex]

SOLUTION

[tex]90 \% \times 25000[/tex]

Reduce the fraction 90/100 to lowest terms by extracting and canceling out 10

[tex]\frac{9}{10}\times 25000 [/tex]

Express 9/10 x 25,000 as a single fraction.

[tex]\frac{9\times 25000}{10} [/tex]

Multiply 9 and 25000 to get 225000.

[tex]\frac{225000}{10} [/tex]

Divide 225,000 by 10 to get 22,500.

[tex]x = 22500[/tex]

ANSWER 22,500

STEP BY STEP PROCEDURE

• First change 90% into a decimal which is 0.90.

• Then multiply 0.90 x 25,000 (Moshe's monthly salary) = 22,500
