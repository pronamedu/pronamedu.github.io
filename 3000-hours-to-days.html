7. Find the velocity of a plane that traveled 3000 miles west in 5 hours. ______


Answer:

600mph

Velocity is the distance travelled in a given time, or

v = x t

where

v is velocity,

x is distance and

t is time.

Here, x = 3000 and t = 5.0 , so

v = x t = 3000 × 5 =600

Don't forget units. Since the question's data is in miles and hours, the units will be miles per hour, or mph, so

3000 miles

5 hours = 600 miles hour or 600 mph
