ANSWER: 52 servings

Both terms must first be in the same unit. There are 16 ounces in a pound.

In 26 pounds there are:
26*16 ounces.

That's 416 ounces of dog food.

From 416 ounces, Celua's dog eats 8 ounces a day:
416 ÷ 8 = 52.

There are 52 servings of 8 ounces of dog food for Celua's food.
