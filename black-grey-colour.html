2. Mr ray has a total of 120rabbits in his pet shop. Some are black, some are grey, and some are white. There are three times as many grey rabbits as black rabbits. There are twice as many white rabbits as grey rabbits. How many white are there?


Black rabbits: x
Grey rabbits: 3x
White rabbits: 2(3x) or 6x
Total rabbits:  120

Equation:
(x) + (3x) + (6x) = 120
10x = 120
10x/10 = 120/10
x = 12

Substitute, x = 12:
Black rabbits: x = 12 rabbits
Grey rabbits: 3(12) = 36 rabbits
White rabbits: 2(36) = 72 rabbits

ANSWER: There are 72 rabbits.

Check:

12 + 36 + 72 = 120
120 = 120  (True)

17. A rooster with grey feathers is mated with a hen of the same phenotype.they produce 15 chicks with grey feathers, 6 chicks with black feathers and 8 chicks with white feathers what is the simplest explanation for this inheritance pattern


Incomplete or codominance. Feather color is controlled by 2 genes B = black and b = white. The third phenotype is the result of a 50-50 mix of black and white to produce gray.

The 15 gray, 6 black, and 8 white birds represent a 2:1:1 ratio&emdash;the result of mating two heterozygous individuals: (Bb x Bb)
