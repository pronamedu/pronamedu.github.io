what is asked is the angle between their paths

length b = 300 mi/hr x 3hr = 900 mi.

[tex]c^{2} = a^{2} + b^{2} - 2ab Cos(C)[/tex]

[tex]1000^{2} =750^{2} +900^{2} -2(750)(900)Cos(C)[/tex]
