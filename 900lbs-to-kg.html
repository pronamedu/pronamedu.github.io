13. The force is 500lb and 30000g of mass what the value of acceleration


Answer:

16.667m/s²

Explanation:

a = acceleration

f = force

m = mass

a = f/m

(Acceleration is equivalent to the force divided by mass)

a = 500N/30000g

First, breakdown 500N.

500N = 500kg/m/s²

Next, convert 30000 into kg so that it can be divided by 500kg/m/s².

1kg = 1000g

So,

30000g/1000g = 30kg

So, proceed with the formula.

a = 500kg/m/s² ÷ 30kg

500/30 = 16.667

kg/m/s² ÷ kg = m/s² (the kg unit is cancelled out)

Together, that would be 16.667m/s².

Therefore, the value of acceleration of an object with 30000g and has 500N is 16.667m/s².

If they ask you for the rounded up number, it is 17m/s².
