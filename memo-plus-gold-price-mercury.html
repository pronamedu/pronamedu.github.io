19. This process is wherein in the pulverized gold ore is washed over copper plates coated with mercury​


Answer:

Crushed ore is washed over mercury-coated copper sheets, and fine gold particles form an amalgam with the mercury. The amalgam was scraped off and the gold then separated from the amalgam by heating and evaporating the mercury, which was then recovered by a condenser for reapplication to the plates. Gold extraction refers to the processes required to extract gold from its ores. Once the ore is mined it can be treated as a whole ore using a dump leaching or heap leaching processes. This is typical of low-grade, oxide deposits. Normally, the ore is crushed and agglomerated prior to heap leaching. The two gold refining methods most commonly employed to derive pure gold are: the Miller process and the Wohlwill process. The Miller process uses gaseous chlorine to extract impurities when gold is at melting point; impurities separate into a layer on the surface of the molten purified gold.

27. what is cost plus pricing?


what is cost plus pricing?

Answer:

Cost plus pricing enable to compute all the amounts plus the percentage to get the final product price or the selling price. It is done by simply adding a markup to the cost of goods and services. To be more specific, in this method, you will add the three types of costs the direct material, direct labor, and the overhead costs for a product. After which, you add the mark up percentage in order to derive the final price or selling price of the product. In addition, Cost plus pricing can also be applicable to a customer contract, wherein the customer refund the seller for all the cost incurred and give money as payment for the agreed profit as additional to the cost incurred during the transaction.
