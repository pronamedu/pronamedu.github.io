Answer:

B. 88

Median is the middle number when you place the numbers in ascending order

87, 90, 86, 95, 92, 87, 88, 94, 86, 87?

The middle number is between 86 and 90. So your middle number can be found by:

[tex] \frac{86 \: - \: 87}{2} = 88[/tex]
