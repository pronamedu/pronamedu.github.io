6. The temperature of coffee decreased from 64° Celcius to 59° Celcius a few minutes after it was poured in a cup. By how many degrees Fahreinheit did the coffe cool?


FORMULA : Celcius - Farenheit
[tex] \frac{(celsius \times 9)}{5} - 32[/tex]
convert 64 and 59 into farenheit in order to subtract it.

1. 64
[tex] \frac{(64 \times 9)}{5} - 32[/tex]
PEMDAS (parenthesis, exponent, multiply, divide, add, subtract)

64 × 9 = 576
576 ÷ 5 = 115.2
115.2 - 32 = 83.2F°

2. 59
[tex] \frac{(59 \times 9)}{5} - 32[/tex]
59 × 9 = 531
531 ÷ 5 = 106.2
106.2 - 32 = 74.2F°

to find how much the coffee cooled, we have the subtract both of the numbers that we have just converted.

83.2 - 74.2 = 9F°

The coffee cooled by 9F°.
