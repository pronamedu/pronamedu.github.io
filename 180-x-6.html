11. supplement of (180 - x) °​


Answer:

Therefore, the supplement of (180-x)° is simply x°.

Step-by-step explanation:

The sum of the measures of supplementary angles is 180°.

Given: m∠1 = (180-x)°

Let the measure of ∠2 or the supplement be y.

To find the supplement y of ∠1 given its measure in expression (180-x)°, add the angles:

(180-x)° + y = 180°

y = 180° - (180-x)°

y = 180° - 180° + x°

y = x°

Therefore, the supplement of (180-x)° is simply x°.
