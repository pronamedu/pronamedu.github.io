3. _____ allows people to make voice calls through the Internet. a. VoIP b. Online Chat c. Videoconferencing d. Wiki


Letter A

VoIP or Voice Over IP is the one that allows people to make voice calls through the internet. It is the transmission of voice and other contents like multimedia (videos and pictures) over IP or internet protocol networks. Some VoIP allows people to call others using the same technology (VoIP) while others allow people to call someone who is using a telephone number, whether it is mobile, long distance or even long distance number.

VoIP serves as a converter in the network. In order for the information to be transmitted, VoIP changes the data into digital data. The use of another device is needed to bring back the information to its standard form (e.i., voice which is an analog data).

11. francisco santiago compositions


Answer:

Francisco Santiago

Filipino musician

Explanation:

Francisco Santiago (January 29, 1889 – September 28, 1947) was a Filipino musician, sometimes called The Father of Kundiman Art Song.[1]

Santiago was born in Santa Maria, Bulacan, Philippines, to musically-minded peasant parents, Felipe Santiago and Maria Santiago. In 1908, his first composition, Purita, was dedicated to the first Carnival Queen, Pura Villanueva, who later married the distinguished scholar Teodoro Kalaw.

Santiago's masterpiece was the "Concerto in B flat minor" for pianoforte and orchestra and his most famous piece "Kundiman, (Anak-Dalita)".

His other compositions are the kundiman "Sakali Man", "Hibik ng Filipinas", "Pakiusap", "Ang Pag-ibig", "Suyuan", "Alaala Kita", "Ikaw at Ako", "Ano Kaya ang Kapalaran?", "Hatol Hari Kaya?", "Sakali't Mamatay", "Dalit ng Pag-ibig", "Aking Bituin", "Madaling Araw" and "Pagsikat ng Araw". He was named UP Emeritus Professor of Piano, on May 25, 1946. When the University of the Philippines Conservatory of Music was celebrating its 30th anniversary, the patriotic musician died of a heart attack. He was buried at the North Cemetery, Manila. A hall in the Head Office of BDO (formerly the PCIBank Twin Towers, head office of PCIB) was named in his honor as the Francisco Santiago Hall. It was mainly used for kundiman contests of the Makati City Government and the awarding of Service Awards of the former Equitable PCI Bank and PCIBank.(hope it helps:))

A wiki is a type of website that is collaboratively generated from multiple users. It is a knowledge base website that allows it users to do collaborative editing and updating of its content and structure, per topic. There is no single author for a Wiki page or website.

Explanation:

The term "wiki" came from a local Hawaiian phrase, "wiki wiki," which means "super fast."  

How does Wiki works

A wiki is usually run using a wiki software, otherwise known as the wiki engine. A wiki engine is a type of content management system in that the content is produced without any defined owner or leader, and wikis have little intrinsic structure, allowing structure to emerge according to the needs of the users.

One best example of a large wiki is the Wikipedia, a free encyclopedia in many languages that anyone can edit. Wikipedia is not a single wiki, as what people might assume, but rather a collection of thousands of wikis, with each one pertaining to a specific language. Wikipedia provides one wiki page for each topic.
