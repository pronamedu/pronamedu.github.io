28. the time in manila is 6 hours ahead of cairo egypt. the flight from manila to cairo egypt takes 14 hours and 25 minutes. if an airplane leaves manila by 9:00 at what time in cairo egypt will the plane arrive?​


Answer:

To solve this problem, we need to add the time difference between Manila and Cairo to the flight duration and then calculate the arrival time in Cairo.

Step 1: Convert the flight duration to minutes.

14 hours and 25 minutes = (14 x 60) + 25 = 865 minutes

Step 2: Add the time difference between Manila and Cairo.

Manila is 6 hours ahead of Cairo, so we need to subtract 6 hours from the departure time in Manila.

9:00 Manila time - 6 hours = 3:00 Cairo time

Step 3: Add the flight duration to the adjusted departure time in Cairo.

3:00 Cairo time + 865 minutes = 16:25 Cairo time

Therefore, the plane will arrive in Cairo at 16:25 (4:25 PM) local time
