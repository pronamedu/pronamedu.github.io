The square root of a number is a value that, when multiplied by itself, equals the original number. So the square root of 1 is 1, and the square root of 2 is approximately 1.414. However, there are an infinite number of other numbers that fall between 1 and 2 whose square roots will also fall between 1 and 2. For example, the square root of 1.5 is approximately 1.224, which is between 1 and 2.
