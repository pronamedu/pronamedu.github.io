11. A lamp transfers 24 J of energy and draws a current of 2 A for 1 sec.What voltage is it operating at?​


Answer:

[tex]V = 12 \: V[/tex]

Explanation:

Given:

[tex]W = 24 \: J[/tex]

[tex]I = 2 \: A[/tex]

[tex]t = 1 \: s [/tex]

Required:

[tex]V[/tex]

Solution:

Step 1: Calculate the power

[tex]P = \frac{W}{t}[/tex]

[tex]P = \frac{24 \: J}{1 \: s}[/tex]

[tex]P = 24 \: W[/tex]

Step 2: Calculate the voltage

[tex]V = \frac{P}{I}[/tex]

[tex]V = \frac{24 \: W}{2 \: A}[/tex]

[tex]\blue{V = 12 \: V}[/tex]
