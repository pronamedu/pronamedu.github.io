13. Nilo accompanied Alice to the market. They bought 2 2/3 kilograms of chicken, 1 4/5 kilograms of beef, and 3 1/4 kilograms of pork. How many kilograms of meat did they buy in all?​


Answer:

7 43/60 kilograms of meat

Step-by-step explanation:

Given:

–2 2/3 kg of chicken

–1 4/5 kg of beef

–3 1/4 kg of pork

Find:

–Total kilograms of meat they buy

Solution:

First, convert the mixed fractions to improper fraction. To convert a mixed number to an improper fraction, follow these steps: Multiply the denominator of the fractional part by the whole number, and add the result to the numerator. Use this result as your numerator, and place it over the denominator you already have. So,

2 2/3➡️ 3×2= 6+2➡️ 8/3

1 4/5➡️ 5×1= 5+4➡️ 9/5

3 1/4➡️ 4×3= 12+1➡️ 13/4

Then, we need to find the Least Common Denominator of the 3 improper fraction which is 3, 4, & 5.

To find the least common denominator, list out the multiples of both denominators until you find the smallest multiple that is shared by both.

So 4×3×5= 60

Then let the denominator be 60

8/3= (60÷3)8= 160/60

9/5= (60÷5)9= 108/60

13/4= (60÷4)13= 195/60

So we can now add the fractions.

(160+108+195)/60

=463/60

Then Convert it to mixed fractions.

To convert an improper fraction to a mixed fraction, divide the numerator by the denominator, write down the quotient as the whole number and the remainder as the numerator on top of the same denominator.

463/60= 7 remainder 43

so the answer is 7 43/60 kg
