4. 32. A car travels 90 miles at a speed of 30 miles per hour and then continues at a speed of 50 miles per hour for the next 150 miles. What is the average speed of the car for the entire journey? A. 90 miles/hour B. 50 miles/hour C. 40 miles/hour D. 30 miles/hour​


Answer: Therefore, the average speed of the car for the entire journey is 40 miles/hour, which is letter C.

To find the average speed of the car for the entire journey, we need to calculate the total distance traveled and the total time taken, and then divide the total distance by the total time.

Total distance = 90 miles + 150 miles = 240 miles

Time taken to travel the first 90 miles at 30 miles per hour = distance ÷ speed = 90 miles ÷ 30 miles/hour = 3 hours

Time taken to travel the next 150 miles at 50 miles per hour = distance ÷ speed = 150 miles ÷ 50 miles/hour = 3 hours

Total time taken = 3 hours + 3 hours = 6 hours

Average speed = total distance ÷ total time = 240 miles ÷ 6 hours = 40 miles/hour
