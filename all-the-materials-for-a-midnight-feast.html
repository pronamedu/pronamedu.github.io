1. what is the feast of the black nazarene all about


The feast of the Black Nazarene is a Catholic-sponsored event in the Philippines observed every January 08, Good Friday of the Holy Week, and eve of Dec 31 each year. Devout followers believe that a mere touch of the statue can lead to miraculous healing and better, stronger relationship to God and His Son Jesus Christ. This Catholic activity has been around since 1787. The statue of the Black Nazarene is housed at the Quiapo Church in Manila. Due to influx of fanatics, the event has often been considered a special local holiday in Manila.

As part of this day of obligation, believers are required to attend church and try not to do any servile work.
