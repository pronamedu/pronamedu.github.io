28. ACTIVITY : Find the missing exterior angle x° 80° at 70° exterior 60° angle​


Answer:

pa brainliest po, thank you

Step-by-step explanation:

To find the missing exterior angle, we can use the fact that the sum of all exterior angles of a polygon is always 360 degrees.

In this case, we know two of the exterior angles: 80 degrees and 70 degrees.

So, we can start by adding those two angles:

80 + 70 = 150

Then, we can subtract that sum from 360 to find the missing exterior angle:

360 - 150 = 210

Therefore, the missing exterior angle is 210 degrees.
