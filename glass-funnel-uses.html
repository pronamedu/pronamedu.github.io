16. What is a funnel and how is it used in a laboratory?


A funnel is something that is made from metal or plastic, which is wide at the top and, as it gets lower, gets narrower at the bottom. In scientific experiments or in laboratories, a funnel is often used to guide a liquid or powder into a small opening. Moreover, it is oftentimes conical in shape.
