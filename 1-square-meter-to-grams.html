3. 1. If the mass of the object touching floor is 4000 grams and the surface area is 55 square meter, determine the pressure that the object exert on the floor.


Answer:

The formula is given by

F = PA

where F = force, P = pressure, A = area

The force, in this problem, refers to the weight of the object. That is F = mg where m = mass, g = gravity (9.8 m/s²)

mg = PA

P = mg / A

Given m = 4 kg, A = 55 m², g = 9.8 m/s²

P = (4 kg)(9.8 m/s²) / (55 m²)

P = 0.713 Pa

The pressure of the object that exerts on the floor is 0.713 Pa.

Answer:

Here are the ratios for the given quantities:

1. 4 weeks to 8 days ratio:

- To simplify the ratio, divide both quantities by 4:

- 4 weeks ÷ 4 = 1 week

- 8 days ÷ 4 = 2 days

- Therefore, the ratio is 1 week to 2 days.

2. 500 square meters to 1 hectare ratio:

- Since 1 hectare is equal to 10,000 square meters, we can simplify the ratio by dividing both quantities by 500:

- 500 square meters ÷ 500 = 1

- 1 hectare ÷ 500 = 0.01

- Therefore, the ratio is 1 to 0.01 or 100 to 1.

3. 575 grams to 0.75 kilograms ratio:

- To simplify the ratio, convert 0.75 kilograms to grams by multiplying by 1000:

- 0.75 kilograms x 1000 = 750 grams

- Then, divide both quantities by 25 to get the simplified ratio:

- 575 grams ÷ 25 = 23 grams

- 750 grams ÷ 25 = 30 grams

- Therefore, the ratio is 23 to 30.

4. An angle whose measure is 30° more than its complement:

- Recall that complementary angles add up to 90°. Let x be the measure of the angle in degrees, then its complement is 90 - x degrees.

- According to the problem, x is 30 degrees more than its complement, so we can write the equation: x = (90 - x) + 30.

- Solving for x, we get: x = 60°.

- Therefore, the measure of the angle is 60° and the measure of its complement is 30°.

5. 9 feet to 1 yard ratio:

- Since 1 yard is equal to 3 feet, we can simplify the ratio by dividing both quantities by 3:

- 9 feet ÷ 3 = 3 feet

- 1 yard ÷ 3 = 1/3 yard

- Therefore, the ratio is 3 feet to 1/3 yard.

6. 5 days to 1 week ratio:

- Since there are 7 days in a week, we can simplify the ratio by dividing both quantities by 5:

- 5 days ÷ 5 = 1 day

- 1 week ÷ 5 = 0.2 week

- Therefore, the ratio is 1 day to 0.2 week.
