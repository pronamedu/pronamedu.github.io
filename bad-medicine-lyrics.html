14. a nurse is giving medicine to two patients patient A has to take medicine every 4 hours abd patient B has to take medicine every 6 hours if she starts giving medicine to both patient at 8:00 at what time will she next give medicine to both at the same time​


Answer: 8:00 PM

Explanation:

Patient A takes medicine every 4 hours, so the next time they need medicine is at 12:00, 4:00, 8:00 PM, and so on.

Patient B takes medicine every 6 hours, so the next time they need medicine is at 2:00 PM, 8:00 PM, 2:00 AM, and so on.

To find the next time the nurse needs to give medicine to both patients at the same time, we need to find the smallest time that satisfies both schedules.

The first time both patients will need medicine at the same time is at 8:00 PM, which is the LCM (Least Common Multiple) of 4 and 6.

Therefore, the nurse will next give medicine to both patients at the same time at 8:00 PM.
