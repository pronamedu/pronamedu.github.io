28. Maja Blanca 2 cups cornstarch 2½ cups sugar 7 cups coconut milk 1 cup evaporated milk 1 teaspoon vanilla Total expenses Price F16.00 40.50 40.00 20.00 F1.00


Answer:

Total expenses for the Maja Blanca recipe are:

2 cups of cornstarch at F16.00 = F32.00

2½ cups of sugar at F40.50 = F101.25

7 cups of coconut milk at F40.00 = F280.00

1 cup of evaporated milk at F20.00 = F20.00

1 teaspoon of vanilla at F1.00 = F1.00

Total expenses: F32.00 + F101.25 + F280.00 + F20.00 + F1.00 = F434.25

This means it would cost F434.25 to make the Maja Blanca recipe.

Please note that the prices might vary depending on the location.
