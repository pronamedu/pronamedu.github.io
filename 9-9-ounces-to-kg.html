7. A hamster drinks 9/10 ounce of water a day. How many days will 3 1/5 ounces of water last?​


Answer:

Approximately 3 days

Step-by-step explanation:

If a hamster drinks 9/10 ounces a day, and theres a 3 1/5 ounces, it will take 3 days (not exactly)

To take the answer, we need to divide 3 1/5 to 9/10

First, we need to convert 3 1/5 to an improper fraction.. 3 1/5 = 16/5

Second, we need to divide them

16/5 ÷ 9/10 = 16/5 × 10/9

(when dividing a fraction, we need to reciprocal the divisor and invert the division to multiplication)

16×10/5×9 = 160/45

Then, we need to find its lowest term

160 and 45 is both divisible to 5 so

160÷5 and 45÷5 will make 32/9

Lastly, since it is an improper fraction we need to convert it to a proper fraction

32/9 = 3 5/9

The answer is 3 5/9 days(approximately 3 days)
