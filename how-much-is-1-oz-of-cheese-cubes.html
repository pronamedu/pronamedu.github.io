2. James works in a cheese factory. He is assigned in the cubic cheeses section where he is packing little cubes of cheese that measure 1 cm on each side. He is to put them in a bigger cube that measure 5 cm on each side. How many small cubes will fit in the bigger cubes?


Answer:

James can fit 125 small cubes in a bigger cube.

Step-by-step explanation:

Volume formula:

V = a³, where a is the measurement of one side of the cube.

Volume for small cube:

V = a³

V = 1³

V = 1 cm³

Volume for bigger cube:

V = a³

V = 5³

V = 125 cm³

Answer:

244/35 or 6 25/35 or 6 5/7

Step-by-step explanation:

Mixed fraction to improper fraction

4 2/5 = ((4*5)+2)/5 = 22/5

2 4/7 = ((2*7)+4)/7 = 18/7

least common denominator of 7 and 5 is 35

(22*7)/35 + (18*5)/35 = 244/35 or 6 25/35 or 6 5/7
