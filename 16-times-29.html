Answer:

1. 4:45 pm in 12-hour time is 4:45 pm (there's no need to convert)

2. 00:59 H in 12-hour time is 12:59 am. To convert, we subtract 12 from the hour since anything after 12:00 am is denoted as "pm" in 12-hour time. So 00:59 H becomes 12:59 am.

3. 10:16 am in 12-hour time is 10:16 am (there's no need to convert)

4. 22:19 H in 12-hour time is 10:19 pm. We subtract 12 from the hour to convert it to 12-hour time, so 22:19 H becomes 10:19 pm.

5. 11:29 pm in 24-hour time is 23:29 H. To convert, we add 12 to the hour since anything after 12:00 pm is denoted as "pm" in 12-hour time. So 11:29 pm becomes 23 + 12 = 35, which we then subtract 24 from to get 11:29 pm in 12-hour time.
