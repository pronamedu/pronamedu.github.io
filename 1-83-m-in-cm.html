21. Sector of a Cicle 1. Find the area of the sector of radius 7.5 cm and its central angle is 83°​


✏️SECTOR===============================

Problem: Find the area of the sector whose the radius is 7.5 cm and its central angle is 83°.

Solution: Find the area of the product of the circle's area and the ratio of the central angle at 360 degrees.

[tex] \begin{aligned} & \bold{ \color{lightblue}Formula:} \\ & \boxed{A_{sec} = \frac{ \theta}{360 \degree} \cdot\pi {r}^{2} } \end{aligned}[/tex]

[tex] \begin{aligned}{A_{sec} = \frac{83 \degree}{360 \degree} \cdot\pi(7.5cm)^{2} } \end{aligned}[/tex]

[tex] \begin{aligned}{A_{sec} = \frac{83}{360} \cdot\pi(56.25 {cm}^{2} ) } \end{aligned}[/tex]

[tex] \begin{aligned}{A_{sec} = \frac{\pi(4668.75 {cm}^{2} )}{360} } \end{aligned}[/tex]

[tex]A_{sec} = \pi(12.97 {cm}^{2} )[/tex]

- Let 3.14 be the approximate value of pi.

[tex]A_{sec} ≈ (3.14)(12.97 {cm}^{2} )[/tex]

[tex]A_{sec} ≈ 40.73{cm}^{2}[/tex]

- Therefore, the area of the sector is approximate:

[tex] \large \boxed{ \rm \green{ \: 40.73 \: sq. \: cm. \: }}[/tex]===============================
