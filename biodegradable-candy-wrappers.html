Which is NOT included as Biodegradable Waste?

Answer: [tex]\Large\bold\red{\tt{D.Candy\:Wrapper}}[/tex]

Explanation:

A biodegradable waste is a waste that can be decomposed by bacteria and other organisms.

Fruits, leaves and grasslyard cuttings are biodegradable wastes because of their organic properties that can allow microbes to decompose them. However a candy wrapper does not have these natural proprties. Hence, letter D is the answer.
