Answer:

Explanation:

A cartridge printer draws ink from a disposable cartridge. Cartridges can cost much, but at least they are clean and easy to change and time can be saved.

A bottomless printer draws ink from the tank you use to refill. Ink might be cheaper than cartridges, But the ink is harder to find than cartridges and refiling can be a waste of time.
