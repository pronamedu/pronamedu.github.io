Answer:

11.A steel tape is made of steel or stainless steel. It consists of a steel strip of 6mm to 16mm wide. It is available in lengths of 1m, 5m, 8m, 10m, 20m, 30m and 50m. Meters, decimeters, and centimeters are graduated in the steel strip.

12.a pointed instrument used for making marks on wood, bricks, etc., to guide a saw or in sign painting.

13.room divider a screen or piece of furniture placed so as to divide a room into separate areas. a person or thing that divides.

14.The definition of a caliper is a tool with two curved, movable legs used to measure thickness and distances. An example of a caliper is a device for measuring the distance between two points on a map. The assembly housing the brake pads in a disc brake.

15.The standard two-foot framing square, also referred to as a "carpenter's square" and historically as a "steel square" is an L-shaped tool that is used to mark angles for cuts used in building framing, particularly roof rafters, stair stringers, and many other cuts or angles other than 90°.
