Answer:

The proportional relationship between the content of the silica chains and the viscosity is directly proportional, the viscosity increases when the silica chains are also high. High viscosity lava flows very slowly and covers relatively small areas, otherwise, high viscosity lava can cover large areas of thousands of square kilometers. When the magma is of low viscosity the exhaust gases can escape easily, which is not the case in high viscosity magma in which there are violent eruptions. So the correct answer is C. Amount of Gas.
