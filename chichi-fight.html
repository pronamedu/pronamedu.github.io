Question 1: What is the Point of View used in the story?

Answer: First Person, Because the pronouns used were I.

First Person: This refers to the writer of the story who is one of the characters of the story. (Therefore, when first person, the pronouns I or We will be omitted in the story because the narrator is the person who wrote the story)

First Person: Ito ay tumutukoyng nagsulat ng kwento na isa sa mga karakter ng kwento. (Kaya naman kapag first person, mapapasin ang pronouns na I o We sa kwento dahilang nagkukuwento ay ang mismong sumulat ng kwento)
