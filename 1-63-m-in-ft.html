22. Select the expression below that is equal to 7(9 - m). A. 7m - 63 B. 63 - 7m C. 63 - m D. 16 - m

7(9 - m) can be simplified using the distributive property of multiplication over addition/subtraction, which states that a(b - c) = ab - ac.

Applying this property, we get:

7(9 - m) = 79 - 7m

Simplifying further:

7(9 - m) = 63 - 7m
