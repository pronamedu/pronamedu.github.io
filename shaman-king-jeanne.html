3. define shamanism and roles of shamans? 5 sentences po sana​


Answer:

Shamanism is a religious practice that involes a practitioner who is believed to interact with a spirit world through altered states of consciousness, such as trance. The goal of this is usually to direct these spirits or spiritual energies into the physical world, for healing or another purpose.

There are many variations of shamanism throughout the world, but several common beliefs are shared by all forms of shamanism.

Spirits exist and they play important roles both in individial lives and in human society.The shaman can communicate with the spirit world.Spirits can be benevolent or malevolent.The shaman can treat sickness caused by malevolent spirits.The shamans can employ trances inducing techniques to incite visionary ecstacy and go on vision quests.The shaman evokes animal images as spirit guides, omens, and message-bearers.The shaman can perform other varied forms of divination, scry, throw bones or runes, and sometimes foretell of future events.

Shamans function as healers, prophets, diviners and custodians of religious mythology.

In some societies, all these functions are performed by the same person; in others, shamans are specialists.

13. what is the difference between shamans and soccerer?​


Answer:

Sorcerer- someone na nagma-manipulates ng energy through content, concentration, at focus.

Shamans- gumagamit ng white magic.

Answer:

A sorcerer is someone who manipulates energy through intent, concentration and focus. Often referred to as using magic. The person may do this strictly by using their mental ability or to assist in their working they may use rituals and/or physical objects. Energy in and of itself is neutral, not black/negative/harmful or white/positive/benevolent. It all comes down to how well the person can focus, concentrate and their intent and competence which direction it will be used and what results will occur, if any. When I work with someone, I teach them to do so without any tools or rituals because if needed or for spontaneous use, they may not have any items with them or a ritual may not be appropriate.

A shaman is a person who has the ability to shift their subconscious awareness to go to or journey to the spiritual realm and interact with entities that exist or reside in that realm. Although the common terms consider that the person goes to the spiritual realm, they do not leave their body as such. They expand their subconscious awareness and through the subconscious pass information to the conscious. Often a shaman is considered to be a healer but that is not necessary, just one of the things they may do with the information they gain in the spiritual realm. Pure shamanism is done through the shaman’s own control and may use drums, music, movement or a variety of other methods to attain the trance state in which they expand the subconscious to the spiritual realm and return by choice.
