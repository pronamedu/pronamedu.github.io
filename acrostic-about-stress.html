1. acrostic for understanding stress​


Stress is the body's biological response to a perceived threat.

The stress response sends chemicals and hormones surging through your body.

Resting can help stop stress.

Everyone can get affected by stress;

Stress has a powerful impact on various aspects of your life.

Simply live a life that pleases you.
