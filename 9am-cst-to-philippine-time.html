7. my watch is set correctly at 9am. if it loses 2 minutes every hour what will be the time on my watch if the correct time is 4pm the next day


9am to 4pm the next day is 34 hours.
You lose 2 minutes per hour.
2x34 is 68. So you lose 68 minutes in 34 hours.
34 hours have 2,040 minutes.
So 2,040-68 is 1,972. Converting 1,972 minutes to hours is equal to 32.87 hours or 32hrs and 52 minutes.
Therefore the answers is 2:08 pm
