Answer:

Nancy is 18 years old right now.

Step-by-step explanation:

Let x be the age of nancy.

Eight years from now will be: x + 8

Nancy will be twice her age 5 years ago: 2(x - 5)

Equate:

x + 8 = 2(x - 5)

x + 8 = 2x - 10

Transpose:

x - 2x = -10 - 8

-x = -18

Divide both sides by -1,

Final Answer: x = 18
