5. If a car travels for 4 hours to cover distance of 64 Kilometers what is the car's speed? ​


Answer:

﻿16

Explanation:

To calculate the speed of the car, we can use the formula:

Speed = Distance / Time

where Distance is the distance covered by the car, and Time is the time taken to cover that distance.

In this case, we know that the distance covered by the car is 64 kilometers, and the time taken to cover that distance is 4 hours. So, we can plug these values into the formula:

Speed = 64 kilometers / 4 hours

Simplifying the expression on the right-hand side, we get:

Speed = 16 kilometers per hour

Therefore, the car's speed is 16 kilometers per hour.

23. 2. Vic walked 1/3kilometer to school. After classes, she walk 5/8 kilometer to Manny's house and 34 kilometer to her house. How many kilometers did Vic walk?​


Answer:

To find the total distance that Vic walked, we need to add up the distances of each of the three legs of her journey:

Distance to school = 1/3 km

Distance to Manny's house = 5/8 km

Distance from Manny's house to her own house = 34 km

Total distance = 1/3 + 5/8 + 34

We need to find a common denominator for 3, 8, and 24 (the product of 3 and 8) in order to add these fractions:

1/3 = 8/24

5/8 = 15/24

So now we have:

Total distance = 8/24 + 15/24 + 34

Adding the fractions, we get:

Total distance = 23/24 + 34

Finally, we add the two terms:

Total distance = 57 23/24 km

Therefore, Vic walked a total of 57 23/24 kilometers.
