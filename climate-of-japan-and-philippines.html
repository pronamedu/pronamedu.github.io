15. Using the 5 climate types, identify what climate is present in the following countries and tell why these countries experience that climate 1. Philippines 2. Japan 3. Dubai 4. America 5. Canada​


Answer:

Philippines: The Philippines has a tropical rainforest climate (Af) characterized by high temperatures and abundant rainfall throughout the year. This climate type is common in countries located near the equator, where the sun is directly overhead and there is no distinct summer or winter season. The high temperatures and humidity in the Philippines are due to its location within the tropical zone and its proximity to the Pacific Ocean, which contributes to the formation of typhoons and monsoons.

Japan: Japan has a humid subtropical climate (Cfa) characterized by hot, humid summers and mild winters with occasional snowfall. This climate type is found in coastal areas at mid-latitudes, where the influence of both tropical and polar air masses is felt. The warm, moist air from the Pacific Ocean creates the humid conditions in Japan, while the cold air from Siberia causes occasional winter storms and snowfall.

Dubai: Dubai has a hot desert climate (BWh) characterized by high temperatures and low rainfall. This climate type is common in arid regions with little vegetation, such as the Middle East and North Africa. The hot, dry conditions in Dubai are due to its location within the Arabian Desert and the surrounding arid landscape.
