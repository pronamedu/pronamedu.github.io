14. the length of a rectangle is 4 cm longer than its width what are the possible integral with if the area of the rectangle is lesser than 437 square centimeters​


Step-by-step explanation:

Length = W + 4

Area = LW < 437

Since L = W + 4

W(W + 4) < 437

w² + 4w < 437

w² + 4w - 437 = 0

w = -23 or 19

Since any measurement cannot be negative, width must be equal to 19 cm and length must be equal to 19 + 4 = 23 cm.
