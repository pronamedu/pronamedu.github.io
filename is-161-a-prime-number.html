25. Sixty percent (70%) of what number is 161


Answer:

If sixty percent (60%)

Given:   [tex]R=60\%[/tex],   [tex]P=161[/tex]

Find:   [tex]B=?[/tex]

Formula:   [tex]B=\frac{P}{R}[/tex]

Solution:

[tex]B=\frac{P}{R}\\B=\frac{161}{60\%}\\B=\frac{161}{0.6}\\\boxed{\boldsymbol{B=268}}[/tex]

If seventy percent (70%)

Given:   [tex]R=70\%[/tex],   [tex]P=161[/tex]

Find:   [tex]B=?[/tex]

Formula:   [tex]B=\frac{P}{R}[/tex]

Solution:

[tex]B=\frac{P}{R}\\B=\frac{161}{70\%}\\B=\frac{161}{0.7}\\\boxed{\boldsymbol{B=230}}[/tex]
