3. In what ways are entrepreneur useful to suppliers


IN WHAT WAYS ARE ENTREPRENEURS USEFUL TO SUPPLIERS?

Suppliers are the ones that supply products or services sold or offered by enterprises. That is, entrepreneurs are useful to suppliers in a way that:

entrepreneurs are the main customers of suppliers

entrepreneurs consume the products and services produced by suppliers

In other words, without entrepreneurs, products, and services produced or offered by suppliers will be wasted. At the most basic level, suppliers need an enterprise to supply whatever product they are supplying or services they are offering.

7. What are the Responsibilities of an entrepreneur to the suppliers


The responsibilities of an entrepreneur to his/her suppliers are,  to pay monthly dues, follow what have been stated in the contract, none disclosure of the goods formula or ingredients supplied. proper handling of goods supplied and adheres standards provided by the suppliers. Suppliers are the one who supply finished goods, raw materials and services to a particular businessman or what we call entrepreneur. Both parties are doing business and should trust each other. Both parties are required to act in good-faith.
