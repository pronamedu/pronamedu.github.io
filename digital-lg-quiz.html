1. Quiz 6-Permutations 1. How many four-digit numbers can be formed from the digits 2,3,4,5,6, and 7 without repetition?​


Answer:

=360 po

no repetition...sana po makatulong

In solving permutation without repetition, our formula is given as:

nPr = n! / (n - r)!

where n is the number of things

and r is how many times they are taken

If n = 6 and r = 4, then:

nPr = n! / (n - r)!

nPr = 6! / (6 - 4)!

nPr = 6! / 2!

nPr = 6×5×4×3×2×1 / 2×1

nPr = 6×5×4×3

nPr = 360

Therefore, there are 360 four-digit numbers that can be formed from the digits 2 to 7 without repetition.
