11. What is the Japanese of "excuse me" and "thank you"?


Answer:

Excuse me in Japanese is "Sumimasen"

and Thank you is "Arigato gozaimas" or Arigato gozaimashita with sincerity.

Japanese of thank you is "arigato" or "arigatōgozaimasu"

Example :

1.Thank you for coming.

Kitekurete arigatō.

2. Thank you for understanding.

Gorikai arigatōgozaimasu

Japanese of excuse me is " sumimasen"

Example :

Excuse me, is this the math class

Sumimasen, koreha sūgaku no kurasudesu.

Excuse me sir, are you busy?

Sumimasen, isogashīdesu ka?

13. what is i love you in japanese?​


Answer:

Watashi wa, anata o aishiteimasu

In Japan, there are 3 ways to say "I LOVE YOU"

1. 'DAISUKI' - For your friends and the person you like.

2. 'AISHITERU' - For a more serious relationship.

3. 'KOISHITERU' - For the person you want to spend the rest of your life with.

They made this rule to preserve the real meaning af 'I LOVE YOU'
