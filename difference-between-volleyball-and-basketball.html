Ratio of volleyballs to basketballs: 10 : 12

Simple form: 5 : 6

How did I get the simple form?

First find their GCF. Use listing method.

10 - 1, 2, 5, 10

12 - 1, 2, 3, 4, 6, 12

Their GCF is 2

Let's use 2 to divide 10 and 12.

[tex] \frac{10}{12} \div \red{ \frac{2}{2}} = \tt \green{ \frac{5}{6} }[/tex]

Thus, the simple form is 5 : 6

volleyball, game played by two teams, usually of six players on a side, in which the players use their hands to bat a ball back and forth over a high net, trying to make the ball touch the court within the opponents’ playing area before it can be returned. Volleyball-specific shoes have a gum rubber sole. This type of sole provides a lot more traction than a regular sport shoe or cross-trainer.
