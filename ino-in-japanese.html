4. Inoichi is three times as old as Ino, and Inojin is 3 years younger than Ino. If the sum of their ages three years ago was 63 years, find the present age of Inoichi.


Answer:

45 years old.

Step-by-step explanation:

Let's start by using algebra to represent the relationships between their ages:

Inoichi's age: I = 3×Ino

Inojin's age: J = Ino - 3

We also know that the sum of their ages three years ago was 63, which means that:

(I-3) + (Ino-3) + (J-3) = 63

Substituting the expressions we found for I and J:

(3Ino-3) + (Ino-3) + (Ino-6) = 63

Simplifying and solving for Ino's age:

5Ino - 12 = 63

5Ino = 75

Ino = 15

Now that we know Ino's age, we can find Inoichi's age:

I = 3Ino = 3(15) = 45

Therefore, Inoichi is currently 45 years old.

PROBLEM:

To visit his grandmother, Ino takes train 1.67 kilometers and a car 3.712 meters. How many meters does Ino's journey in total?

SOLUTION:

The term "total" tells us that we need to find the some of the given values.

In the problem, we have 1.67 kilometers and 3.712 meters as our given. They don't have the same unit, thus we need to convert first 1.67 kilometers to meters.

In order to convert kilometers into meters, we need to multiply the length value by 1000.

[tex] \sf 1.67 \times 1000 = 1670 \: meters[/tex]

Now, we need to add 1670 meters to 3.712 meters to get the total distance.

[tex] \sf 1670 + 3.712 = 1673.712 \: meters[/tex]

[tex] \boxed{ \begin{array}{}\sf 1670 \\ + \: \underline{\sf \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: 3.712} \\ \sf \: \: \: \: \: \: \: 1673.712{} \end{array}}[/tex]

ANSWER:

Ino's journey has a total distance of 1673.712 meters from riding a train to riding a car.
