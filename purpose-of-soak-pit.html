16. a gargabe pit is 5 m wide and 2m deep what is the volume of the pit


Answer:

50 meters cubed

Step-by-step explanation:

To find out how to find volume of any 3-dimensional figure, first find the length, width and height.

Since this "garbage pit" is 5x5x2m pit, multiply the length width and height.

5 x 5 = 25

25 x 2 = 50

The volume is measured out as: centimeters cubed, meters cubes, millimeters cubed, etc.


17. ano ang compost pit? anong paraang ng compost pit? anong ibig sabihin ng compost pit?
