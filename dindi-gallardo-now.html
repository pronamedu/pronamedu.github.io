14. Mr. Gallardo made $35,500 in the stock market while Mr. Gomez lost $24,800. What is the difference in their performance?


Mr. Gallardo's gain (positive): $35,500

Mr. Gomez's loss (negative): - $24,800

Find the difference in their performance (Subtract):

Rule in subtraction of integers: Change the sign of the subtrahend then proceed to addition rule.

35,500 - (-24,800)

= 35,500 + 24,800

= 60,300

ANSWER: There's a difference of $60,300 in their stocks' performance in the stock market.
