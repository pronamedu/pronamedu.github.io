OLD MUSIC is, by and large, the music that people grow up knowing because it is widely played in society. New music, by definition, is music that people have not heard before, and about which society has not yet made up its mind.

NEW MUSIC is either based largely on the music of the past, such as neo-Romantic contemporary classical music, which is mostly very bad, or it is original, in which case it is difficult for people to hear or get used to. Most new music, also, is very bad, because the old music tends to be the cream of the cream and it’s hard for any arbitrary new musician to compete with that.

But ultimately, new music is the only source of growth in musical culture. Without it, we would eventually die of boredom. Only new music can really let us hear who we are, who we want to be, who we will be. But this is often disturbing.

Because of this, authoritarian politicians on both the right wing and the left wing hate new music. They want people to stay the same and to do as they are told, but new music makes people want to change, and to do what they feel.

Sometimes, people say that new music is dissonant, but the fact of the matter is, except for a brief interlude of 12-tone music in the first half of the twentieth century, and some explorations in microtonality, the harmonic palette has not not usually exceeded bounds first explored by 16th century composers. Sometimes, as with Mozart or punk rock or modal jazz or Minimalism, new music has used only a restricted part of this harmonic palette. 11. Compare and Contrast Fliptop battle and Old Balagtasan


OLD BALAGTASAN VS. FLIPTOP BATTLE RAP

Old Balgtasan- Balagtsan is an old form of debate among filipinos, where the two poet exchanges poetic verses. According to researchers, balagtasan consists of three speakers: a narrator, a male, and a woman. The three interact with each other in a sort of a “debate.” Each of the speaker takes turns in sharing and reciting lines in poetic rhyme in which the next speaker will try to respond in a form of rebuttal. Topics are primarily political, including mostly with Filipino heroism in a formal manner. Sometimes the speaker also applies comedy in their poetic lines.

Fliptop Battle Rap- better known as Fliptop, is a Battle Rap league recently established by a group of flipino rappers that aims to upheave and revive the filipino hip-hop in the Philippines.

The Fliptop consists of multiple judges that ranges from five to rarely ten, and three speakers: an arbiter, and two male/female emcees also known as rappers, sometimes it is a battle between a female and a male emcee.

The three interacts with each other in a form of informal debate. The two rappers takes turns in reciting their written bars and verses, mostly about each other's life and their deeds as a rapper. Usually Fliptop lasts for about 3 rounds and is a battle of creativity, in a form of insulting, criticizing, dissing, bragging, joking, rapping and showing off. Mostly, rapper tends to joke about the appearance of their opponents, and blustering words about how and why they are worthy of winning the ongoing battle between them. The winners are usually the ones who pulled off an exceptional extravaganza, and creatively knocked out their opponents using, figures of speech, double meanings, wordplays, rap bars and hilarious jokes. In Fliptop you should convince the crowd and judges by effectively using your own words in order to win. At least in my own opinion as a guy who watched Fliptop Battles a thousand times now.

27. how do the old cars compare today?​


Answer and Explanation:

Design, parts, handling, and safety are all different between vintage and modern automobiles. Modern automobiles typically come equipped with a central computer, sensors, and sophisticated systems. Modern engines outperform earlier ones in intelligence, size, power, and efficiency.

Numerous lives have been saved by advancements in automotive safety over time. The 1960s saw the advent of seat belts. Since then, improved crumple zones, anti-lock braking systems, and airbags that help protect occupants during a collision have become commonplace. Modern automobiles also safeguard lives on the road with driver alerts and crash avoidance technologies. Additionally, newer cars often have better suspension, reduced noise, and adjustable driving positions for easier access to the controls.

Classic automobiles offer a more engaging driving experience because they are devoid of automatic systems, and they can be simpler to maintain oneself. Additionally, due to supply and demand, the value of antique cars tends to increase, especially for well-kept ones, but modern cars will lose value over time. Standard vehicle insurance is required for historic cars driven regularly, but those only sometimes driven may be eligible for classic car insurance, which often offers more coverage for less money.
