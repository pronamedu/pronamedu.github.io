2. Compare the Exodus and the crossing of the River Jordan. How relevant are these events to Christian life today?


Answer:

The Exodus and the crossing of the River Jordan prefigure Christian baptism where we leave the slavery and desert of oppressive sin, pass by the waters and reemerge with a deeper friendship with God. They also mirror God's grace in keeping us strong and making ways for us to overcome daily challenges and challenges which are too far beyond our human potential wherein we need divine assistance. They show the mercy and majesty of God who does wonders that will be remembered forever as a sign that He wills all to be saved.

0.25 = 1/4

Tom: ¹/₄ x  (1/4 of Jordan's age)

Ages in 2 years:
Jordan: x + 2
Tom: ¹/₄ x + 2

Equation: In 2 years, Tom's ages will be ¹/₃ of Jordan's age

¹/₄ x + 2 = ¹/₃ (x + 2)
¹/₄ x + 2 = ¹/₃ x + ²/₃

LCD: (3)(4) or 12

12 [¹/₄ x + 2 = ¹/₃ x + ²/₃] 12

3x + 24 = 4x + 8

4x - 3x = 24 - 8

x = 16
