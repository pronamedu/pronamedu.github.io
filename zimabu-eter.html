4. A rectangular plot has a length of 2 m eters longer than its width the area of the plot is 15 square meters, find the length of the plot GIVEN:WORKING EQUATION:SOLUTION AND ANSWER:​


Answer:

length = 5 meters

Step-by-step explanation:

Given:

length = 2 + w

width = w

Area = lw = 15 m²

Solution:

A = lw

15 = (2 + w)(w)

w² + 2w - 15 = 0

(w + 5)(w - 3) = 0

w = -5 and 3

we need to take the positive value only

therefore, w= 3

length = 2 + w = 2 + 3 = 5

therefore

length = 5 meters
