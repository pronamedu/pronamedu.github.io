11. she repented millions of times yet, did not receive forgiveness. what type of figure of speech is this​


Answer:

I believe that this is an example of hyperbole.

This refers to a figure of speech that makes use of exaggerated claims or statements that are not supposed to be taken in a literal manner. After all, there is no way that a single person could actually repent millions of times.

The usage of this phrase in the statement simply means that she has asked for forgiveness as much as possible. This goes to emphasize the number of times she has done this, even though it might not actually be millions of times as expressed. By doing this, the sentence has more of an impact and sounds more poetic in a way.
