9. 3. There are two variations of a snake in an area, a black snake and a white snake. There is a hawk that serves as the predator of that area. Some snakes are eaten especially those snakes that stand out. They move into a new area where the rocks are very dark, but still, there are snakes that have been eaten by the hawk until there is only one variation of snakes left. The black snake have a higher chance of living because of their adaptability to the environment. What Theory explains this?


Answer:

The theory that explains the scenario described is natural selection, which is a key concept in the theory of evolution. Natural selection is the process by which certain characteristics or traits that are advantageous to an organism's survival and reproduction become more common in a population over time, while traits that are disadvantageous become less common.

Explanation:

In this case, the black snake has a higher chance of survival because its dark coloration allows it to blend in with the dark rocks in the new area, making it less visible to the hawk predator. The white snake, on the other hand, stands out against the dark background, making it an easier target for the hawk. As a result, the white snake population has been reduced by the predator, while the black snake population has survived and adapted to the new environment.

This scenario illustrates how natural selection operates in response to changes in the environment and the presence of selective pressures such as predation. The black snake's adaptation to the new environment is an example of how natural selection can lead to the evolution of new traits and characteristics in a population over time.

28. a snake erawled 400cm 10second what is the total speed of the snake​


Answer:

To find the speed of the snake, we need to divide the distance it traveled by the time it took to travel that distance.

The distance traveled by the snake is 400 cm, and the time it took to travel that distance is 10 seconds.

Therefore, the speed of the snake is:

Speed = Distance / Time

Speed = 400 cm / 10 s

Speed = 40 cm/s

So the total speed of the snake is 40 cm/s.

30. it is an injury cauced by a bite of a snake,particularly a venomous snake


Answer:

A snakebite is an injury caused by the bite of a snake, especially a venomous snake. A common sign of a bite from a venomous snake is the presence of two puncture wounds from the animal's fangs. Sometimes venom injection from the bite may occur.
