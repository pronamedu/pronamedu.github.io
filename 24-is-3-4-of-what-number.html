2. 3 times a number added to the product of 4 and 3 is 24 what is the number


Answer:

3 times a number added to the product of 4 and 3 is 24

Let x be the number.

[tex]3x + (4 \times 3) = 24[/tex]

[tex]3x + 12 = 24 \\ \\ 3x = 24 - 12 \\ \\ 3x = 12 \\ \\ \frac{3x}{3} = \frac{12}{3} \\ \\ x = 4[/tex]

Check for verification.

[tex]3(4) + (4 \times 3) = 24 \\ \\ 12 + 12 = 24 \\ \\ 24 = 24[/tex]

☑️

Hence, the unknown number is 4.

6. 4. What is the next number?What is the 9th term?3, 6, 12, 24,​


Answer:

768

Step-by-step explanation:

The sequence 3, 6, 12, 24, is a geometric sequence. Therefore we need to get the common ratio first to get the 9th term.

Without solving makikita naman natin na if dinivide natin yung 6 by 3, 12 by 6, and 24 by 12, ay makukuha natin is 2, so 2 is the common ratio.

Then let's use the implicit formula:

[tex]a_n = a_1(r)^{n-1}[/tex]

Subtitute:

[tex]a_9 = 3(2)^{9-1} \\a_9 = 3(2)^8\\a_9 = 3(256)\\a_9 = 768[/tex]

Therefore the 9th term is 768.
