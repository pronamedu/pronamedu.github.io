ANSWER

------------------------------------------------------------

Find the length and width of a rectangle has perimeter is 36 feet and toes area is 77 ft.²

------------------------------------------------------------

Assemption: The length and width are L and W.

SOLUTION

[tex]\tt 2(L+w)=36[/tex]

[tex]\tt Lw=77[/tex]

[tex]\tt L+w=18[/tex]

[tex]\tt L=18-w[/tex]

[tex]\tt (18-w)w=77[/tex]

[tex]\tt 18-w²=n[/tex]

[tex]\tt w²-18w+77=0[/tex]

[tex]\tt (w-7)(w-11)=0[/tex]

[tex]\tt w=7 \: or \: 11[/tex]

[tex]\tt 1=11 \: or \: 7[/tex]

[tex]\tt L>w[/tex]

[tex]\tt w=7 , L=11[/tex]

------------------------------------------------------------

[tex]\tt Length: 11 feet[/tex]

[tex]\tt width: 7 feet[/tex]
