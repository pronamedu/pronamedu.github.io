2. If the angle of elevation to the top of a tree is 20°. How far are you away from the tree, if the tree's height is 31 feet?


Answer:

85.17 feet

Step-by-step explanation:                            

                        / |  

                 /        |   31 ft.      

         /                |                              

 /²⁰ _ _ _ _ _  _ |  

First, we need to determine the relationship of the sides in relation to the angle. The given is opposite from the angle, while the base is adjacent to the circle.

Thus, this means that we make use of the tangent function,

[tex]tan\hspace{3}\theta = \frac{o}{a}\\a= \frac{o}{tan\hspace{3}\theta}\\\\a = 31/tan20\textordmasculine\\a \approx 85.17[/tex]

Therefore, the person is approximately 85.17 feet away from the tree.
