29. what is your role as a person working in tourism industry and as a tourist in maintaining the sustainability tourism in your place


Explanation:

The main responsibilities of sustainable tourism include: Protecting the environment, natural resources, and wildlife. Providing socio-economic benefits for communities who live in tourist destinations. Conserving cultural heritage and creating authentic tourist experiences.

The objective of sustainable tourism is to retain the economic and social advantages of tourism development while reducing or mitigating any undesirable impacts on the natural, historic, cultural or social environment. This is achieved by balancing the needs of tourists with those of the destination.
