9. Marlon's age is twice as his friend Maryon. Maryon is 5 years older than Chris. In 5 years Marlon will be three times as old as Chris. How old is myron now?


In the given problem, apply your knowledge in algebra and basic mathematics specially in solving unknown variables of linear equation.

Let

x Marlon's age

y Maryon's age

z Chris's age

Mathematical Equation

Marlon's age is twice as his friend Maryon.

x = 2y

Maryon is 5 years older than Chris.

y = z+5

In 5 years Marlon will be three times as old as Chris.

2y + 5 = 3(z+5)

Solution

x = 2y

y = ½x

y = z+5

z = y-5

z = ½x - 5

2y + 5 = 3(z+5)

2y + 5 = 3(y-5+5)

2y + 5 = 3y

3y - 2y = 5

y = 5

z = y - 5

z = 5 - 5

z = 0

x = 2y

x = 2(5)

x = 10

Check

Marlon's age is twice as his friend Maryon.

x = 10 (Marlon)

y = 5 (Maryon)

x = 2y

10 = 2(5) → 10 = 10 ✓

Maryon is 5 years older than Chris.

y = 5 (Maryon)

z = 0 (Chris)

y = 0 + 5

y = 5 ✓

In 5 years Marlon will be three times as old as Chris.

x = 10 (Marlon)

5 years, 10 + 5 = 15

z = 0 (Chris)

5 years, 0 + 5 = 5

x = 3z

15 = 3(5)

15 = 15 ✓

How old is Maryon now?Answer: 5 years old

=2(80)(70)/(80+70)

=74.6kph
