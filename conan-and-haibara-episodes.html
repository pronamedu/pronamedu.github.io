24. 1.) Conan has two numbers. He gives two clues about his numbers:Clue #1: The larger number is 10 more than the smaller number.Clue #2: Three times the larger number is five times the smaller numbeWhat are Conan's two numbers?​


Answer:

The two numbers are 15 and 25.

Step-by-step explanation:

Clue #1:  The larger number is 10 more than the smaller number.

Let:

Smaller number :  xLarger number: x + 10

Clue #2: Three times the larger number is five times the smaller number.

3(larger number) = 5(smaller number)

Equation:

3(x + 10) = 5(x)

Find x, the smaller number:

3(x + 10) = 5(x)

3x + 30 = 5x

5x - 3x = 30

2x = 30

2x/2 = 30/2

x = 15

When x = 15, find the larger number:

x + 10  ⇒  (15) + 10 = 25

The two numbers are 15 and 25.

Check:

Three times the larger number is five times the smaller number.

3(larger number) = 5(smaller number)3(25) = 5(15)75 = 75  (True)
