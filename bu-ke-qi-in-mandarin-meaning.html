23. A 5000 kg bus has a velocity of 8 m/s. What is the kinetic energy of the bus? Given: m________kg Find: KE Formula: (KE = ½mv²) Solution:​


✎ Problem:

A 5000 kg bus has a velocity of 8 m/s. What is the kinetic energy of the bus?

✎ Answer and solution:

✍︎ Kinetic energy is the energy possessed by a moving body by virtue of its mass and velocity. It is calculated by the formula

KE = ½ (mass × velocity²), where KE is in joules, mass is in kg and velocity is in m/s.

The solution:

Given that

mass: 5,000 kgvelocity: 8 m/sKE: ?

We can now calculate the KE of the bus

KE = ½ (mass × velocity²)KE = ½ [5,000 kg × (8 m/s)²]KE = ½ (5,000 kg × 64 m²/s²)KE = ½ (320,000 J)∴ KE = 160,000 J <answer>
