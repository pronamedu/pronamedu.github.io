40+y = 80
y = 40
∴ the dimensions are 40 by 40

(40)² ≥ ab
(40)(40) ≥ ab
∴
a = 40
b = 40

∴ The dimensions of the area if the enclosed space is to be large as possible is 40 ft. by 40 ft.

Answer:
The distance covered by Rey is 90ft, and the net displacement is 42.43ft (approx).


Solution:
The distance covered by Rey is:

Distance = 30ft (North) + 30ft (West) + 30ft (South) = 90ft

To find the displacement, we need to find the net displacement. Net displacement is the straight-line distance between the starting point and the ending point.

The horizontal displacement is 30ft (West) because he returns to his starting point in the west direction.

The vertical displacement is zero because he ends up at the same height as his starting point.

Using the Pythagorean theorem, we can find the net displacement:

Net Displacement = sqrt((30ft)^2 + (30ft)^2) = sqrt(1800) = 42.43ft (approx)

Therefore, the distance covered by Rey is 90ft, and the net displacement is 42.43ft (approx).
