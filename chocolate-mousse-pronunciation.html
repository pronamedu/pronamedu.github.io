To prepare a 250-g chocolate mousse, you need to use the percentages given in the recipe to determine the amount of each ingredient needed.

If the chocolate mousse is 35% chocolate, 30% cream, 20% milk, 10% sugar, and 5% butter, then:

The amount of chocolate needed is 35% of 250g = 87.5g

The amount of cream needed is 30% of 250g = 75g

The amount of milk needed is 20% of 250g = 50g

The amount of sugar needed is 10% of 250g = 25g

The amount of butter needed is 5% of 250g = 12.5g

So, you will use 75g of cream to prepare a 250-g chocolate mousse.
