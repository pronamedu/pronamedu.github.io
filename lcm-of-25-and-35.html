25. gcf and lcm of 35 and 45​


Answer:

GCF = 5

LCM = 315

Step-by-step explanation:

Factors of 35 = 1, 5, 7, 35

Factors of 45 = 1, 3, 5, 9, 15, 45

∴ The GCF is 5.

Find the prime factorization of the numbers.

Prime factorization of 35 = 5 · 7

Prime factorization of 45 = 3² · 5

Multiply the terms

LCM = 3² · 5 · 7

        = 315

∴ The LCM is 315
