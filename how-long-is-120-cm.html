17. Some pupils dug a pit which measures 120 cm long, 90 cm wide, and 30 cm deep. How many cubic cm of soil were removed? A. 300 000 cu. cm B. 432 000 cu. cm C. 400 000 cu. cm D. 324 000 cu. cm


QUESTION :

Some pupils dug a pit which measures 120 cm long, 90 cm wide, and 30 cm deep. How many cubic cm of soil were removed?

A. 300 000 cu. cm

B. 432 000 cu. cm

C. 400 000 cu. cm

D. 324 000 cu. cm

SOLUTION :

Given :Length ( L) : 120 cmWidth (W) : 90 cmHeight ( H) : 30 cm

Formula :V = L • W • H

Solve :V = L • W • HV = 120 • 90 • 30V = 324,000

Answer :Hence, the cubic centimeter of soil removed from a pit is 324,000 cubic cm.
