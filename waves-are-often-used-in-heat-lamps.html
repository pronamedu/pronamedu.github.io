Fast food restaurants keep food warm by placing them under heat lamps. These lamps transfer heat by what means?A. ConductionB. RadiationC. EvaporationD. Conduction​


Answer:

CONVECTIONRADIATION

Explanation:

1.Convection Heating -Making popcorn by CONVECTION heating.Plug in and turn on the popper. How it works: Hot air begins to rise in the popper while cooler air falls. As hot air circulates past the popcorn kernels, the kernels absorb the heat. When the kernels absorb enough heat to reach 100°C, the water inside turns to steam.

2.Fast food restaurants keep food hot with infrared lamps. The heat is transferred to the food by d. radiation. There is convection in between the heat source, but the heat is still radiated. Radiation is the transfer of pure energy, unlike convection and conduction which are basically molecule to molecule contact.
