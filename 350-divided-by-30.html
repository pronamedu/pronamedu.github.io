4. the original price of the dress is ₱350. it is sale for 30% what is the new price of the dress?​


Answer:

₱105

Step-by-step explanation:

original price = ₱350

discount = 30%

new price = original price × discount

» Substitute original price as ₱350 and discount as 30% based on the given formula.

new price = ₱350 × 30%

» To multiply the orignal price by the discount, you will need to change 30% into decimal. Move two decimal places to the left (0.30%) and remove the percent sign (0.30). Thus, 30% = 0.30.

new price = ₱350 × 0.30

new price = ₱105

Therefore, the new price of the dress costs ₱105.

13. A small toy car costing 350 pesos is reduced by 30% in the sale.how much is the total amount reduced?​


A small toy car costing 350 pesos is reduced by 30% in the sale. How much is the total amount reduced?

• Identifying

350 is the base & 30% is the rate. So we find the percentage or the total amount reduced.

• Formula

If we're finding the percentage, then our formula will be R x B = P

• Turning

Ok, before we multiply. How are going to multiply with a percent? (30%) we just move the decimal point two times to the left that can result of a decimal.

[tex]30\% \: = \: 0.30[/tex]

• Solving

Now, we multiply R x B.

[tex]350 \: \times \: 0.30[/tex]

[tex] = 105[/tex]

• Answer

P = 105
