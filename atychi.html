• WHO (is involved)

27. what is catchy headline


CATCHY HEADLINEA catchy headline is a headline which consist of words and thoughts which are designed to catch or get someone's eyes and get that person's interest to read the article.A catchy headline is very important in a newspaper or magazine because it entices readers to read the article that follows the headline.In advertisement, catchy headline is also very important to be able to gain prospect and hopefully successful buyers or customers.

HOW TO MAKE A CATCHY HEADLINE?Use numbers to give concrete takeaways. Use emotional objectives to describe your reader's problem. Use unique rationale to demonstrate what the reader will get out of the article. Use what, why, how, or when. Make an audacious promise.
