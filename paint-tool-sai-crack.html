8. What to do when your gouache paint starts to dry and crack? It's completely dry.


Explanation:

The dried gouache film is brittle and will flake off if applied to a flexible surface such as regular water color paper or a sketchbook page. The best way to avoid cracking is to paint on a rigid surface like the original illustration board or better yet, a gesso coated masonite or metal panel.

23. scientists have stated that the lithosphere is cracked into slabs of rocks.do you think they have scientific basis for saying so?


#BrainlyFast

What the scientist is referring to the cracked in which the lithosphere are divided into a several segments of slabs rock, this cracked refers to the plate boundaries. One of the major basis for the scientist in locating the plate boundaries are the location of the mountain ranges, volcanoes and earthquake epicenter. The scientific basis also for the scientist that the lithosphere are made up of rocks are that magma which comes from the mantle and expelled on the surface of the earth will cools down and forms rock.

Types of Plate

1. Continental Plate

Continental plates are much thicker compared with oceanic plate. Continental plate carries the major continents around the world.

2. Oceanic Plate

Oceanic plate are much thinner and denser compared with continental plate. The pacific plate is an example of oceanic plate.

Three Types of Plate Boundaries Created by Plate movement:

Transform Fault Boundary -  Two plates are sliding past each other. Divergent Plate Boundary -  Two plates are moving away from each other. Convergent Plate Boundary -   Two plates are moving towards each other.
