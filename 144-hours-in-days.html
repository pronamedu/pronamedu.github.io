21. Elena drives for 3hours at 44mph. Claire drives 144 miles in 4 hours. How far would elana travel if she drove for 3 hours at the same speed as claire


Answer:

108 miles

Step-by-step explanation:

Distance is the amount of space a thing covered as it traveled. Its units are miles, kilometers, and meters.

Time is how long something moved or traveled.

Speed is the rate at which something moves. It measures the amount of distance traveled per unit of time. Its units are miles per second/minute/hour, kilometers per second/minute/hour, and meters per second/minute/hour.

To determine the distance, speed, and time or an event, make use of the following formulas:

[tex]distance = speed \: \times \: time[/tex]

[tex]time = \frac{distance}{speed} [/tex]

[tex]speed = \frac{distance}{time} [/tex]

It is also advisable to solve the problems using the GAS method covering the Given, Asked, and Solution.

For this particular problem, there is a comparison between the distance, speed, and time of Elena and Claire.

For Elena, it was stated that she drove for 3 hours at 44 mph.

Given:

3 hours - time

44 mph - speed

Asked:

distance

Solution:

distance = speed x time

distance = 44 mph x 3 hours

distance = 132 miles.

Within 3 hours and at a speed of 44 mph, Elena was able to travel 132 miles.

For Claire, it was stated that she drove for 4 hours and was able to cover 144 miles.

GIven:

4 hours - time

144 miles - distance

Asked:

speed

Solution:

speed = distance/time

speed = 144 miles/4 hours

speed = 36 mph

Within 4 hours, Claire was able to travel 144 miles at a speed of 36 mph.

The main question is, how far would Elena travel if she drove for 3 hours at the same speed as Claire?

Already knowing the speed of Claire, substitute it with that of Elena's.

Given:

3 hours - time

36 mph - speed

Asked:

distance

Solution:

distance = speed x time

distance = 36 mph x 3 hours

distance = 108 miles

If Elena traveled at the same amount of time she traveled before but with the same speed as that of Claire's, she would only be able to cover 108 miles.
