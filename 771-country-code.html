Answer:

No

Step-by-step explanation:

The divisibility rule of 6 is when a number is divisible by 2 and 3.

The divisibility rule of 3 is when you add all of its digits, it must be divisible to 3 (7 + 7+1 = 15) so it is divisible by 3

The divisibility rule of 2 is when the last digit of a number is an even number (771) 1 is not an even number, so

3 - check

2 - wrong

It is not divisible by two and 3, so it is not divisible by 6.
