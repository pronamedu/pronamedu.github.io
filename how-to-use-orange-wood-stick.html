2. How to use orange wood stick?


Answer:

An orange stick is a manicure tool used for cleaning fingernails and pushing back cuticles. One end of the stick is generally pointed and somewhat sharp while the other is flat and angled, The tool is very portable and generally inexpensive, making it an easy way to maintain nails on the go.

11. Pumice Stone - A type of volcanic rock used to remove callouses.

12. Callous Remover - A tool designed to strip off calluses and corns.

13. Cuticle Nipper - An implement used to cut cuticle.

14. Foot File - It is made up of metal or sandpaper with a rough file on one side to remove calluses.

15. Pedicure Nail Brush - A tool used to removed cosmetics and cleanse nails.

16. Manicure Nail Brush - Is a plastic with nylon bristles used to clean dirt and cosmetic residue from the nail surface and skin.

17. Cuticle Scissors - An implement used to cut stubborn cuticle.

18. Nail Cutter/Clipper - A hand tool which is made of metal and is used to trim fingernails and toenails.

19. Cuticle Nail Pusher - The tool that is used to push back or loosen the cuticles.

20. Orange Wood Stick - The implement tool with pointed and rounded ends to remove excess polish
