17. 16. A light ray is reflected 40° at the plane mirror. What is it angle of incidence? 40° A. 3650 B. 180° C. 800 D. 40° ​


Answer:

D. 40°

Explanation:

Similarly, the angle of reflection is the angle made by the reflected ray with a normal drawn to the surface of reflection. Here, the incident ray makes an angle of 40° with the normal to the reflecting surface and hence the angle of incidence is also 40°.
