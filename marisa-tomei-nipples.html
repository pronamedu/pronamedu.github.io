6. Marisa purchases a pot for her father's garden. The diameter of the bottom of the pot      is 18 cm. What is the area of the pot's bottom?Marisa purchases a pot for her father's garden. The diameter of the bottom of the pot      is 18 cm. What is the area of the pot's bottom?


Answer:

The answer is 254.34².

Step-by-step explanation:
                                                Problem:
Marisa purchases a pot for her father's garden. The diameter of the bottom of the pot      is 18 cm. What is the area of the pot's bottom?

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

In this problem, the diameter is clearly shown here. Well, if the diameter is given, the formula we're going to be using is:

pi x radius squared divided by 4.

A = ℼ x d²
             4

Therefore, pi's (ℼ) value is 3.14. And our diameter is 18 cm, which looks like:

(3.14) x   18²            ---------------->        A = 254.34².
               4

SOLUTION:
-
3.14 TIMES 18 TIMES 18
(3.14 x 18 x 18 OR 3.14 x 18²)

OUR PRODUCT IS 1017.36, let us now divide 1017.36 with 4.
1017.36/4= 254.34cm²

And that is our answer.

This is a shortcut but you could also multiply the diameter two times without the pi value or 3.14, then after you multiply the diameter two times, multiply the product with the 3.14, ends up with dividing by 4 and that quotient is the final answer.

"So, in school we learn the formula for the area of a circle as

d=2r

r=d/2

Now by substituting this back into our formula:

A=π(d/2)2
