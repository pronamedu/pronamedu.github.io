Six classification of drugsGateway Drugs - legal drugs that a non-drug user might try (ex. cigarette, alcohol)Depressant Drugs - drugs that slow down a person's central nervous system and relax muscles and nerves (ex. alcohol, tranquilizers)Stimulant Drugs - drugs that speed up a person's central nervous system and make a person's energy high (ex. caffeine, cocaine, shabu)Narcotics - drugs that relieve pain and induce sleepiness (ex. heroin, marijuana)Hallucinogens - drugs that distorts reality and facts (ex. psilocybin)Inhalants - drugs that are found in household chemical products and anesthetics (ex. acetone, rugby)
