9. mr santos borrowed 18000 at 2% interest for one year what was the total amount he paid at the end of the year​


Mr. Santos borrowed Php 18 000 at 2% interest for one year. What was the total amount he paid at the end of the year?

Given:

P = Php 18 000

r = 0.02 (2%)

Find:

F

Formula:

F = P + i

where,

F = Future Value

P = Principal Value

i = amount of interest (P × r)

Solution:

F = 18 000 + (18 000 × 0.02)

F = 18 000 + 360

F = 18 360

Answer:

The total amount Mr. Santos paid at the

end of the year was Php 18 360.

23. solution of 18000 joules of electrical energy in 1hr of usage how much power does the bulb has generated​


To find the power generated by the bulb, we can use the formula:

Power = Energy / Time

Here, the energy consumed by the bulb is 18000 joules, and the time it took to consume this energy is 1 hour, which is equivalent to 3600 seconds.

Substituting these values into the formula, we get:

Power = 18000 joules / 3600 seconds Power = 5 watts

Therefore, the bulb has generated 5 watts of power.
