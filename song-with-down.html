30. Think of a Filipino song which is about intersubjectivity. Write down the lyrics of the song and analyze how intersubjectivity is explored by the songSONG:ANALYSIS​


Answer:

SONG: Kung Iniibig Ka Niya by Laarni Lozada

ANALYSIS:

The song undoubtedly possesses various conceptions of empathy and intimately related to it. When listening to the song, we put ourselves to the feelings of the singer and we felt like we endured the same torment as the melody itself. We endured the same feelings the way the song portrayed it to be and feel the sensation brought by the song.

Intersubjectivity, a term originally coined by the philosopher Edmund Husserl (1859–1938), is most simply stated as the interchange of thoughts and feelings, both conscious and unconscious, between two persons or “subjects,” as facilitated by empathy.
