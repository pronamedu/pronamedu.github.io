6. Matt, Phoebe, Geo, and Ruby form a straight line. Geo is exactly in the middle of Matt and Phoebe. On the other hand, Ruby is between Matt and Phoebe. The ratio of the distance of Matt and Ruby to the distance of Ruby and Phoebe is 13 : 5 . If Matt is 36 ft away from Geo, how far is Ruby from Matt and from Phoebe, respectively?


Answer:

Ruby is 20 ft away from Phoebe. On the other hand, Ruby is 52 ft away from Matt.

Step-by-step explanation:

Step 1: Write the given:

Geo is exactly in the middle of Matt and Phoebe.Matt is 36 ft away from Geo.Ruby is also in the middle of Matt and Phoebe however the ratio of the distance of Matt and Ruby to Ruby and Phoebe is 13:5.

Step 2: Determine the distance between Matt and Phoebe

Since the distance between Matt and Geo is 36 ft, then the distance from Geo and Phoebe is also 36 ft. Then the distance between Matt and Phoebe is twice that of the distance between Matt and Geo, which is 72 ft.

Step 3: Determine the distance of Ruby from Matt and Phoebe.

Since the ratio of distance of Matt and Ruby to Ruby and Phoebe is 13:5, divide the 72 ft to 18 parts (13+5).72 ft divided to 18 parts is 4 ft.13 parts multiplied to 4 ft is 52 ft5 parts multiplied to 4 ft is 20 ftRuby is closer to Phoebe than MattRuby is 20 ft from Phoebe and 52 ft from Matt.

Therefore, Ruby is 20 ft from Phoebe and 52 ft from Matt.

11. if 10 rubies can be exchange for 2 sapphires,7 sapphires can be exchanged for 3 emeralds, 12 emeralds can be exchanged for 2 diamonds,how many rubies can 7 diamonds be exchanged for?


Answer:

490 rubies


Step-by-step explanation:

10 rubies = 2 sapphires

7 sapphires = 3 emeralds

12 emeralds = 2 diamonds


Find the number of emeralds 7 diamonds can exchange for:

2 diamonds = 12 emeralds

1 diamond = 12 ÷ 2 = 6 emeralds

7 diamonds = 6 x 7 = 42 emeralds


Find the number of sapphires 42 emeralds can exchange for:

3 emeralds = 7 sapphires

1 emerald = 7 ÷ 3 = 7/3 sapphires

42 emeralds = 7/3 x 42 = 98 sapphires


Find the number of rubies 98 sapphires can exchange for:

2 sapphires = 10 rubies

1 sapphire = 10 ÷ 2 = 5 rubies

98 sapphires = 5 x 98 = 490 rubies


Answer: 7 diamonds can exchange for 490 rubies.


12. Rosie sold an emerald necklace that cost P397,500 and she will get 1%commission. She sold ruby earrings that cost P106,000 and she will get 4%commission. Which piece of jewelry gives her more commission?​


GIVEN⤵️

Rosie sold an emerald necklace that cost P397,500 and she will get 1% commission. She sold ruby earrings that cost P106,000 and she will get 4% commission.

WHAT IS ASKED⤵️

Which piece of jewelry gives her more commission?

SOLUTION⤵️

Formula => C = Sales × Rate of C

where C = commission

For the emerald necklace:

1% = 0.01

C = P397,500 × 0.01 C = P3,975

For the ruby earrings:

4% = 0.04

C = P106,000 × 0.04C = P4240

Base on the solution the commission of ruby earrings is greater than the commission of emerald necklace.

ANSWER⤵️

Ruby earrings gives more commission to Rosie.
