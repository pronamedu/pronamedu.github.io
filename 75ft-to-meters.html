5. A church spire in the form of a regular hexagonal pyramid whose base edge is 8ft. and whose altitude is 75ft. is to be painted at a cost of 22 dollars per square yard. What is the total cost


the area to be painted is only the lateral surface area or LSA
LSA= pl/2

where
 p= perimeter of the base
l= slant height 

to get the slant height, compute for the apothem..

apothem = sin60° x base edge
               = √3/ 2 x 8
apothem = 4√3

l= √(h²+ap²)
  = √(75²+ (4√3)²)
  = √5625 +48
l = 75.32 ft

LSA = (6(8) x 75.32) / 2
LSA= 1, 807.68 ft² 

1 807.68 ft² x  (1 yd)²/ (3ft)2 = 200.85 yd²
