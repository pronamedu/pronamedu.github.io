9. if 10 rubies can be exchange for 2 sapphires,7 sapphires can be exchanged for 3 emeralds, 12 emeralds can be exchanged for 2 diamonds,how many rubies can 7 diamonds be exchanged for?


Answer:

490 rubies


Step-by-step explanation:

10 rubies = 2 sapphires

7 sapphires = 3 emeralds

12 emeralds = 2 diamonds


Find the number of emeralds 7 diamonds can exchange for:

2 diamonds = 12 emeralds

1 diamond = 12 ÷ 2 = 6 emeralds

7 diamonds = 6 x 7 = 42 emeralds


Find the number of sapphires 42 emeralds can exchange for:

3 emeralds = 7 sapphires

1 emerald = 7 ÷ 3 = 7/3 sapphires

42 emeralds = 7/3 x 42 = 98 sapphires


Find the number of rubies 98 sapphires can exchange for:

2 sapphires = 10 rubies

1 sapphire = 10 ÷ 2 = 5 rubies

98 sapphires = 5 x 98 = 490 rubies


Answer: 7 diamonds can exchange for 490 rubies.

23. How is grand Bank formed


The Grand Banks of Newfoundland are a group of underwater plateaus south-east of Newfoundland on the North American continental shelf. These areas are relatively shallow, ranging from 50 to 300 feet (15 to 91 m) in depth. The cold Labrador Current mixes with the warm waters of the Gulf Stream here.

The mixing of these waters and the shape of the ocean bottom lifts nutrients to the surface. These conditions helped to create one of the richest fishing grounds in the world. Fish species include Atlantic cod, swordfish, haddock and capelin; shellfish include scallop and lobster. The area also supports large colonies of seabirds such as northern gannets, shearwaters and sea ducks and various sea mammals such as seals, dolphins and whales.

In addition to the effects on nutrients, the mixing of the cold and warm currents often causes fog in the area. It is also noted for its proximity to the sinking of the RMS Titanic and thus the launching point of Titanic shipwreck expeditions.
