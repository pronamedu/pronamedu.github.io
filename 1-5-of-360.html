28. 1 and 1/3 of what number is 360? ​


Answer:

The value of the missing number is 270.

Step-by-step explanation:

1.) Let [tex]x[/tex] be the number stated in the problem.

2.) Rewrite the problem in algebraic form.

⇒ [tex]1\frac{1}{3} x = 360[/tex]

3.) Change all mixed numbers or mixed fractions to improper fractions.

⇒ [tex]\frac{4}{3} x = 360[/tex]

4.) Multiply both sides by 3, to get rid of all the denominators in the equation.

⇒ [tex](3) \frac{4}{3} x = 360 (3)[/tex]

⇒ [tex]4x = 1080[/tex]

5.) Divide both sides by 4, to isolate or get the value of the variable [tex]x[/tex].

⇒ [tex]\frac{4x}{4} = \frac{1080}{4}[/tex]

⇒ [tex]x = 270[/tex]

6.) Therefore, the value of [tex]x[/tex], or the value of the missing number is 270.
