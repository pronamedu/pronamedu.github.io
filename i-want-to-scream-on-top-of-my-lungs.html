16. Classify the following statements by writing Direct Speech or Reported Speech on the blanks. 1. They told me that they went to a meeting. 2. The lady said, "I gave the police my address." 3. Jane says, "I want to buy a magazine." 4. He said that they trust the quality of our product. 5. The child scream, "I want some ice cream!"


Answer:

1. Reported Speech

2. Direct Speech

3. Direct Speech

4. Reported Speech

5. Direct Speech

Direct Speech- repeats exactly what someone said or quotes the same words spoken. Direct Speech has quotation marks like this ( " ).

Reported Speech- (Inderect Speech) is narrating or retelling what the speaker said in the past and so there are changes in the actual words. Reported Speech does not use quotation marks.

Examples:

Direct Speech

Lizel said, "I like your story book."

Reported Speech

Lizel said that she liked my story book.
