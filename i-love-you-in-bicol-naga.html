Tabaco City is 81 km from Naga. How many

meters are you going to travel if you want to visit your grandmother in Naga?

ANSWER 81 000 meters

[tex]1km = 1000m[/tex]

1 kilometers is equivalent to 1,000 meters.

[tex]81 \times 1000 = 81000m[/tex]

So, 81 kilometers (distance of Tabaco city going to Naga city) multiplied by 1000 meters is 81,000 meters.
