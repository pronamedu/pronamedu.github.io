Answer:

x - intercept = (20/3, 0)

y - intercept = (0, 2)

Step-by-step explanation:

x - intercept (y = 0)

y = -0.3x + 2

0 = -0.3x + 2

(3/10)x = 2

3x = 20

x = 20/3

y - intercept (x = 0)

y = -0.3x + 2

y = -0.3(0) + 2

y = 2
