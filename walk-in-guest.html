1. what is walk in customers/guests?​


Answer:

A walk-in' is a guest who walks in through the door seeking accommodation for the night without having made a reservation. Walk-ins represent a very small proportion of guests but hotels like to have Walk-ins because they tend to be able to be charged more.


2. Why should walk-in guests be treated with the same courtesy and hospitality as guests who made a reservation?​


Answer:

Treating guests like family improves the guest's experience. It helps make their stay much more comfortable and satisfactory and could lead them to visit us again in the future (consolidation).


3. how do you deal with walk in guest ​


Answer:

Treat the guest warmly with ready smile as like as registered guest. Create such situation so that the guest does not feel hesitated. Ask his desire length of stay and room preference. Check whether such room is available or not in those days.
