Similar polygons are similar if they have the exact shape, but can be in different sizes. Congruent polygons have the same number of sides and all corresponding sides and interior angles are congruent. These will have the same shape and size, but are may be rotated, or be the mirror image of the other.

27. what is congruent polygon​


Answer:

Congruent polygons are polygons that have the same shape and size. In other words, if you can move one polygon so that it coincides exactly with the other polygon, then they are congruent. To be congruent, the corresponding angles and sides of the polygons must be equal. Congruent polygons are denoted by the symbol ≅.

For two polygons to be congruent, they must have an equal number of sides (and hence an equal number—the same number—of vertices).
