2. Describe the woman from the poem liham ni pinay mula sa brunei, from your own words.​


Answer:

I think that Pinay’s decision to work abroad is a true act of courage. It takes guts for a Filipino, especially a woman, to work overseas and face entirely foreign culture and customs. Giving up the role of a subservient wife to be a mother that provides for her children is indeed a great sacrifice for her. Even though it hurts for a mother to not be by her children’s side in the most important days of their lives (birthday, Christmas, graduation), if it means she can give their needs that her husband is unable to provide, then she would definitely do it. Pinay has greatly portrayed the essence of a Filipina mother: a woman that prioritizes her children over everything, even over the norms of the society.
