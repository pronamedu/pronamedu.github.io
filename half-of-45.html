1. What is half of 60 added to a third of 45?


Answer:

The answer is 45.

Explanation:

By analysis of the problem, we can see that there are 2 numbers that will be added to each other. The first number is "half of 60" and the second number is "third of 45".

The phrase "half of 60" is also the same with "one half (1/2) of 60".

Solving for the first number, we have,

60 ÷ 2 = 30  or (60) * (1/2) = 30Hence, our first number is 30.

On the other hand, the phrase "third of 45" is the same as "one third (1/3) of 45".

Solving for the second number, we have,

45 ÷ 3 = 15  or(45) * (1/3) = 15Hence, our second number is 15.

Solving the problem, we add the first and second number.

30 + 15 = 45

Therefore, the answer to the question is 45.
