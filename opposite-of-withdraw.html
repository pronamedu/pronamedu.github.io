In real life application of integers, there are keywords we use to represent them.

The following are mostly used:

Deposit:  +         Withdrawal:  -

Profit/Income/Gain:  +    Loss:  -

Increase:  +   Decrease:  -

North/Up:  +    South/Down:  -

Wins:  +   Losses:  -

Above sea level:  +    Below sea level:  -

Rise:  +           Fall/Drop:  -

Gain:  +      Loss:  -

A withdrawal of 250 means a deduction from one's bank savings or current account.  

To translate to integers  ⇒   - 250  (read as: negative 250)
