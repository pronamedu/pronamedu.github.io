10. if an airplane travels at 5000 miles for one hour snd 45minutes.what distance does it travel​


Answer:

If the airplane travels at 5000 miles for one hour and 45 minutes, we need to convert the time to hours:

60 minutes = 1 hour

45 minutes = 45/60 hours = 0.75 hours

Therefore, the total time is 1 hour + 0.75 hours = 1.75 hours.

To find the distance traveled, we can use the formula:

distance = rate x time

where rate is the speed of the airplane, which is 5000 miles per hour.

distance = 5000 miles/hour x 1.75 hours

distance = 8750 miles

Therefore, the airplane travels 8750 miles in one hour and 45 minutes.

24. A 5000-N horizontal force is appliednto Lolo Dads stalled car whose mass is 1500 kg. find the cars speed after 10 second


Answer:

30 m/s

Step-by-step explanation:

Using Newton's Third Law of Motion, we have the equation F = ma

We are given

F = 5000 Nm = 1500 kg

From that we manipulate the equation to find acceleration

5000N / 1500 kg = acceleration

we get acceleration = 3.333 m/s^2

To get the speed after 10 seconds, Assuming that the car was at rest at 0 seconds applying one of the Kinematic Equations

Speed = Initial Velocity + Acceleration • time

Speed = 0 + (3.333 m/s^2)(10 s)

Speed = 30.333 m/s

Applying Significant Figures, we get

Speed = 30 m/s
