28. Why tears naturally come out of your eyes when you slice onions?


It has something to do with the onion.

What do onions contain?

Onions contain amino acid sulfoxides that form sulfenic acids in the onion cells. When you cut the onion, the otherwise separate enzymes start mixing up and produce propanethiol S-oxide, which is a volatile sulphur compound that starts wafting towards your eyes. The gas emitted reacts with the water of your eyes and forms sulphuric acid. The sulphuric acid then produces burning sensation in your eyes and this in turn leads to tear glands. Therefore you end up watery tears or "crying" every time you cut onions.
