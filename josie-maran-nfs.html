Answer:

Josie is 18 years old

Step-by-step explanation:

let x be julie

let x-4 be josie

Equation:

x + (x-4) = 40

2x - 4 = 40

2x = 44

x = 22

Substitute the value to x: x = 22, 22 - 4 = 18
