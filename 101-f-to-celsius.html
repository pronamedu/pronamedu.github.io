Problem:What is 250°F to °C?

Answer:

250°F is equal to 121.11°C.

Step-by-step explanation:

To find the Farenheit to Celcius, use this formula:

[tex]°C = \frac{5}{9} (°F - 32)[/tex]

Given:

250°F

Solution:

[tex]°C = \frac{5}{9} (°F - 32)[/tex]

Step 1: Change the °F to the given Farenheit.

[tex]°C = \frac{5}{9} (250 - 32)[/tex]

Step 2: Solve for the numbers on the parenthesis first.

[tex]°C = \frac{5}{9} (218)[/tex]

Step 3: Multiply the 218 to the numerator of 5/9.

[tex]°C = \frac{5 \times 218}{9} \\ °C = \frac{1090}{9}[/tex]

Step 4: Divide the fraction.

[tex]°C = 1090 \div 9 \\ °C = 121. 111...[/tex]

Step 5: Round off it to the nearest hundredths.

[tex]°C = 121. 11[/tex]

The final answer is...

[tex]\blue{\boxed{121.11°C}}[/tex]
