23. 3. The sum of two angles is 180 degrees. Their difference is 70 degrees. Find the angles?


Answer:

The angles are 125° and 55°

Step-by-step explanation:

Write both equations

[tex]x + y = 180 \\ x - y = 70 [/tex]

Find x in terms of y

[tex] x = 70 + y [/tex]

Substitute to the other equation

[tex](70 + y) + y = 180 \\ 70 + 2y = 180 \\ 2y = 180 - 70 \\ 2y = 110 \\ \frac{2y}{2} = \frac{110}{2} \\ y = 55[/tex]

If y = 55, then:

[tex]x + y = 180 \\ x + 55 = 180 \\ x = 180 - 5 \\ x = 125[/tex]

Checking:

[tex]125 + 55 = 180 \\ 125 - 55 = 70[/tex]

Therefore, correct.
