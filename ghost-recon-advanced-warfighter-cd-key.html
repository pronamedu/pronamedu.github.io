27. If you see a message to press any key to boot the CD and you fail to do, what do you think will happen? Will the computer will restart?


Answer:

If you failed to press any key from the prompt "Press any key to boot the CD" the computer will not restart but it will not carry on to its installation process. If you don't have any OS installed on your computer, you will get the message repeatedly. The “Press any key message” will appear once you set your boot drive priority on your BIOS setting.  

Typically here are the steps to enter the BIOS on windows:

Restart your PC.Press F2 or Del after the start-up screen appears.On the BIOS screen, change your boot sequence in the BIOS Setup Utility Menu.After changing the boot order you can now press “Save and Exit” key.

If your installation is done but you still get the "Press any key" message, you just have to remove your USB stick, CD, DVD, or any external device. You can also change your boot priority. However, if your computer hangs at the "Press any key to boot from CD" screen just try to unplug and plug your installation devices.
