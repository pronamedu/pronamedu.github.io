28. Mrs. Pascual bought a lot that has the shape of a parallelogram. Its base is 60 meters and its height is 40 meters. What is the area of the lot?​


Answer:

A = 2400m²

explenation:

hope it helps=)

[tex]\red{\bold{Answer:}}[/tex]

Mrs. Pascual has a lot with a

2400 meters area.

[tex]\blue{\bold{Explanation:}}[/tex]

Let A be the area of the lot. let h be the height, b be the base of the lot.

Given

60 m and 40 m.

Answer:

2400 meters

Solution:

A = b x h

= 60 x 40

= 2400 m

You don't get it? Alright here is a basic step-by-step guide.

Step 1, Name the Area, height and base. Step 2, Write A, then =, and then put b x h. Step 3, At the bottom of it write =, then 60 x 40. Step 4, Multiply the given numbers.
