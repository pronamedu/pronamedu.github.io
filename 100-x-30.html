Answer: B.80
........................SOLUTION:

(x + 30) + 2(x - 30) = 180

x + 30 + 2x - 60 = 180

3x - 30 = 180

3x = 210

3x/3 = 210/3

x = 70


Substitution:

(x + 30)°

[(70) + 30]°

100°


2(x - 30)°

2[(70) - 30]°

2(40)°

80°

Therefore, the two consecutive angles are: 100° & 80°


Smaller Angle: 80°

Larger Angle: 100°


ANSWER: B. 80°
