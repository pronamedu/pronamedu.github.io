Answer:

Despite large number of teeth, they don't chew their food - they swallow their prey in one piece. All dolphins are carnivores (meat-eaters). Smaller dolphins eat fish and squids, while large dolphins like orca eat seals, sea lions and other type of dolphins and whales.
