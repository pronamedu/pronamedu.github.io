Answer:

Add all the ratios.

[tex]1+2+3=\boldsymbol{6}[/tex]Step 2:

Divide the total number of cookies by the sum of the ratios.

[tex]\frac{120}{6}=\boldsymbol{20}[/tex]Step 3:

Multiply all the ratios by the quotient in Step 2.

[tex]1\times20=\boxed{\boldsymbol{20}}[/tex][tex]2\times20=\boxed{\boldsymbol{40}}[/tex][tex]3\times20=\boxed{\boldsymbol{60}}[/tex]
