ADDITIONAL INFORMATION ABOUT LEARNING PROGRESSIVE TENSES

» Progressive Tense is a kind of verb that is used continuously or doing something perhaps rather. They use -ing, are/is + -ing, and be + -ing

Present progressive tense» Present progressive tenses is a kind of verb tense that shows present of action continuously. They use are/is + -ing.

Past progressive tense» Past progressive tenses is a kind of verb tense that shows something that was continously doing. They use was/we're + -ing

Future progressive tense» Future progressive tense is a kind of verb tense that shows something that we be doing continously. They use will be/shall be + -ing.
