From the given problem, we will first compute only the area of one triangle:

The formula for finding the area of a triangle is as follows:

Area = 2/bh

Where:

b stands for the base andh stands for height.

Now, we gonna substitute them to their corresponding values.

We have:

6 inches for the base and4 inches for the height.

After substituting them, we will next evaluate them by the indicated operation.

SOLUTION:

Lastly, we will multiply the answer by 3.

12 sq. inch. x 3 = 36 sq. inch.

Therefore, the combined area of all (three) triangles is 36 sq. inches.
