17. In a garden plot of 600 square meters, 180 square meters is planted with eggplants. What percent of the garden plot has eggplants? (with solution pls)


To solve this problem, we need to find the percentage of the garden plot that is planted with eggplants.

The percentage of the garden plot that is planted with eggplants can be found by dividing the area of the eggplants by the total area of the garden plot and then multiplying by 100.

Area of eggplants = 180 square meters

Total area of garden plot = 600 square meters

Percentage of garden plot with eggplants = (Area of eggplants / Total area of garden plot) x 100

Percentage of garden plot with eggplants = (180 / 600) x 100

Percentage of garden plot with eggplants = 0.3 x 100

Percentage of garden plot with eggplants = 30%

Therefore, 30% of the garden plot has eggplants.
