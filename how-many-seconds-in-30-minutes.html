9. A clock was reading the time accurately on Friday at noon. On Monday at 6pm the clock was running late by 468 seconds. On average, how many seconds did the clock skip every 30 minutes?​


Answer:

WORD PROBLEM

The clock was skipping 3 seconds every 30 minutes. It is important to note the intervals to accurately divide the total seconds the clock was running late by. We also have to bear in mind the number of hours in a day.

The clock was still accurate by Friday noon. The clock was late by 468 seconds by Monday, 6 pm. To solve the problem, we must:

1. Know how many 30-minutes have passed during the time period.

    1 day = 24 hours

    1 hour = 60 minutes = 2 × (30 minutes)

    1 day = 24 hours × 2 × (30 minutes)

    1 day = 48 × (30 minutes)

Thus, there are 48 30-minutes in a day. On Friday, however, we start counting at noon, which is half of the day. Moreover, on Monday, the mark is only up to 6 pm, which is three-fourths of the day.

    Friday = 48 × \frac{1}{2}21  = 24

    Saturday = 48

    Sunday = 48

    Monday = 48 × \frac{3}{4}43  = 36

    TOTAL = 24 + 48 + 48 + 36 = 156

Hence, the total number of 30-minutes that have passed is 156. There were 156 30-minutes that have passed during the time period.

2. Divide the number of total seconds late to the number of 30-minutes passed.

    x = 468 seconds ÷ 156 30-minutes

    x = 3 seconds  

Therefore, the clock was skipping 3 seconds every 30 minutes from Friday noon to Monday 6 pm.

19. A clock was reading the time accurately on Tuesday at noon. On Thursday at 3pm the clock was running late by 612 seconds. On average, how many seconds did the clock skip every 30 minutes?


Answer:

0.16667 seconds

Step-by-step explanation:

The problem states that:

: at Tuesday noon ( 12:00 pm) the clock was still accurate

: However at 3pm on Thursday, the clock was running late by 612 seconds

Find: average seconds the clock skipped every 30 minutes

To solve this, we have to determine how many minutes have elapsed from the time the clock read accurately (Tuesday at 12:00 pm) until the last reading (Thursday at 3:00 pm).

Solution:

Tuesday 12:00 pm

12:00pm - 12:am (12 hours passed)

Wednesday

12:00am - 12:00pm (12 hours passed)

12:00pm - 12:00am (12 hours passed)

Thursday

12:00am -12:00pm (12 hours passed)

12:00pm - 3:00pm (3 hours passed)

Get the total hours:

Total hours = 12+12+12+12+3 = 51 hours

The question asks on the basis of every 30 minutes, so we have to solve how many 30 minutes is there in 51 hours.

(Remember in 1 hour there are 2 - 30 minutes, so we multiply 51 by 2)

= 51 x 2 = 102

: there are 102 thirty minutes in 51 hours

Now, the clock was late for 612 seconds.

To get the average seconds the clocked skipped for every 30 minutes, let's name it X, we use division

X = total number of 30 minutes / total seconds the clock was late

X = 102/612

X = 0.16667 seconds

Conclusion: The clock skipped 0.16667 seconds for every 30 minutes.
