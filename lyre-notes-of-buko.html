22. what is the description of lyres​


Answer:

An ancient stringed musical instrument (a yoke lute chordophone) of Greek origin, consisting of two arms extending from a body to a crossbar (a yoke), and strings, parallel to the soundboard, connecting the body to the yoke.Any instrument of the same musicological classification; any yoke lute.A lyre-shaped sheet music holder that attaches to a wind instrument when a music stand is impractical.(obsolete) A composer of lyric poetry.

Sana Makatulong

Explanation:

Lyre, stringed musical instrument having a yoke, or two arms and a crossbar, projecting out from and level with the body. The strings run from a tailpiece on the bottom or front of the instrument to the crossbar.
