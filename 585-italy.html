15. two numbers differ by 9. the sum of their square is 585. what are the numbers​


Let:

x = 1st number

y = 2nd number

Given:

x - y = 9 (1st equation)

x² + y² = 585 (2nd equation)

From x - y = 9,

x = y + 9 (3rd equation)

Plug in x = y + 9 to the 2nd equation:

(y + 9)² + y² = 585 (Use this equation to solve for y)

y² + 18y + 81 + y² - 585 = 0

2y² + 18y - 504 = 0

where, a = 2, b = 18, c = - 504

Solve using quadratic formula:

y = ( -b +- √b² - 4ac) / 2a

y = [-18 + √18² - 4(2)(504)] /2(2)

y = 12

To solve for x, plug in the value of y which is 12 to the 3rd equation:

x = y + 9

x = 12 + 9

x = 21

To check:

* Two numbers differ by 9.

21 - 12 = 9 ✓

*Sum of their squares is 585

12² + 21² = 585 ✓
