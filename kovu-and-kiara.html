14. how can we apply the division of decimals in real life? a sample of application is when we go out and buy some items in the grocery store solve this problem for the last four weeks kiara's mother recorded the grocery receipts as follows: Php1,427.00; Php1,805.75; Php1,753.25 and 1,263.50. what was the average bill per week


Answer:

Php1562.375 or if you round it off, Php1562.38 (Depends on what your teacher prefers)

Step-by-step explanation:

First, add all of the grocery receipts:

Php1,427 + Php1,805.75 + Php1,753.25 + Php1,263.50 = Php6,249.5

Then to get the average, you must divide Php6,249.5 by 4 (divide it on how many numbers were added)

Php6,249.5 / 4

= Php1562.375 is average bill per week.

( / is another sign for division)
