1. Me SMITH Had 4 daughter each daughter had a brother...how many children does Mr SMITH have?


Answer:

Mr. Smith has 5 children.

Explanation:

Logic

The term "logic" came from the Greek word logos, which is sometimes translated as sentence, discourse or reason. Logic can be defined as the study of the principles of correct reasoning since it involves reasoning and logical thinking skills.

Going back to the given logic above, let us analyze each points.

Mr. Smith had 4 daughter.

It is clear that he has 4 children already.

Each daughter had a brother.

This sentence tells us that his daughters have a brother and since they are siblings, they have the same brother. So aside from the 4 daughters that he has, he has a son.

4 (daughters) + 1 (son) = 5 (children)

Therefore, Mr. Smith has 5 children.
