c

Explanation:

Milliliters is a volume unit and grams is a mass unit. ... One milliliter of water and one milliliter of air take up the same amount of space. On the other hand, mass is the amount of matter. You can make an object smaller to change its volume, but this will not change its mass.

25. Direction: Convert the following metric system of measurement.1. 15 ml =______ teaspoon2. 2 L =______ cups3. 45 ml =______ tablespoon4. 90 grams =______ ounce5. 3 kg =______ pound​


Answer:

1. 15mL = ____ teaspoon

1mL = 0.2029 teaspoon (divide by 4.929)

15 × 0.2029 (15 ÷ 4.929)

= 3.04 teaspoon

2. 2L = ____ cups

1L = 4.227 cups

2 × 4.227

= 8.454 cups

3. 45mL = ____ tablespoon

1mL = 0.0676 tablespoon (divide by 14.787)

45 × 0.0676 (45 ÷ 14.787)

= 3.04 tablespoon

4. 90grams = ____ ounce

1gram = 0.0353 ounce (divide by 28.35)

90 × 0.0353 (90 ÷ 28.35)

= 3.18 or 3.17 ounce

5. 3kg = ____ pound

1kg = 2.205 pound

3 × 2.205

= 6.615 pound
