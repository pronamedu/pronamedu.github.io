3. _______ 1. Like a torch, you are bringer of light, Ma’am a. simile b. metaphor c. personification​


Answer:

A.

Explanation:

Answer:A. Simile

Explanation:

Simile - it is a figure of speech that uses the words "like" and "as" to compare two things that are not related to each other.

Example: She is as pretty as a flower.

He is like a lion.

Metaphor - it is a figure of speech that directly compare two things that are not really related to each other.

Example: Maria is the brightest star tonight.

Love is a war.

Personification - this is a figure of speech that attributes human characteristics to things that are non-human.

Example: Annika is like a walking dictionary.

The skies are crying.
