29. SEVEN SUBTRACTED FROM A NUMBER HALF OF A NUMBER


Answer: 14/3


Step-by-step explanation:

Step 1. Equate

(7-n) = n/2

Step 2. Separate the constant and the n

2(7-n) = n

14-2n = n

14 = n + 2n

Step 3. Add the like terms.

14 = 3n

Step 4. Simplify by dividing 3 on 14

14/3 = n

Or

4.67
