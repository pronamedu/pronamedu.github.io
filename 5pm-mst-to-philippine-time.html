29. In OM below, diameter IT bisects chord CH. If mIT = 40 and mST = 8, find the following:​

  mSM = 20 - 8

  mSM = 12 units

2. mSH

(mSH)^2 = 20^2 - 12^2

(mSH)^2 = 400 - 144

(mSH)^2 = 256

mSH = [tex]\sqrt 256[/tex]

mSH = 16 units

3. mCH

mIT = mST + mCH

40 = 8 + mCH

mCH = 40 - 8

mCH = 32 units
