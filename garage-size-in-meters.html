16. The front of a garage needs to be painted except for the gate. The measurement of the garage is 5 meters long and 2 meters wide. The gate has a dimension of 1.5 meters by 2 meters. How many square meters will be painted? * 1 point A. 16.75 m² B. 10.75 m² C. 9.25 m² D. 6.75 m²


AREA OF RECTANGLE

Problem: The front of a garage needs to be painted except for the gate. The measurement of the garage is 5 meters long and 2 meters wide. The gate has a dimension of 1.5 meters by 2 meters. How many square meters will be painted?

Answer: The area of the front of the garage that will be painted is 7 square meters.

Solution:

The solution for this word problem is simply solving for the area of the two surfaces and then get their difference. Since, by assumption, the shape of the gate and front of the garage is rectangle. The formula in order to calculate the area of a rectangle is simply the product of its base and height or in mathematical equation: A = bh

where A refers to the area of the rectangle, b refers to its base and h refers to its height, as shown here: https://brainly.ph/question/110308.

To solve for the entire area of the front of garage including the gate:

A = 5(2) = 10 square meters

To solve for the area of the gate:

A = 1.5(2) = 3 square meters

Since the gate will be excluded, let us subtract the two areas:

A = 10 - 3 = 7 square meters

Therefore, the area to be painted is equal to 7 sq. meters.
