18. Tula tungkol sa Klima ng asya​


Answer & Explanation:

THE TYPES OF THE CLIME OF ASIA

• CLIMA - refers to the state of atmosphere of a nation for a long time e.g. rainy season, summer, winter, summer and spring

• TIME - refers to an area's atmospheres at the scheduled time.

1. IMPORTANT ASIA Central Continental. Winter is long which typically lasts six months, and summer is short, but there are some places that possess fertile soil. However, a large part of the region cannot afford human residence due to too cold.

2. WEST ASIA Not always the weather. This place may have excessive or less heat or cold. A large part of the region rarely and almost never gets rain. If it rains, it usually falls in places near the sea.

3. TIMOG ASIA Climate is different in a year. Humidity June to September, winter months of December to February, and March to May, summer and drought. The Himalayas and other parts of the region remain cold due to snow or ice.

4. THEY ASYA Monsoon Climate is the region's kind of climate. Because of the extent of this region, countries are experiencing different weather-hot weather for countries that are in low latitude, cold and ice-covered in some parts of the region.

5. TIMOG - ASYA, almost all countries in the region have tropical climate, experiencing summer, winter, summer and rainy season.

Two (2) Types of Monsoon

Southwest wind - Southwest southwest monsoon, coming from the ocean to the continent.. It comes from the south west. Typically heavy pouring rainfall that causes flooding when southwest.

Amihan wind - cool wind from the southwest carrying storm. Amihan Air or Northeast Monsoon coming from Siberia to Ocean
