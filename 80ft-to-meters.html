3. 13. A man with 160ft. of fencing materials wishes to fence off an area in the shape ofa rectangle. What should be the dimensions of the area if the enclosed space isto be as large as possible?a. 20ft by 20ftc. 40ft, by 40ftb. 30ft. by 30ft.d. 80ft. by 80ft.​


Answer:

D

Step-by-step explanation:

Dko dn alam kumopya Lang ako :)

The Total Length of Fencing Material is 160 ft.

P = 160
2x+2y = 160
x+y = 80

A = xy
A = x(80-x)
A = 80x-x²
A' = 80-2x
2x = 80
x = 40

x+y = 80
40+y = 80
y = 40
∴ the dimensions are 40 by 40

Another approach is Using AM-GM Inequality

2x+2y = 160
x+y = 80

(x+y)/2 ≥ √(ab)
80/2 ≥ √(ab)
40 ≥ √(ab)
(40)² ≥ ab
(40)(40) ≥ ab
∴
a = 40
b = 40

∴ The dimensions of the area if the enclosed space is to be large as possible is 40 ft. by 40 ft.
