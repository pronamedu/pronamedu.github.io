Howlong was the flight?a. 10 hrs & 40 min. b. 10 hrs & 50 min.C. 8 hrs & 10 min. d. 9 hrs & 20 min.SHOW YOUR SOLUTION Nonsense=Report​


Answer:

D. 9 hrs & 20 min.

Step-by-step explanation:

If the flight was delayed and the plane took off 1 hour and 30 minutes later, then the plane took off at 6:15 + 1:30 = 7:45 A.M

Now, we will convert the time to minutes so it will be easier to solve.

7:45 means 7 hours and 45 minutes. An hour consists of 60 minutes, therefore 7:45 is 7 × 60 + 45 = 465 minutes.

She arrived at the Korea at  5:05 P.M ⇒ 17 × 60 + 5 = 1025 minutes (since 5:00 P.M is 17:00 in 24-hour time)

Therefore the flight is 1025 - 465 = 560 minutes = 9 hours & 20 min. long.
