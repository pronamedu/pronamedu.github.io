15. Define anime/manga and why?


Answer:

anime is the one that you watch and maga is the one that you read

Explanation:

there you go

Answer and Explanation:

Anime is a term referring to the cartoon animations of the Japanese Culture. Though the term anime on a global scale refers to Japanese animation, the Japanese use the term anime to refer to any animations globally.

Manga or Manhwa refers to the comics of Japanese Culture. It was created after World War 2 as a countermeasure to the growing depression of Japanese people following the Nuclear Bombing of Hiroshima and Nagasak, and the collapse of the Imperial Japan economy. The first manga ever to be serialized was Machiko Hasegawa's Sazae-san which began in 1946 and Osamu Tezuka's Mighty Atom.

Other Facts:

Both Anime and Manga are quite popular worldwide and have a distinct style in the design of characters, animations, and stories. Anime and Manwha characters are influenced by the art movement of Art Nouveau.A person who is a hardcore fan of anime and/or manga/manhwa are called "Otaku".
