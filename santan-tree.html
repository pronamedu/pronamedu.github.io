3/4 x 2/3

= 1/2

1/2 + 2/3

21. A homozygous red santan flower (rr) is crossed with a homozygous pink santan flower (rr).


Answer:

All red flowered

The crossing of a homozygous red flowered plant (RR) with a homozygous white flowered plant (rr), will result in the production of flowers containing the genotype of the offspring Rr.

∙The allele R is the dominant allele whereas r is the recessive allele for the color of the flower.

∙Therefore, the offspring containing the genotype (Rr) will be red in colour.
