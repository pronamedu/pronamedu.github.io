23. A 25-kg bag of special rice costs 712.50.Mrs. Leynes buys 5 kg of his rice.How much will she pay?


BUYING A BAG OF RICE

If Mrs. Leynes buys 5 kilograms of rice from a 25-kg bag of special rice that is worth 712.5, she will pay 142.5.

How did we solve the answer?

First, we have to analyze the given.We are given the fact that there is a 25-kg bag of special rice worth 712.50.Then, Mrs. Leynes buys 5 kilograms of it.We first have to calculate the price per kilogram of the special rice. Then, we can now identify how much will she have to pay if she wants to buy 5 kilograms of it.

Solving:

Price Per Kilogram = [tex]\frac{712.50}{25}[/tex] = 28.5

Price @5 kg = 28.5 x 5 = 142. 5
