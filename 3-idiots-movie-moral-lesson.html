13. 3 idiots (Movie) : Compare and contrast of characters Need 2 examples help​


Answer:

One way in which these characters can be compared and contrasted is in terms of their personalities and approaches to life. Farhan is a more studious and conventional character who is initially focused on achieving good grades and getting a good job. In contrast, Raju is more laid-back and impulsive, and he is more interested in having fun and enjoying life than in achieving traditional markers of success. Rancho, on the other hand, is a free-spirited and unconventional character who is more interested in pursuing his own interests and passions than in conforming to societal expectations.

Another way in which these characters can be compared and contrasted is in terms of their relationships with their families and their priorities. Farhan is initially conflicted about his relationship with his father, who is pressuring him to become an engineer, while Raju is more distant from his family and is struggling to find his place in the world. Rancho, on the other hand, has a close relationship with his family and is able to balance his own interests with his responsibilities to his loved ones.

Overall, Farhan, Raju, and Rancho are distinct and complex characters who each bring their own unique perspectives and experiences to the story.
