7. The cost of the cup of coffee, a sandwich, and fries is p49. the sandwich costs twice as much as the coffee. the fries costs p17 more than the coffee. what is the cost of the coffee?


Answer:

The cost of the coffee is ₱8.

Explanation:

Let:

x + 17 = cost of the fries

2x = cost of the sandwich

x = cost of the coffee

x + 17 + 2x + x = 49

4x + 17 = 49

4x = 49 - 17

4x = 32

x = ₱8 <= cost of the coffee

PRODUCT, PROCESS AND TOOL/GADGETS

What is a product?

A product refers to something that is grown, produced or made to be used or sold or something that is the result of a particular process.

What is a process?

Refers to a collective or series of action that leads to a certain result or that produces something.

What is a gadget?

A gadget refers to a tiny electrical or mechanical object and device with a particular purpose or use.

What is a tool?

Something that is driven or manipulated by others. To drive or ride in a vehicle.
