1. Do you know how to dress a chicken​


1. Start by plucking the feathers: Dip the chicken in hot water to loosen the feathers, then pluck them out by hand or using a plucking machine.

2. Remove the head: Cut off the chicken's head with a sharp knife.

3. Remove the feet: Cut off the feet at the joint where they meet the legs.

4. Cut open the abdomen: Make a cut along the chicken's breastbone and use your hands to open up the body cavity.

5. Remove the internal organs: Reach into the body cavity and pull out the internal organs, including the heart, lungs, liver, and intestines. Be sure to remove the gallbladder carefully, as it contains bile that can ruin the meat.

6. Clean the chicken: Rinse the chicken inside and out with cold water to remove any remaining debris or blood.

7. Chill the chicken: Store the chicken in the refrigerator or freezer until ready to cook.

Note: Dressing a chicken can be a messy process, and it's important to follow safe food handling practices to prevent the spread of bacteria. Always wash your hands thoroughly before and after handling raw chicken, and clean any tools or surfaces that come into contact with the chicken.


2. the price of a dressed chicken per kilo is php 220.00. you money on hand is only 60% of it. how much do you have ?​


Answer:

Php 132.00

Step-by-step explanation:

You can use the principle of Percentage, Base, Rate calculations,

P = B * R

P = ?

B = 220

R = 60% = 0.60

Solve for P

P = 220 * 0.60

P = 132 pesos

You only have Php 132.00.

Addon answer:

Php 220.00 - Php  132.00 =  Php 68.00

You need Php 68.00 more to be able to buy a kilo of chicken.
