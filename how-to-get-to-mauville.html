9. How to get base by rate and percentage How to get percentage by rate and base How to get rate by percentage and base Help me pls Also rounding off numbers How?


Formulas:

Base = Percentage/Rate

Percentage = Base x Rate

Rate = Percentage/Base

To round off numbers, identify first the rounding digit. Then, look at the number at its right. If the number beside it is 5 and above, round up by adding 1 to the rounding digit and replace the remaining numbers to its right to 0. If it is 4 and below, round down. The rounding digit will remain the same but the numbers to its right will be replaced by 0.

Example:

225 - rounded off to nearest hundreds

Rounding digit = 2

Number next to it = 2

Round down.

Rounded number = 200
