13. alisa had 1/2 liter of juice in a bottle she drank 3/8 liters of juice what fraction of juice in the bottle did Alisa drink


Answer:

The fraction of juice in the bottle that Alisa drank is ‌3⁄16‌.

Step-by-step explanation:

Let us analyze the given problem.

What is asked?

The fraction of juice in the bottle that Alisa drank.

What are the given?

amount of juice in a bottle - ½ liter

amount of juice Alisa drank - ⅜ liter

What is the number sentence?

To know what fraction of the juice in a bottle Alisa drank, we need to find the ⅜ of ½. To find a fraction of a fraction, we need to multiply. Just change the word "of" to multiplication sign (×).

⅜ × ½ = N

What is the solution?

⅜ × ½ = N

⅜ × ½ = ‌3⁄16‌

Final Answer:

The fraction of juice in the bottle that Alisa drank is ‌3⁄16‌.

There are three steps to multiply fractions.

Simplifying Fractions

Simplifying means reducing or to make the fraction as simple as possible.

How to simplify fractions?

We can simplify fractions by using the greatest common factor or GCF.

Steps in Simplifying Fractions:

Find the GCF.Divide the numerator by the GCF.Divide the denominator by the GCF.

Example:

Simplify 6⁄20‌.

Step #1 (List their factors, then choose the greatest common factor that they have.)

6 - 1, 2, 3, 6

20 - 1, 2, 4, 5, 10

Step #2

Step #3

20 ÷ 2 = 10

18. Alisa had 1/2 liter of juice in a bottle. She drank 3/8 liters of juice. What fraction of the juice in the bottle did Alisa drink


Answer

To find the fraction of the juice in the bottle that Alisa drank, you need to divide the amount of juice she drank by the total amount of juice in the bottle. In this case, Alisa drank 3/8 liters of juice and there was 1/2 liter of juice in the bottle, so the fraction of the juice in the bottle that she drank is 3/8 / 1/2 = (3/8) * (2/1) = 6/8 = 3/4. Therefore, Alisa drank 3/4 of the juice in the bottle.
