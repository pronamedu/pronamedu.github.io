1. A family wants to construct a house with a perimeter of 90 meters and an area of 500 square meters. What are the dimensions of the house? Show the solutions please.


Step-by-step explanation:

Let x be the width.

Perimeter= 2L +2W

2L + 2X= 90

2L = 90 - 2X

L = 45 - X

X ( 45 - X) = 500

45X - X^2 = 500

X^2 - 45X = - 500

X^2 - 45X +500 = 0

(X - 25)(X - 20) = 0

X = 25 and X = 20

If we choose X= 25

The dimensions are:

W= 25 m

L= 45 - 25

L = 20 m

The other way around will be the same answer. Therefore, the dimensions are 25m and 20m.


2. Kurt had a goal of finishing painting at least 80% of the 500 square meters of a house’s walls by today. He actually finished painting 415 square meters today. Did Kurt meet his goal?


Step-by-step explanation:

Total Area to be painted = 500 square meters

Total Area Finished Painting = 415 square meters

Percentile Goal to achieve painting = 80%

We need to find whether he achieved his goal or not.

Area to be covered to achieve goal can be calculated by Percentile Goal to achieve painting multiplied by Total Area to be painted and then divide by 100.

framing in equation form we get;

Area to be covered to achieve goal =

[tex] \frac{80}{100} \times 500 = 400 {m}^{2} [/tex]

Since Total Area which has been Finished in Painting is greater than Area to be covered to achieve goal.

Hence We can say Kurt has met his goal of painting house's walls.

3. Ayden had a goal of finishing painting at least 80% of the 500 square meters of a house's walls by today. He actually finished painting 415 square meters today.Did Ayden meet his goal? Why?


Answer:

The answer is yes. Ayden had met his goal of painting at least 80% of the 500 square meters.

Step-by-step explanation:

In order to find out if Ayden had painted at least 80% of the 500 square meters of the house' walls, multiply 80% by 500, thus, 80%(500). Change 80 % to decimal before multiplying it to 500 then 80 % = .80, .80(500) = 400 square meters. Since it is stated in the problem that Ayden had painted 415 square meters already which is greater than the exact 80% of 500 square meters which is 400 square meters. Therefore, it is just right to say that he had achieved his goal.
