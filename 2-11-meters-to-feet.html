[tex]\huge\color{light}\colorbox{lightpink}{DEFINITIONS}[/tex]

SPEAKER

A speaker is someone who delivers a speech or a device that makes sounds louder.

LINES

A line is made of a set of points which is extended in opposite directions infinite.

COUPLET

A couplet is two lines of poetry that usually rhyme.

TERCET

A poetic unit of three lines, rhymed or unrhymed.

QUATRAIN

A piece of verse complete in four rhymed lines.

RHYTHM

A "movement marked by the regulated succession of strong and weak elements, or of opposite or different conditions".

METER

Metre or meter is the basic rhythmic structure of a verse or lines in verse.

RHYME

The repetition of syllables, typically at the end of a line.

FOOT

A basic repeated sequence of meter composed of two or more accented or unaccented syllables.

IMAGERY
