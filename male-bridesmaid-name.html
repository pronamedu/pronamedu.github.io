14. janice is a bridesmaid for her cousin's wedding in new delhi the wedding is scheduled at 6:00 p.m. on a saturday jenny's flight leaves naia at 1:00 p.m. saturday afternoon and is scheduled to take 6 hours if the time in manila is 2.5 hours and of new delhi the assuming it will take her an extra are to get to the ceremony location from the airport and another are to get ready wilderness be ready for the wedding on time?​


Answer:

She will not arrive for the wedding on time since she will will arrive at 6:30 p.m. (New Delhi time) which is 30 minutes after the scheduled wedding.

Step-by-step explanation:

Start with the initial time which is 1:00 p.m. Then count the number of hours it will take to reach the airport (New Delhi) from Naia (Manila)(6 hours) which is 7:00 p.m.

                (6 hours)

1:00 p.m. ---------------------> 7:00 p.m.

It will take her an extra hour to get to the ceremony location from the airport. From, we now have to count an hour from the time of arrival which will give us 8:00 p.m.

                  (1 hour)

7:00 p.m. ---------------------> 8:00 p.m.

It will take her another hour to get ready for the ceremony location. From, we now have to count an hour from the time of arrival to the wedding location which will give us 9:00 p.m.

                  (1 hour)

8:00 p.m. ---------------------> 9:00 p.m.

Since there is a 2.5 hr-time difference between Manila and New Delhi, the final step is to just take away the time difference from 9:00 p.m.

9:00 p.m. - 2 hours and 30 minutes = 6:30 p.m.
