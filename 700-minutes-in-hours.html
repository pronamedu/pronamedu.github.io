5. Alliyah and Abigail together can type 90 words per minute. How fast can Abigail type 700 words if Aliyah can type 300 words in 4 minutes?


Answer:

Alliyah and Abigail together can type 90 words per minute. How fast can Abigail type 700 words if Aliyah can type 300 words in 4 minutes?

~~~~~~~~~~~

Together they type 90 words per minute.

Individually, Aliyah can type 300 words in 4 minutes, or 300/4 = 75 words per minute.

Hence, Abigail types 90-75 = 15 words per minute.

To type 700 words, Abigail needs 700%2F15 = 46 2%2F3 minutes, or 46 minutes and 40 seconds.

9. A bus travels 150 km in 5 hours. what is the time taken by the bus to travel 700 km


✏️SPEED

[tex]\red{••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••} [/tex]

[tex] \underline{\mathbb{PROBLEM}:} [/tex]

A bus travels 150 km in 5 hours. what is the time taken by the bus to travel 700 km.

[tex]\red{••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••} [/tex]

[tex] \underline{\mathbb{ANSWER}:} [/tex]

[tex] \qquad\LARGE » \:\tt\purple{23 \frac{1}{3} \: hours} [/tex]

[tex]\red{••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••} [/tex]

[tex] \underline{\mathbb{SOLUTION}:} [/tex] Determine the bus' speed if it travels 150 km in 5 hours using the speed formula.

[tex] \begin{align} & \bold{Formula:} \\ & \boxed{\green{Speed(S) = \frac{distance(d)}{time(t)}}} \end{align} [/tex]

[tex] S = \frac{150 \, km}{5 \, hrs.} \\ [/tex]

[tex] S = 30 \, kmph [/tex]

» Now determine the time it takes when it travels 700 km with the speed of 30 kmph.

[tex] 30 \, kmph = \frac{700 \, km}{t} \\ [/tex]

[tex] (30 \, kmph)t = 700 \, km \\ [/tex]

[tex] \frac{(\cancel{30 \, kmph})t}{\cancel{30 \, kmph}} = \frac{700 \, km}{30 \, kmph} \\ [/tex]

[tex] t = \frac{70}{3} \, hrs. \\ [/tex]

[tex] t = 23\frac{1}{3} \, hrs. [/tex]

[tex] \therefore [/tex] The time taken by the bus to travel 700 km with the speed of 30 kmph is 23⅓ hours.

[tex]\red{••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••} [/tex]

27. An airplane flies with a constant speed of 700 miles per hour. How long will it take to travel a distance of 1050 miles?​


Answer:

Step-by-step explanation:

time = distance / speed

Substituting the given values, we get:

time = 1050 miles / 700 miles per hour

Simplifying, we get:

time = 1.5 hours

Therefore, it will take the airplane 1.5 hours to travel a distance of 1050 miles at a constant speed of 700 miles per hour.
