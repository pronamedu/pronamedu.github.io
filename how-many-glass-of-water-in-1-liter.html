7. d. 3 450 000 m B. Solve the following: 1. Elisse has 20 meters of ribbon. How many 15 cm of ribbon can be cut from it? 2. Drinking 8 glasses of water a day is good for your health. If each glass of water is equivalent to 148 mL, how many liters of water you need to consume everyday? 3. Your mother asked you to buy 2.5 kg of sugar. Each pack of sugar in the store weighs 250 grams. How many packs of sugar will you buy? ​


Answer:

1. 133 (15cm) can be cut from this

2. 1.184 L

3. 10 packs

Explanation:

1. 1m = 100cm. For 20m to be divided by 15cm, we need to convert 20m to centimeters for it to have the same unit.

20m x 100cm = 2,000cm

2,000cm / 15cm = 133.3cm

Therefore, we can only cut 133cm from a 20m ribbon.

2. 1L = 1,000mL

148 mL x 8 glasses = 1,184mL

In order to get the number of liters, divide 1,184mL by 1,000.

1,184ml / 1000ml = 1.184

Therefore, 1.184L liters of water you need to consume everyday.

3. 1kg = 1,000g

2.5 kg x 1000g = 2,500g

Then, divide 2,500g by 250g to get the number of packs.

2,500 g / 250g = 10 packs

Therefore, you need to buy 10 packs of sugar.
