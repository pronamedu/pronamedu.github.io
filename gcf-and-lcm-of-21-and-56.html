Answer:

What is the GCF of 21 and 56?

The GCF of 21 and 56 is 7.

What is the LCM of 21 and 56?

The LCM of 21 and 56 is 168.

Step-by-step explanation:

Steps to find GCF

Find the prime factorization of 21

21 = 3 × 7

Find the prime factorization of 56

56 = 2 × 2 × 2 × 7

To find the GCF, multiply all the prime factors common to both numbers:

Therefore, GCF = 7

Steps to find LCM

Find the prime factorization of 21

21 = 3 × 7

Find the prime factorization of 56

56 = 2 × 2 × 2 × 7

Multiply each factor the greater number of times it occurs in steps or above to find the LCM:

LCM = 2 × 2 × 2 × 3 × 7

LCM = 168
