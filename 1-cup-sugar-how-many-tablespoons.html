3. 5. A recipe calls for 5 cups of flour to 7 tablespoons of sugar. How many cups of flour and how many tablespoons of sugar will be needed for 4 recipes?​


Step-by-step explanation:

To find out how much flour and sugar is needed for 4 recipes, we first need to calculate how much is needed for one recipe and then multiply that by 4.

For one recipe, we need 5 cups of flour to 7 tablespoons of sugar.

So for 4 recipes, we have:

Flour: 5 cups x 4 = 20 cups

Sugar: 7 tablespoons x 4 = 28 tablespoons

However, it's common to measure ingredients in cups and tablespoons, so we need to convert the tablespoons of sugar to cups.

There are 16 tablespoons in one cup, so we have:

28 tablespoons ÷ 16 tablespoons/cup = 1.75 cups

Therefore, we need 20 cups of flour and 1.75 cups of sugar for 4 recipes
