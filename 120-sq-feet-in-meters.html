9. What is the width of an auditorium whose area is 1 120 sq. meters and length of 35 meters?


The area of a rectangle is equal to:
[tex]Area=length*width[/tex]
So we substitute the values given:
[tex]35*width=1120 \\ width= \frac{1120}{35} =32m[/tex]
The formula for the area of the rectangle:
A=lw

Substitute with the given measurements:
1,120 m²=35 m(w)
Divide both sides by 35 m
w=32 m
