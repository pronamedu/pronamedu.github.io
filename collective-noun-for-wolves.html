27. wolves have supernatural power​


Hello! There is no scientific evidence to back up the belief that wolves possess supernatural powers. Wolves are a canid species recognized for their intelligence, social behavior, and hunting skills. They do not, however, have any supernatural talents or capabilities other than what is naturally linked to their physical and behavioral adaptations. The belief that wolves have magical powers is prevalent in mythology, folklore, and popular culture, although it is not supported by scientific evidence.
