1. what s doodle art? how to make/draw doodle art?


Answer:

Doodle art

A doodle is an unfocused drawing made while a person's attention is otherwise occupied. Doodles are simple drawings that can have concrete representational meaning or may just be abstract shapes. ... Doodle art is sometimes capable of reassuring its creator.

The best way to do this is to pull out a blank piece of paper, set a timer for 10-minutes, and start free doodling. Don't overthink it or set any expectations during this time; just draw. Draw shapes, words, symbols, etc., and don't put your pen down for those 10-minutes. See things as basic shapes
