29. I have a house that has a base of 130 square feet and a height of 20 feet. What is the volume?


Answer:

The answer is the HOUSE has a volume of 1,980 cubic feet.

Step-by-step explanation:

Answer: To calculate the volume of a house with a given base and height, you can use the formula:

Volume = base area x height

In this case, the base area is given as 130 square feet and the height is 20 feet.

So, the volume of the house is:

Volume = 130 sq ft x 20 ft

Volume = 2,600 cubic feet

Therefore, the volume of the house is 2,600 cubic feet.

Step-by-step explanation: Here are the steps to calculate the volume of the house:

Identify the given values: The problem gives you the base area (130 square feet) and the height (20 feet) of the house.

Recall the formula: To calculate the volume of a house, you need to use the formula Volume = base area x height.

Plug in the values: Substitute the given values into the formula. That gives you:

Volume = 130 sq ft x 20 ft

Simplify: To get the final answer, multiply the two values:

Volume = 2,600 cubic feet

So, the volume of the house is 2,600 cubic feet.

How many trips will it take for the truck to deliver 945 cubic feet of sand? pasagot ng maayos please


Answer :

1.

To find the volume of a rectangular prism, multiply its 3 dimensions: length x width x height. The volume is expressed in cubic units. The formula for the volume of a rectangular prism is, Volume (V) = base area × height of the prism. Another way to express this formula is, Volume = l × w × h.

Given:

130cm length, 70cm width and 110 cm height

Solution:

V = L × W × HV = 130 × 70 × 110V = 1,001,000 cm^3

Hence it needs to fill the jar with 1,001,000 cubic centimeters volume of Water.

2.

The problem also in no. 2 is a rectangular prism, hence the formula for finding the volume of a rectangular prism is V = L × W × H

Given:

90 cm length, 70cm height and 40cm width

Solution:

V = L × W × HV = 90 × 70 × 40V = 252,000 cm^3

Hence the volume of water inside the Aquarium is 252,000 cubic centimeters.

3.

Formula of finding the volume of a Rectangular Prism

V = L × W × H

Given:

9 ft, 7 ft, 5 ft

Solution:

V = L × W × HV = 9 × 7 × 5V = 315

Hence the Volume of the Truck is 315 cubic feet.

But, the question is : How many trips will it take for the truck to deliver 945 cubic feet of sand?

But a Truck Container is only 315 ft^3.
