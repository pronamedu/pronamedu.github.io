1. QuestI need to fence a plot of dimensions 3m by 5m with 5 (five) turns of barbed wire. each roll of this wire has 20. I will need 2 rolls3 rolls4 rolls 5 rolls​


Answer:

4 rolls

Step-by-step explanation:

We have a rectangular plot of length C = 5 meters and width L = 3 meters.

As it will be surrounded by wire, we need to calculate the perimeter of the rectangular plot

The perimeter is calculated by P = 2.(C + L)

P = 2.(5 + 3)

P = 2.(8)

P = 16 meters

As it will be done 5 turns, we will spend wire: 5 x 16 = 80 meters

Knowing that each roll of wire is 20 meters long, we will use: 80/20 = 8/2 = 4 rolls of wire.

Answer: 4 rolls

Find the perimeter of Georgia's square garden.Convert its perimeter in feet to meters.Multiply its perimeter by 2 since there are two rows of barb wire. Multiply the barb wire by Php 12.

Step 1: Perimeter of the Garden

Perimeter of Square = 4s

= 4 (20 feet)

= 80 feet

Step 2: Convert Feet to Meters

1 feet = 0.3048 m

80 × 0.3048 = 24.384 m

Step 3: Total length of the barb wire Georgia needs

24.384 m × 2 = 48.768 m

Step 4: Round off to the nearest hundredths

48.768 × Php 12 = Php 585.216 or Php 585.22

Xian needs 56 meters of barbed wire to enclose the square field.

Area = s²

s² = 196 m²

s = √(196 m²)

s = 14 meters

Find the perimeter of the square field to determine the length of barbed wire needed to enclose the field:

Perimeter = 4(side)

Perimeter = 4 (14 m)

Perimeter = 56 meters

20. Jonna wants to fence her square garden, which has a side of 25 feet with three rows of barb wire. The store sold barb wire by meter at Php 15. 00/ meter. How much money will Jonna need to buy the barbwire she needs?.


Jonna needs to fence a square garden with a side of 25 feet and three rows of barb wire. Since a square has four sides, Jonna will need a total of 4 x 25 = <<4*25=100>>100 feet of barb wire.

Since each row of barb wire is 25 feet long, Jonna will need a total of 25 x 3 = <<25*3=75>>75 feet of barb wire for the three rows.

Therefore, Jonna will need a total of 100 + 75 = <<100+75=175>>175 feet of barb wire.

Since 1 meter is equal to 3.28 feet, Jonna will need 175 / 3.28 = <<175/3.28=53.125>>53.125 meters of barb wire.

At Php 15.00 per meter, Jonna will need to pay a total of 53.125 x 15 = Php 796.88 for the barb wire she needs. Answer: \boxed{796.88}.

28. Jonna wants to fence her square garden, which has a side of 25 feet with three rows of barb wire. The store sold barb wire by meter at Php 15.00/ meter. How much money will Jonna need to buy the barb wire she needs?​


Answer:

P= 4S

P= 4(25) ft

P= 100 ft

Step 2: Convert feet into meters.

100 ft × (1m/0.3048 ft)

=328.08 m

Step 3: Multiply the answer in step 2 by 3 since three rows of barb wire is required.

328.08 m × 3 = 984.24 m

Step 4: Multiply the answer in step 3 by 15 since the barb wire is 15 pesos per meter.

984.24m × Php 15.00 = Php 14763.6 / m
