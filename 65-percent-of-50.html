EXPLAINThe SNV is z = (x — 60) /6

When x = 50, z = ( 50 — 60) /6 = — 1.67

When x= 75, z = (75 — 60)/ 6 = 2.5

The area under the standard normal curve between z =— 1.67 and 2.5 is

= 0.4525 + 0.4938

= 0.9463
