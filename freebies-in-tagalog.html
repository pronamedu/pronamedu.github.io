19. A newly opened store is giving freebies to its customers. Even6th customer receives a ball pen and every 8th customerreceives an ecobag. How many customers gof both items ionly the first 60 customers were given freebies?​


Answer:

Two customers

Explanation:

The common multiples of 6 and 8 will determine how many times a customer will get both freebies.

LCM of 6: 6, 12, 18, 24, 30, 36, 42, 48, 54, 60, 66, 72, 78, 84, 90, ...

LCM of 8: 8, 16, 24, 32, 40, 48, 56, 64, 72, 80, 88, 96, 104, 112, 120, ...

There are two common multiples appeared before 60. Therefore, there will be two customers who will receive both a ball pen and an ecobag and it will be the 24th and 48th customer.
