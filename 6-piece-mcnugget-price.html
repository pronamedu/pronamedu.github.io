17. each price of candy cost php 2.00 the price of x pieces of candy is php 36.00.​


Answer:

[tex]\huge\color{purple}{\boxed{{\colorbox{black}{Question}}}}[/tex]

each price of candy cost php 2.00 the price of x pieces of candy is php 36.00.

[tex]\huge\color{purple}{\boxed{{\colorbox{black}{ANSWER}}}}[/tex]

We can use algebra to solve for the value of x:

Let x be the number of pieces of candy.

Let p be the price of each piece of candy, which we know is PHP 2.00.

Then the total price of x pieces of candy is:

Total Price = Number of Pieces of Candy * Price per Piece

Total Price = x * p

Total Price = x * 2.00

We also know that the total price of x pieces of candy is PHP 36.00, so we can set up an equation:

x * 2.00 = 36.00

Solving for x:

x = 36.00 / 2.00

x = 18

Therefore, the number of pieces of candy is 18
