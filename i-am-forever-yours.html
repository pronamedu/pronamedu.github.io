5. make an interpretation from the speech below."I am a filipino born to freedom, and i shall not rest until freedom shall have been added unto my inheritance for myself and my children and my children's children-forever"​


Answer:

The speaker, who is a Filipino, asserts his/her commitment to freedom and declares that he/she will not stop fighting for it until it is fully realized and secured for himself/herself, his/her children, and his/her future generations. The use of the phrase "born to freedom" suggests that the speaker sees freedom as an inherent right that is integral to his/her identity as a Filipino. The emphasis on the continuity of this inheritance "for my children and my children's children" suggests a long-term vision for the future that is deeply rooted in the speaker's values and beliefs. Overall, the speech expresses a strong sense of determination and a deep commitment to the ideal of freedom.
