Complementary Angles

Two angles that have measurements that add up to 90°  are complementary.

How to Solve for the Complement of An Angle

Subtracting the given angle from 90° will give the difference that is also the complement of the said angle.

Example

1) What is the complement of 25°?

90° - 25° = 65°65° is the complement of 25°

Proof

25° + 65° = 90°

2) What is the complement of 18°?

90° - 18° = 72°72° is the complement of 18°

Proof

18° + 72° = 90°
