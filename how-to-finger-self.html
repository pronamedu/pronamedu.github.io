20. a girl uses her finger in pushing the wall and exerts a 50N force? How much FORCE is exerted by the wall to the finger?


#BrainlyFast

The force exerted by the wall to the finger will also be 50 N force but in opposite direction. The third law of motion " For every action, there will always be an equal and opposite reaction". The girl pushes 50 N force and as the opposite reaction, the wall also exerted 50 N force in opposite direction to the force applied by the girl's finger.

In Newton's Third Law of motion, It states that for every action, there should be an equal and opposite reaction. Example: when we walk, we pushed our feet on the ground and the opposite will happened in doing this, and that is we move forward. The same with jumping, you pushed your legs to the ground, and the was opposite, you move upward.

The amount of force you pushed to the floor, will always have equal opposite reaction. You jump law when you pushed the floor less during jumping, and you jump high when you pushed the floor with higher force.
