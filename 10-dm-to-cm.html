6. How many jewelry boxes 10 cm by 6m by 4 cm can be packed into a box 50 dm long, 4 dm wide and 3 dm high?


Answer:

2500 jewelry boxes

Step-by-step explanation:

1dm = 10cm

times 10 to find cm

[tex]50dm \times 10 = 500cm[/tex]

[tex]4dm \times 10 = 40cm[/tex]

[tex]3dm \times 10 = 30cm[/tex]

Volume of jewelry box = 240cm³

[tex]10 \times 6 \times 4 = 240[/tex]

Volume of the box = 600000cm³

[tex]500 \times 40 \times 30 = 600000[/tex]

Then, divide the volume of the boxes to find how many jewelry box fitted inside.

[tex]600000 \div 240 = 2500[/tex]

So, 2500 jewelry boxes can be packed inside the box.
