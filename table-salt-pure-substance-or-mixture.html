Sodium chloride (table salt) dissolves in water very well. Which is not true in the following statements?

A.Water is a pure substance

B.Sodium chloride (table salt) is a pure substance

C.Dissolving sodium chloride (table salt) with water produces a mixture

D.Dissolving sodium chloride (table salt) with water produces a new substance

ANSWER

Among the given choices above the correct answer is letter D. Dissolving sodium chloride (table salt) with water produces a new substance .

Dissolving water with table salt or sodium chloride, cannot actually create a new substance, because a new substance can only be created if two or more different kinds of substance are mixed together.
