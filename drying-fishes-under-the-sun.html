7. drying of fresh fish under the sun decantation,evaporation,sieving handpicking,use of magnet, filtration​


Answer:

Evaporation

Explanation:

Evaporation is turning liquid into vapor using heat, which is what is happening when you are drying fresh fish under the sun. The sun's heat dries the fresh fish- which separates the solid (the fish) from the liquid (water and other fish juices). And in drying, the liquid becomes vapor; therefore it is evaporation.
