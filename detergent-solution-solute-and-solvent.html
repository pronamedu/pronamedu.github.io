The solution is the substance which is composed of two parts namely solute and the solvent.

The part of the solution in which substance is dissolved, it is called the solvent.

On the other hand, the part of the solution which is being dissolved in the solution, it is called as solute.

To find out the solute and solvent in the lemonade, things involved in lemonade must be identified.

Lemonade is prepared by mixing lemon juice and sugar with the water.

Thus lemon juice and sugar are solutes whereas water is the solvent in Lemonade.
