10. 4. Rubio and Bisana established a trading partnership. They allowing salaries of P40,000 per year for Rubio and interest on partner's capital at 5% per year. On Jan. 1, 2015, their capital balances are as follows: Rubio, P200,000 and Bisana, P100,000. On July 1, 2015, Bisana invested an additional P100,000 and Rubio's salary was discontinued. The partnership profit for the year ended Dec. 31, 2015 was P337,500. What was Rubio's total profit share for the year ended Dec. 31, 2015? a. P182,500 b. P178,750 c. P180,000 d. P190,000​


Rubio's share of the profit will be the residual profit after deducting Bisana's interest on capital and Rubio's salary.

From January 1 to June 30, 2015, Rubio's salary is P20,000 (P40,000/2). Thus, the residual profit for this period is P157,500 (P337,500 - P20,000 - 5% x P300,000).

From July 1 to December 31, 2015, there were no salaries provided to Rubio. Bisana's capital balance increased to P200,000 (P100,000 + P100,000). Thus, the interest on partner's capital for this period is P10,000 (5% x P200,000). The residual profit for this period is P170,000 (P337,500 - P10,000).

Therefore, Rubio's total profit share for the year ended December 31, 2015 is P327,500 (P157,500 + P170,000).

The correct answer is option a. P182,500.
