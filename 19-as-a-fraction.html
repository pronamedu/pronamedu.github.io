14. Express 19 7/13 into improper fraction. ​


Answer:

Improper fraction for 19 7/13

=254/13

Step-by-step explanation:

First, note that 19 7/13 is a mixed number, also know as mixed fraction. It has a whole number and a proper fraction. The numbers in the mixed fraction are defined as follow:

19 = whole number

7 = numerator

13 = denominator

To convert mixed number 19 7/13 to improper fraction, you follow these steps:

MULTIPLY the whole number by the denominator

19 × 13 = 247

ADD the product from Step 1 to the numerator

247 + 7 = 254

WRITE answer from Step 2 over the denominator

254/13

The mixed number - 19 7/13 converted to improper fraction is therefore:

=254/13
