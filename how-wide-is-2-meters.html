14. A rectangular water tank is 1/2 meter wide, 1 meter long and 1 1/2 meters high. If is half filled, how water does it contain?


Answer:

The volume of water at the tank is 0.375 cubic meters.

Step-by-step explanation:

Step 1: Write the given

Rectangular water tank is 1/2 meter wide (0.5 m)Rectangular water tank is 1 meter longRectangular water tank is 1 1/2 meters high (1.5 m)

Step 2: Identify the formula/s that will be utilized

Volume of rectangular prism is equal to (length)(width)(height)

Step 3: Find the volume of the water tank

The volume of the water tank is (1 m)(0.5 m)(1.5 m) which is equal to 0.75 cubic meters.

Step 4: Solve for the volume of water the tank contains

The tank is half-filled, which means the water is only half of the volume of the tank. Volume of water is (0.5)(0.75 cubic meters) is equal to 0.375 cubic meters.

Therefore, the volume of water at the tank is 0.375 cubic meters.
