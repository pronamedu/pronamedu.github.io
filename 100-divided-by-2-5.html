Given:

Let 2x be the first part

Let 3x be the second part

Let 5x be the third part

Mathematical expression:

2x + 3x + 5x = 100

Solution:

Solve for x

2x + 3x + 5x = 100

10x = 100

x = 10

First part = 2(x)

= 2(10)

= 20

Second part = 3(x)

= 3(10)

= 30

Third part = 5(x)

= 5(10)

= 50
