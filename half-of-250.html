18. a rock formed with 1,000 atoms of radioactive parent element, but only contains 250 radioactive parent atoms today. If the half-life is 1,000,000 years, how old is the rock?


Answer:

t = 2,000,000 years old

Explanation:

Compute for the rate constant.

k = (ln 2) / t½

k = (ln 2) / 1,000,000 year

k = 6.93147 × 10^-7 /year

Solve for the age of the rock

ln (N / No) = -kt

t = -ln (N / No) / k

t = -ln (250 / 1000) / 6.93147 × 10^-7 /year

t = 2,000,000 years old
