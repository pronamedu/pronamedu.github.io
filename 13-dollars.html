4. Eight dollars a week or a million dollars a year how different are they?​


Answer:

Big difference

Explanation:

Because Eight Dollars a week is equivalent to 416 Dollars only.

Answer:

A year is equivalent to approximately 52 weeks.

If we convert 1 million dollars a year to week we would get:

[tex] \frac{1000000 \: dollars}{year} \times \frac{1 \: year}{52 \: weeks} =19230.77 \: dollars \: per \: week[/tex]

Eight dollars a week converted to dollars per year is:

[tex] \frac{8 \: dollars}{1week} \times \frac{52 \: weeks}{1 \: year} = 416 \: dollars \: per \: year[/tex]

19230.77 > 8

and

1000000 > 416

Hence, the rate of getting a million dollars a year is much greater that the rate of getting 8 dollars per week.

14. While traveling to Europe, Phelan exchanged 250 US dollars for euros. He spent 150 euros on his trip. After returning to the United States he converts his money back to US dollars. How much of the original 250 US dollars does Phelan now have? 1 European euro = 1.3687 US dollars 44.70 US dollars 73.06 US dollars 136.87 US dollars 140.41 US dollars


First, we convert 250 US dollars to euros. Let's use the conversion that 1 euro = 1.3687 us dollars.

250/1.3687 = 182.655 euros

Then, we subtract from this his expenses.

182.655 - 150 = 32.655 euros

Then, let's convert this back to us dollars using the conversion:

32.655 euros * 1.3687 = 44,695 us dollars

Therefore, the answer is 44.70 us dollars.
