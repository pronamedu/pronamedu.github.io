Formulate and design a survey instrument that will fit in to the issue and welfare of the community in relation to the outreach programs of the National Service Training Program (NSTP). 3. Tabulate the data gathered from the Surveys and interviews and identify issues of concern based on the Outreach learning programs of NSTP. 4. Assess the available resources and limitations of the NSTP outreach programs.5. Formulate "Action Plan" for every prioritized outreach to be conducted to the community​Patulong Mga Boi ^^


Answer:

Conduct a community assessment by gathering data from multiple sources, such as community leaders, government officials, and local organizations.

Analyze the data to identify patterns and trends in the community's needs and challenges.

Involve the community in the assessment process by holding meetings and workshops to gather input and feedback.

Prioritize the identified needs and challenges based on the level of urgency and potential impact on the community.

Develop a comprehensive action plan that addresses the prioritized needs and challenges, and includes specific goals, objectives, and strategies for addressing them.

Regularly monitor and evaluate the effectiveness of the action plan, and make adjustments as needed to ensure that the community's needs are being met.

Collaborate with local organizations and government agencies to leverage resources and support the implementation of the action plan.

Communicate the results of the assessment and the proposed action plan to the community and other stakeholders to ensure transparency and accountability.
