WHAT IS ASKED?

Choose from the given choices where would an employee find the most help in bargaining for better wages.

ANSWER

The correct answer is Option C - Labor Union.

Explanation:

Labor unions nowadays are making an impact on the current situation of the labor force. Labor unions provide various help to every worker or employee. One of these is 'collective bargaining'.

What is Collective Bargaining?

Collective bargaining is a process of negotiating important elements of employment conditions together with the employers.

One of these important elements is the wage. Hence, an employee finds the most help in bargaining for better wages  in labor unions.
