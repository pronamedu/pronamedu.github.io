2. how many gram are there in 1/4 kg​


Answer:

250

Explanation:

To calculate a value in kilograms to the corresponding value in grams, just multiply the quantity in kilograms by 1000 (the conversion factor).

Here is the formula:

Value in grams = value in kilograms × 1000

Suppose you want to convert a kilogram into grams. In this case you will have:

Value in grams = 1/4 × 1000 = 250
