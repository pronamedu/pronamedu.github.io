Let the following expressions represent the value of the item that Joey and Anton made.
Joey = x
Anton = 39 + 3x
.
Solution:
x + 39 + 3x = 79
x + 3x = 79 - 39
4x = 40
4x/4 = 40/4
x = 10
.

28. Carlo, Joey, and Noel have a total of 4, 800 pesos. Joey’s money is 800 pesos more than Carlo’s money, while Noel’s money is 400 pesos less than Joey’s money. If Carlo has 1, 200, how much do Noel and Joey have?​


Answer:

Noel has 1,600 Pesos while Joey has 2000 Pesos.

Step-by-step explanation:

Carlos has 1,200. We can set this as "base". If Joey has 800 pesos than Carlos' money, add 1,200 and 800. Sum will be 2,000(Joey's money). Noel's money is 400 pesos less than Joey's. Subtract the 2,000 and 400. Difference will be 1,600.
