1. a large pizza has a 24 slices and 5 over 6 of slices of pizza is eaten, how many slices are left?


We first multiply (5/6) to know how many slices are eaten: (5/6)(24) = 20 slices. Therefore, 24 - 20 = 4 slices are left.

2. You have found that a good ratio of pizzas to people is 1:3. You are having a party and there will be 12 people there. How many pizza do you need for the party?


Answer:

4 pizzas

Step-by-step explanation:

Let the number of pizza you need be x. In proportion, we can essentially treat the ratios as fractions,

[tex]\frac{1}{3} = \frac{x}{12}[/tex]

We can then multiply 12 to both sides to get only x,

[tex]12(\frac{1}{3}) = x\\4 = x[/tex]

Therefore, you need 4 pizzas in the party.

24. The area of an extra large circular pizza from Gambino's Pizzeria is 484π cm 2 .What is the diameter of an extra large pizza from Gambino's Pizzeria


Answer:

44 cm

Step-by-step explanation:

What is asked?

The measurement of the diameter of extra large pizza from Gambino's Pizzeria

What are the given?

The area is 484π square centimeters.

Operation/Formula:

A pizza takes the shape of a circle which means that we could use the formula:

[tex]A= \pi r^2 \: [/tex]

This is the formula used to find the area of a circle. Observing the given, it is enough to find the radius of the circle. We find first the radius and that way we can easily find the diameter.

Solution:

[tex]A= \pi r^2 \\ 484 \: \pi \: {cm}^{2} = \pi r^2 \\ \frac{484 \: \pi \: {cm}^{2}}{\pi} = \frac{\pi {r}^{2} }{\pi} \\ 484 {cm}^{2} = {r}^{2} \\ \sqrt{484 {cm}^{2} } = \sqrt{{r}^{2}} \\ r = 22cm[/tex]

The radius measures 22cm. Now a radius is only half the diameter. That is,

[tex]d = 2r \\ d = 2(22cm) \\ d = 44cm[/tex]

Therefore, the diameter is 44cm.

29. In a class party 3 pizzas were ordered for the children. Each pizza was divided into 8 slices. Each child ate 2 slices. One-fourth of a pizza was left. How many children were at the party? ​


Answer:

11 children

Step-by-step explanation:

There are 3 pizza's ordered. Each pizza was divided into 8 slices:

In a pizza, there are 8 slices. If there are 3 pizza's ordered and each were cut into 8 slices, then there are a total of 24 slices.

(Or simply 3×8)

Each child ate 2 slices. One-fourth of a pizza was left:

*Take note: one-fourth of a pizza

Each pizza is cut into 8 slices. One-fourth of it was only left. That means one-fourth of 8 slices was left so there are only 2 slices are left.

If there are a total of 24 slices and only 2 are left, then there were 22 slices eaten by these children.

*Note that each child ate 2 slices.

Since there were 22 slices eaten, and a child ate 2 slices, we divide the number of slices eaten to the number slices were eaten by a single child

22÷2=11

There are 11 children at a party.
