[tex]18 = {2}^{1} \times {3}^{2} [/tex]

Prime factors of 27 are 3. Prime factorization of 27 in exponential form is:

[tex]27 = {3}^{3} [/tex]

Now multiplying the highest exponent prime factors to calculate the LCM of 18 and 27.

LCM(18,27) =

[tex] {2}^{1} \times {3}^{3} [/tex]

LCM(18,27)= 54
