CAPACITOR

After one time period, a capacitor charges to 63 percent of the source voltage that is charging it. A capacitor charges up to nearly 99 percent of its source voltage after 5 time periods. As a result, it's acceptable to state that a capacitor needs 5 time constants to charge up to the supply voltage.

The formula for calculating a capacitor's time constant is =RC. This figure represents the time it takes a capacitor to charge to 63 percent of the voltage that is charging it up (in seconds). The capacitor will be charged to over 99 percent of the voltage supplied after 5 time constants.

As a result, the formula for calculating how long a capacitor takes to charge is:

Time for a Capacitor to Charge= 5RC

For all intents and purposes, the capacitor will be charged up to very close to the source voltage after 5 time constants. A capacitor never fully charges to its source voltage's maximum voltage, but it comes near. The capacitor charges with infinite current in 0 time if the voltage source and capacitor are both "ideal" (zero internal resistance). Interesting. When a charged ideal capacitor is subjected to an ideal short, it discharges to zero volts in zero time, and the energy is radiated away once more. 3. Write your observations when you are wearing and not wearing your 3d glasses. And how do you think does it happen?​


Answer:

3D glasses work by using different filters or lenses for each eye. When viewing a 3D image or movie, the glasses cause each eye to see a slightly different image, creating the illusion of depth and dimensionality. Without the glasses, both eyes would see the same image, and the illusion of 3D would not be possible.

The specific type of 3D glasses you are using will affect the experience. Some use red and blue lenses, while others use polarized lenses. Some newer 3D technologies, such as those used in virtual reality headsets, use complex displays and tracking systems to create an immersive 3D experience without the need for glasses.

In general, when wearing 3D glasses, you should be able to perceive a greater sense of depth and immersion in the content you are viewing compared to when you are not wearing them. This is because the glasses are able to trick your brain into perceiving the image as 3D, which can make it feel more realistic and engaging.

However, some people may experience discomfort or headaches when wearing 3D glasses for an extended period of time. This is because the glasses are creating a slight visual mismatch between the two eyes, which can cause strain or discomfort for some people.

Overall, 3D glasses are an effective tool for creating a more immersive and engaging viewing experience, but they are not always necessary or appropriate for all types of content or viewers

20. How do you determine that a circuit is fully functional?​


Answer:

By using a multimeter

Explanation:

An open circuit is one that is discontinuous at a point preventing electricity from flowing through it. Although you can close and open a circuit at will using a switch, some open circuits may be caused by other reasons such as a wire cut in the circuit or an accidentally blown fuse. You can test for an open circuit by testing the continuity of the circuit using a multimeter.

23. My phone battery lasts for 24 hours when fully charged. the duration that the battery lasts is proportional to its percentage charge. how long will the battery last if it is 25% charged


Answer:

6 hours

Explanation:

24 hours charged = 100% used

100% ÷ 25% = 4

24 hours ÷ 4 = 6 hours

6 hours = 25% charged

Answer:

Your 3d flowers can be made in twomethods, Digital and Crafting.

Explanation:

Basically, you can use both methods to speed up your production. It really depends on your resources available.

Digital

To make a 3D flower you need a computer capable of running 3d software like Autodesk 3DMax or Maya. I recommend Maya or Blender both are lesser on the technical aspect of 3D making and Blender is friendly for beginners.

If you have the means, you can also 3d print your 3D flower.

The downside to this option is that you'll need to study the software which is quite complicated and will take quite some time.

Another option is, you can also use photo editing programs like Adobe Photoshop and print your cut-outs for your 3d Flower.

Crafting

For Crafting I recommend those papers that are hard and flexible like Cardstock and Construction paper but you other papers depending on the texture you want to make. You may also use wires for the "skeleton" of the flower stem and leaves.

As previously mentioned, there is also the art of Origami. You can either see step-to-step instructions online or buy a book about origami. The paper used in Origami is simply called by the name itself "Origami Paper".

The best thing is, just have fun making your 3d Flower.
