13. a snake erawled 400cm 10second what is the total speed of the snake​


Answer:

To find the speed of the snake, we need to divide the distance it traveled by the time it took to travel that distance.

The distance traveled by the snake is 400 cm, and the time it took to travel that distance is 10 seconds.

Therefore, the speed of the snake is:

Speed = Distance / Time

Speed = 400 cm / 10 s

Speed = 40 cm/s

So the total speed of the snake is 40 cm/s.
