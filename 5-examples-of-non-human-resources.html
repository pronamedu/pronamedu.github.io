5 examples of non Human Resources



Non-human resources refer to those resources that are outside human beings.
Non-human resources are tangible things or objects that exist externally of people. They can be seen, experienced and used by people. Non-human resources are also known as material resources.
Examples include cars, hospitals, banks, libraries, parks petrol, computers, books, clocks, plants and money.
