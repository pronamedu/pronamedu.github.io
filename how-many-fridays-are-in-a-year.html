12. Denver reads his storybook, 1 page on Monday, 3 pages on Tuesday, 5 pages on Wednesday, and 7 pages on Thursday. If he continued that pattern, how many pages should he read on Friday? How many pages did he read on Monday? Tuesday? Wednesday? Thursday? Friday?​


Denver's pattern suggests that he reads an increasing odd number of pages each day of the week. We can observe that this pattern is adding 2 pages to the previous day's reading every day.

So, using this pattern, we can calculate the number of pages that Denver should read on Friday as follows:

Number of pages on Friday = Number of pages on Thursday + 2

= 7 + 2

= 9

Therefore, Denver should read 9 pages on Friday.

To determine the number of pages that Denver read on Monday, Tuesday, Wednesday, and Thursday, we can simply list the given numbers:

Denver read 1 page on Monday.

Denver read 3 pages on Tuesday.

Denver read 5 pages on Wednesday.

Denver read 7 pages on Thursday.

Therefore, Denver read 1 page on Monday, 3 pages on Tuesday, 5 pages on Wednesday, 7 pages on Thursday, and should read 9 pages on Friday if he continues the pattern.
