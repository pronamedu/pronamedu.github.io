5. coffee solution or suspension?​


Answer:

solution

Explanation:

because madami nang nalagay sa kape

Answer:

Solution

Explanation:

When grounds are exposed to/submerged in water, they slowly dissolve into the water. The rate at which this happens is determined by various factors (grind size, water temperature, pressure...), and not all of the coffee is soluble, but the resulting beverage is a solution.

There is a reason why the strength of coffee is measured using a refractometer to calculate its TDS - total dissolved solids.

Even without a refractometer or any other fancy and expensive equipment, you can (easily) measure this dissolution by drying out your grinds after brewing in a low oven and then comparing its weight to the weight of the grinds before brewing. The weight loss will have dissolved into the beverage.
