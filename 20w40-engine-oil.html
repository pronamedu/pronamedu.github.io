lubricants is a substance that reduces friction between 2 moving parts it could be gear oil, transmission oil/ fluid etc while engine oil is a specific lubricant used in engine that reduces friction in parts such as connecting rod, crankshaft, camshaft etc..

16. it is easier to drain the oil completely when the engine is warm​


Answer:

You warm up the oil so it's less viscous and it flows better. That way, less of the old oil remains inside the engine — stuck to the walls of the oil pan and other engine parts. Recently circulated oil also picks up more contaminants and holds them in suspension.
