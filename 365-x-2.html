SCIENTIFIC NOTATION

[tex]__________________________[/tex]

0.000000365 = B. 3.65 x 10^-7In converting numbers that are in standard form to scientific notation you have to move its decimal point. Since the number is lower than 1, we will move the decimal point to the right until we get a number greater than 1 but less than 10.Refer to the picture for the movement of the decimal pointWe moved the decimal point 7 places to the right so the exponent will be -7. For the base, it will remain 10 and for the mantissa, it will be 3.65.

↬ Hence, the answer is B. 3.65 x 10^-7

9. Find the surface area in sq. in. of each right prism with dimensions 8 in x 12 in x 6 in (Base = 8 in x 6 in) A. 336 B. 392 C. 365 D. 304


The answer is B. 392 sq. in.

To find the surface area of a right prism, you need to find the area of each of its faces and add them up. A right prism has two congruent bases, and each base is a rectangle with dimensions 8 inches by 6 inches. The area of each base is therefore 8 inches * 6 inches = 48 sq. in.

In addition to the bases, the prism also has lateral faces, which are parallelograms with dimensions 8 inches by 12 inches. There are two lateral faces, one on each side of the prism. The area of each lateral face is therefore 8 inches * 12 inches = 96 sq. in.

Altogether, the surface area of the right prism is 48 sq. in. + 96 sq. in. + 96 sq. in. = 240 sq. in. + 96 sq. in. = 336 sq. in. + 96 sq. in. = 432 sq. in.

Therefore, the correct answer is B. 392 sq. in.
