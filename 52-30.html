29. Find the answer: 52-30+43-20 +75-60=​


Answer:

60.

Step-by-step explanation:

Starting with 52 - 30, the result is 22.

Next, adding 43 to 22 gives 65.

Then subtracting 20 from 65 gives 45.

Finally, adding 75 - 60 gives 15, and adding it to 45 gives the final answer of 60.

52 - 30 + 43 - 20 + 75 - 60 = 22 + 43 - 20 + 15 = 65 - 20 + 15 = 45 + 15 = 60.
