2. Six men can eat 3 bunches of grapes in 45 minutesHow long will it take for 2 men to eat the sameamount of grapes? using inverse proportion​


Answer:

135 minutes

Step-by-step explanation:

Inverse proportion:

If there are more men eating 3 bunches of grapes, the faster they will finish eating the grapes.

If there are fewer men eating the same amount of grapes, the slower they will finish eating the grapes.

2x = (6)(45)

2x = 270

2x/2 = 270/2

x = 135 minutes

11. Bob packed twenty grapes and a pear in his lunch. He ate thirteen grapes and a pear. What percent of the grapes did Bob eat​


Answer:

Bob packed a total of 20 grapes and 1 pear in his lunch. He ate 13 grapes and 1 pear.

So, the number of grapes he ate is 13 grapes.

To find out what percentage of grapes Bob ate, we can use the formula:

Percentage = (Number of grapes eaten / Total number of grapes) x 100

Substituting the values, we get:

Percentage = (13 / 20) x 100

Percentage = 0.65 x 100

Percentage = 65%

Therefore, Bob ate 65% of the grapes he packed in his lunch.
