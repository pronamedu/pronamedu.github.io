Answer:

39) To solve for x, we need to isolate x on one side of the equation. First, we add 10 to both sides of the equation:

8x - 10 + 10 = 14 + 10

This simplifies to:

8x = 24

To solve for x, we divide both sides by 8:

x = 3

Therefore, x = 3.

40) To solve for n, we need to isolate n on one side of the equation. First, we add 10 to both sides of the equation:

n + 5 - 10 + 10 = 15 + 10

This simplifies to:

n + 5 = 25

To solve for n, we subtract 5 from both sides:

n = 20

Therefore, n = 20.

41) Let's call the number we are thinking of "x". According to the problem, we add 40 to x and then divide the sum by 5 to get 30.

This can be written as:

(x + 40) / 5 = 30

To solve for x, we multiply both sides by 5:

x + 40 = 150

Then, we subtract 40 from both sides:

x = 110

Therefore, the number we are thinking of is 110.
