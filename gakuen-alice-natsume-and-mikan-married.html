24. alice and bob share some money in the radio 7:5. Alice got £4 more than bob. How much did alice get?​


Answer:

14 pounds

Step-by-step explanation:

Alice:Bob = 7:5

difference in the ratio = 2

since Alice got 4 pounds more, one unit in the ratio = 4 / 2 = 2

Plugging in:

Alice:Bob = 7(2):5(2) = 14:10 (satisfies the condition)
