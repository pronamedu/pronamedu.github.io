SUDEP is defined as: “when a person with epilepsy dies suddenly and prematurely and no reason for death is found”. SUDEP in epilepsy often occurs during sleep, either during or just after an epileptic seizure.

At present, scientists are still not sure exactly why people die from SUDEP although they believe it could be because of:

Breathing problems: During a seizure, the person may pause their breathing (called apnea). 14. Cameron works at an electronics store as a salesperson. Cameron earns a 4% commission on the total dollar amount of all phone sales he makes, and earn a 6% commission on all computer sales. Cameron made a total of $3600 in sales and earned $178 in commission. Write a system of equations that could be used to determine the dollar amount of phone sales Cameron made and the dollar amount of computer sales he made. Define the variables that you use to write the system.​


VARIABLES

[tex]\sf{===========================================}[/tex]

[tex]{\underline{\huge \mathbb{ A} {\large \mathrm { NSWER: }}}} \\[/tex]

[tex]\textsf{Let $\boxed{x}=\boxed{\textsf{The total dollar amount of all phone sales}}$}\:.[/tex]

[tex]\textsf{Let $\boxed{y}=\boxed{\textsf{The total dollar amount of all computer sales}}$}\:.[/tex]

[tex] \textsf{System of Equations} : [/tex]

[tex]\qquad\boxed{ \bold{ \: \: x + y = 3600 \: \: } }[/tex]

[tex]\qquad\boxed{ \bold{ \: \: 0.04x + 0.06y = 178 \: \: }} \\ [/tex]

[tex]\sf{===========================================}[/tex]

[tex]{\underline{\huge \mathbb{S} {\large \mathrm {OLUTION : }}}} \\[/tex]

[tex] \textsf{Given Information:}[/tex]

4% commission on the total dollar amount of all phone sales.6% commission on all computer sales.Total sales = $3600Total commission = $178

[tex] \textsf{Define the variables:}[/tex]

Let x = The total dollar amount of all phone sales.Let y = The total dollar amount of all computer sales.

[tex] \textsf{Convert the percentages into decimal form:}[/tex]

[tex] \sf\implies \sf 4\%=\dfrac{4}{100}=0.04[/tex]

[tex] \sf\implies \sf 6\%=\dfrac{6}{100}=0.06 \\ [/tex]

Therefore, the system of equations that could be used to determine the dollar amount of phone sales and computer sales is:

[tex]\begin{gathered}\begin{cases} \sf{x + y = 3600}\\ \sf{0.04x + 0.06y = 178}\end{cases}\end{gathered} [/tex]

[tex] \textsf{Solving the system of equations}[/tex]

[tex] \textsf{Rewrite the first equation to isolate y:}[/tex]

[tex]\sf\implies x=3600-y\\[/tex]

Substitute the expression for x into the second equation and solve for y:

[tex] \sf\implies 0.04(3600-y)+0.06y=178[/tex]

[tex] \sf\implies 144-0.04y+0.06y=178[/tex]

[tex] \sf\implies 144+0.02y=178[/tex]

[tex] \sf\implies 0.02y=34[/tex]

[tex] \sf\implies y=1700\\[/tex]

Therefore, Cameron made $1,700 of computer sales.

Substitute the found value of y into the first equation and solve for x:

[tex] \sf\implies x+1700=3600[/tex]

[tex] \sf\implies x=1900 \\ [/tex]

Therefore, Cameron made $1,900 of phone sales.
