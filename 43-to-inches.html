18. If x = 6 inches, y = 15 inches, z = 16 inches, and w = 3 inches, what is the perimeter of the object? A. 43 inches B. 45 inches C. 40 inches D. 48 inches


Answer:

To find the perimeter of the object, we need to add the lengths of all the sides together.

Given:

x = 6 inches

y = 15 inches

z = 16 inches

w = 3 inches

Perimeter = x + y + z + w

Plugging in the given values:

Perimeter = 6 inches + 15 inches + 16 inches + 3 inches

Perimeter = 40 inches

So, the correct answer is option C: 40 inches.
