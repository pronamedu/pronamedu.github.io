16. Given the relation y = 3x2 + 1, if the input is 4, what is the output? 13 48 49 145


Hey mate,
Here's your answer,

y = 3x² + 1 {given}

if x = 4,

y = 3x² + 1
→ y = 3(4)² + 1
→ y = 3×16 + 1
→ y = 48 + 1 = 49


Hence the output is 49.
