1. A debt of ₱15,000 was paid for as follows: ₱4,000 at the end of three months, ₱5,000 at the end of 12 months, ₱3,000 at the end of 15 months, and a final payment F at the end of 21 months. If the rate of interest was 18% compounded quarterly, find the final payment F.​


Answer:

₱6,221.98

Step-by-step explanation:

The final payment is:

F = (15,000×(1 + (0.18/4)) - 4,000)×(1 + 0.045)^3 - 5,000)×(1 + 0.045) - 3,000)×(1 + 0.045)^2 = ₱6,221.98

R = 6% = 0.06

N = 4 (quarterly)

T = 35 years and 9 months = 35.75 years
