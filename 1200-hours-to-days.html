5. Calculate the horsepower needed for a 1200-kg car to climb to an altitude of 1000 m from sea level in 1 hour.


The horsepower needed : 4.38 HP

Further explanation

Power is the work done every second. Can be formulated:  

P = W/t  

P = watt = J / s  

Work itself is a change in energy that occurs in objects, (changes in kinetic energy or potential energy)  

W = ΔKE = ΔPE

ΔPE = mg(h₂-h₁)

ΔKE = 1/2 m(v₂²-v₁²)  

Given

mass = m = 1200 kg

height = h = 1000 m

time = t = 1 hour = 3600 s

Required

The horse power

Solution

W = mg(h₂-h₁)

W = 1200 x 9.8 x 1000 m

W = 1.176 x 10⁷ J

P = 1.176 x 10⁷ J : 3600 s

P = 3.267 x 10³ watt

1  HP = 746  W

So P = 4.38 HP

22. Ajer can travel 1200 kilometers in 4 hours with the wind. The return trip against the wind takes 6 hours. Find the rate of the jet in still air and the rate of the wind​


Answer:

Rate of jet in still air: 250 km/hr

Rate of wind: 100 km/hr

Step-by-step explanation:

-> First trip:

1200 km/4 hours = 300 km/hr

-> Return trip:

1200 km/6 hours = 200 km/hr

-> Rate of jet in still air:

(300 km/hr + 200 km/hr) / 2 = 250 km/hr

-> Rate of wind

300 km/hr - 200 km/hr = 100 km/hr
