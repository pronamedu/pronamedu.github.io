2. Peter has changed his diet to eat healthier. 2/3 of his meals are gluten free and of these gluten free meals 1/2 are vegan. How much of his total diet is gluten free and vegan?


Add the two fractions. First, find the LCD which is 6. The LCD will become our new denominator. To know/find the new numerator,
[tex] \frac{LCD(numerator)}{denominator} [/tex]
[tex] \frac{6(2)}{3} =4\\ \frac{6(1)}{2} =2[/tex]

[tex] \frac{4}{6} + \frac{3}{6} = \frac{7}{6} or 1 \frac{1}{6} [/tex]

Therefore, the answer to your question is [tex] 1 \frac{1}{6} [/tex]
