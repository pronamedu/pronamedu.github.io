                                             Expat Levi in Riyadh
                                                  Determination

Expats are employees from outside the country. And Levi is one of them.
At first, perhaps everything seems perfect and overwhelming. The joy of being able to earn more and explore a new country can be exciting.

But what really is inside an expats heart, like Levi, when they have decided to make that huge move to work abroad?

Is it for his own good, or for someone else?

Perhaps Levi himself at first did not know what really drives him to leave his country. And gradually as time passes by, and the sting of loneliness comes, obscure answers become clear. Questions become easy to answer. 

It is when you are at the pit of loneliness that you come to weigh what really was in your heart when you left loved ones behind. 

The saddest part is when you think of quitting and going home just to stop the pain from missing those who are left behind. 

Love may be a strong emotion that can sometimes move mountains. But it doesn't always answer the assurance of staying, for you to give your families better lives by working abroad.

Determination is an important ingredient in what ever venture you are in. And this is most especially when you are far from your family, who are the main reasons why you left. 

The will to go should be as firm as the will to stay.

6. B. Determine the time in the following countries if it is 18:30H in the Philippine 6. jerusalem 7. riyadh 8. mexico 9. dubai 20. new york​


Answer:

To determine the time in different countries, we need to consider their time zones and the time difference from the Philippine Standard Time (PST).

6. Jerusalem, Israel is 6 hours behind the PST, so the time in Jerusalem would be:

18:30 - 6 hours = 12:30

Therefore, if it is 18:30 in the Philippines, it would be 12:30 in Jerusalem.

7. Riyadh, Saudi Arabia is 5 hours behind the PST, so the time in Riyadh would be:

18:30 - 5 hours = 13:30

Therefore, if it is 18:30 in the Philippines, it would be 13:30 in Riyadh.

8. Mexico City, Mexico is 14 hours behind the PST, so the time in Mexico City would be:

18:30 - 14 hours = 4:30

Therefore, if it is 18:30 in the Philippines, it would be 4:30 in Mexico City.

9. Dubai, United Arab Emirates is 4 hours behind the PST, so the time in Dubai would be:

18:30 - 4 hours = 14:30

Therefore, if it is 18:30 in the Philippines, it would be 14:30 in Dubai.

10. New York, USA is 12 hours ahead of the PST, so the time in New York would be:

18:30 + 12 hours = 6:30

Therefore, if it is 18:30 in the Philippines, it would be 6:30 the next day in New York due to the time difference and time zone.
