13. Gary’s mother takes her medicine 3 times a day. How many days will 120 mL of her medicine last if 5mL is taken each time?


Answer:

Gary's mother's medicine will last her 8days before it rans out.

Step-by-step explanation:

Given: 3x/day dose

         120 mL medicine

         5mL each dose

You have to first divide the total amount of medicine with the amount of each dose. So, that is

120/5 = 24 (total number of doses)

So this means that Gary's mother has 24 doses for a 120mL medicine that she needs to take thrice a day.

So since we need to determine the number of days her medicine will last, we need to divide still the total to the number of dose each day. So that is,

24/3 = 8

Therefore I can conclude that Gary's mother's medicine will last her 8days before it rans out.
