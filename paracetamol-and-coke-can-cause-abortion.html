25. A dozen of coke in can cost (48x-72) pesos.How much does a can of coke cost?​


Answer:

A dozen of coke is equal to 12 pcs. can of coke.

To find each cost, divide (48x - 72) into 12.

[tex] \frac{48x - 72}{12} = \frac{48x}{12} - \frac{72}{12} = 4x - 6 [/tex]

Therefore, (4x -6) pesos is the cost of each can of coke.
