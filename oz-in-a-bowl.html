1. A punch bowl contains 58 fluid ounces of fruit juice. Ishmat adds 2 containers of lemonade to the bowl. Each container holds 6 fluid ounces of lemonade. How much liquid is in the punch bowl after Ishmat adds the lemonade? please help! 46 fl oz 64 fl oz 66 fl oz 70 fl oz


Each container of lemonade has 6 fluid ounces, so 2 containers have a total of 2 × 6 = 12 fluid ounces of lemonade.

Therefore, the total amount of liquid in the punch bowl after Ishmat adds the lemonade is 58 + 12 = 70 fluid ounces.

Answer: 70 fl oz.
