1. How to solve 5+19x2=?


The answer is 43.

STEP BY STEP SOLUTION

19 X 2 = 38

5 + 38 = 43

Use the PEMDAS method.  

WHAT IS PEMDAS?

PEMDAS is a method of solving an equation according to the following steps .

Parenthesis Exponent Multiplication Division Addition Subtraction

In this case, there is no parenthesis and no exponent so we use MDAS instead of the whole process which was supposed to be PEMDAS. Let us proceed to multiplication first.

Since 19 and 2 have multiplication as their operation, we multiply these two numbers first following the PEMDAS method.

Multiplication and Division are like sisters. You can interchange the steps whichever comes first.  

Example:

10/2x3 =15

10 divided by 2 equals 5 then 5 times 3 equals 15.

Though in PEMDAS, M or multiplication comes first before the D or Division,you can divide first because as being said, multiplication and division can be interchanged depending on whichever comes first. The same rule applies in Addition and subtraction.

ANOTHER EXAMPLE:

20-3x6/2+1

Following the PEMDAS,

Multiply first : 3x 6 =18

Second, divide: 18/2 = 9

REMINDER: Though in PEMDAS, multiplication comes first, they can interchanged with division whichever comes first. Same with addition and subtraction.

So:

20-9 = 11

11+1 = 12

Therefore;

The answer is 12!
