8. What is Sulfur trioxide?​


Answer:

Sulfur trioxide is the chemical compound with the formula SO₃. It has been described as "unquestionably the most important economically" sulfur oxide. It is prepared on an industrial scale as a precursor to sulfuric acid. Sulfur trioxide exists in several forms - gaseous monomer, crystalline trimer, and solid polymer.

Formula: SO3

Molar mass: 80.06 g/mol

Boiling point: 45 °C

Melting point: 16.9 °C

Density: 1.92 g/cm³
