20. what is the estimated quotient of 541 divide 87?


Question:What is the estimated quotient of 541 divided by 87?

Answer:

The estimated quotient of 541 divided by 87 is 6.

Step-by-step explanation:

Since the question just finding the estimated quotient, we need to round of the dividend and divisor so that we can get the estimated value of 541 divided by 87.

How to round off numbers?

To round off numbers, we just need to look at the right ( ➝ ) side of the number we’ll rounding off.

If the number on right side is 1-4, we must remain the number number and change into 0 all the number/s on right side of it.If the number on right side is 5-9, we must add 1 to the number we’ll rounding off and change all into 0 all the number/s on the right side of it.

Rounding off the Divisor:

87 = 90

Rounding off the Dividend:

541 = 540

Since we’re done rounding of the dividend and divisor now, we can now change the equation 541 ÷ 87 into 540 ÷ 90.

It’s easy to solve now but it will be more easy if we’ll remove the 0 on both side since they both have one 0.

540 ÷ 90 = 54 ÷ 9

Now, we can solve it literally without using long division since the divisor is just only a single number.

54 ÷ 9 = 6
