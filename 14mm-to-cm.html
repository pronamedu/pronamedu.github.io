14. Diameter; 7mm,height; 14mm


Answer:

If you are asking about volume, the answer is 538.78 [tex]mm^3[/tex]


If you are asking about total surface area, the answer is 384.85 [tex]mm^2[/tex]


Step-by-step explanation:


A cylinder is a solid bounded by a closed cylindrical surface and two parallel planes.  

To calculate the volume of the cylinder, the following formula will be used


[tex]V = (\frac{\pi d^2}{4})h[/tex]


Using the given height and diameter we have


[tex]V = (\frac{\pi (7mm)^2}{4})(14mm)\\V = 538.78 mm^3[/tex]


To calculate the total surface area TSA of the cylinder, the following formula will be used


[tex]TSA = \frac{\pi d^2}{2} + \pi dh\\TSA = \frac{\pi (7mm)^2}{2} + \pi (7mm)(14mm)\\TSA = 384.85 mm^2[/tex]
