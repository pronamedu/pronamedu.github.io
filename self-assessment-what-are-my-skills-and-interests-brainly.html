28. what are my skills and interests brainly


Answer:

Skills and interests are two different thing but can be linked to each other depends on your choices and preferences.

Explanation:

To explain further, a skill is something that you acquire. Either you acquire it from someone or from an institution like a school. Say for example, baking, creative writing, carpentry, those are skills. A skill is not something you can instantly do. Most often than not, it requires proper education to be able to master a skill.

Meanwhile, interest is what you want or what you like. If you will relate it to a skill, this means you can learn a skill that you're interested learning. This is what I mean when I say that skills and interests can be linked with each other. Say you have an interest to cooking, then you go and acquire cooking skills in a culinary school.

Bottom line, it is only you who can determine your skills and interests. Reflect on them so you will arrive at the best answer.
