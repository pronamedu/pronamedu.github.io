2. What is the meaning of wayang kulit puppet


What is Wayang?

It is also known as "wajang" it is a traditional form of puppet theatre play originally in the cultures of Java, Indonesia. It refers to kind of puppet theaters, the puppet itself is referred to as wayang. The performances of shadow puppet theaters are accompanied by a Gamelan orchestra in Java and by Gender Wayang in Bali.

What is Wayang Kulit?

It is a shadow puppet best known as Indonesian Wayang. Kulit means ''skin''. Wayang kulit refers to the leather construction of the puppets that are carefully shaped and supported with carefully shaped buffalo horn handles and control rods. Wayang means theatre ''performance or show''. Wayang Kulit puppets are made from water buffalo leather.

What is kulit in Indonesia?

Its equivalent in Indonesian is bayang. In modern daily Javanese and Indonesian vocabulary, wayang can refer to the puppet itself or the whole puppet theatre performance. Kulit means "skin" or "leather", the material from which the figures are carved.

What is the meaning of wayang?

puppet shadow play

: an Indonesian and especially Javanese dramatic representation of mythological events in a puppet shadow play or by human dancers.

What is the meaning of Dalang?

The dhalang or dalang (Javanese: dhalang; Indonesian and Malay: dalang) is the puppeteer in an Indonesian wayang performance. In a performance of wayang kulit, the dalang sits behind a screen (kelir) made of white cotton stretched on a wooden frame.
