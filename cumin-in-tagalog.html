2. anong klase ang cumin ibahagi ang anyo​


Cumin is a flowering plant in the family Apiaceae, native to the Irano-Turanian Region. Its seeds – each one contained within a fruit, which is dried – are used in the cuisines of many cultures in both whole and ground form.

7. Applied problems involving Linear EquationsExample 1: A student meceived 58% 34%, 70% and 51% on four examinations in Algebra. What musthe get on the next cumination to get a passing grade of 55%?Example 2: Find the dimensions of a rectangle whose length is 9 less than twice its width if theperimeter is 120 cm.​


EXAMPLE 1

Answer: 62%

Solution:

For this solution, I just ignored the percent sign.

Passing Grade is 55.

Number of exams is 5.

In order to find the total score needed to get the average of 55%, I multiply the passing grade with the number of exams.

So, 55x5=275.

Next, I find the total of the exams already taken.

So, 58+34+70+51=213.

Finally, I subtract 213 to 275.

So, 275-213=62.

So the student needs to get the score 62 to be able to get the passing grade of 55%.

EXAMPLE 2

Answer: 37 (length) by 23 (width)

Explanation:

From the problem, we have the following information:

l = 2w - 9

Perimeter = 120

The formula for finding the perimeter is P = 2w + 2l.

We substitute l with the formula given and P with 120, and solve.

So,

120 = 2w + 2(2w - 9)

120 = 2w + 4w - 18

120 = 6w - 18

120 + 18 = 6w - 18 + 18

138 = 6w

138/6 = 6w/6

23 = w

.

So we now know that the width is 23 cm. With this information, we can now find the length.

So,

l = 2w - 9

= 2(23) - 9

= 46 - 9

= 37

.

So the dimension of the rectangle would be 37 by 23.

Check:

120 = 2w + 2l

120 = 2(23) + 2(37)

120 = 46 + 74

120 = 120
