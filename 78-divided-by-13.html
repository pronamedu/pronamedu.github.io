22. 2 Divide 78 with solution


Answer:

Okay so the first thing we need to do is clarify the terms so that you know what each part of the division is:

The first number, 78, is called the dividend.The second number, 2 is called the divisor.

Step 1

The first step is to set up our division problem with the divisor on the left side and the dividend on the right side.

Step 2

We can work out that the divisor (2) goes into the first digit of the dividend (7), 3 time(s). Now we know that, we can put 3 at the top.

Step 3

If we multiply the divisor by the result in the previous step (2 x 3 = 6), we can now add that answer below the dividend.

Step 4

Next, we will subtract the result from the previous step from the second digit of the dividend (7 - 6 = 1) and write that answer below.

Step 5

Move the second digit of the dividend (8) down like so.

Step 6

The divisor (2) goes into the bottom number (18), 9 time(s), so we can put 9 on top.

Step 7

If we multiply the divisor by the result in the previous step (2 x 9 = 18), we can now add that answer below the dividend.

Step 8

Next, we will subtract the result from the previous step from the third digit of the dividend (18 - 18 = 0) and write that answer below.

So, what is the answer to 78 divided by 2?

If you made it this far into the tutorial, well done! There are no more digits to move down from the dividend, which means we have completed the long division problem.
