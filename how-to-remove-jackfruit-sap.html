21. A 10kg jackfruit is pushed across a table. If the acceleration of the jackfruit is 2m/s to the left, what is the net exerted on the fruit? ​


Answer:

Given: m=10kg

            a=2m/s^2 to the left

Solution: F=ma

                  =(10kg)(2m/s^2)

                  =20N or 20kg·m/s^2 to the left

GRESA method:

G:m=10kg, a=2m/s^2

R:Force(F)

E:F=ma

S:F=(10kg)(2m/s^2)

A:20N or 20kg·m/s^2 to the left

The force exerted on a 10kg jackfruit to accelerate at 2m/s^2 is 20N
