1. what is th biggest polygon


In geometry, the biggest little polygon for a number n is the n-sided polygon that has diameter one (that is, every two of its points are within unit distance of each other) and that has the largest area among all diameter-one n-gons. One non-unique solution when n = 4 is a square, and the solution is a regular polygon when n is an odd number, but the solution is irregular otherwise.

28. what is product of the biggest 2 digits and biggest 1 digit decimals that are less than 1​


The answer is 0.891

To find the biggest 2 digit decimal less than one, its digits should be high as possible.

Because it is two digit, we can only select the two digit numbers in the range 01 to 99 for the two decimal digit, and in this case, the highest number that we can select is 99.

Thus, the biggest two digit decimal less than one is 0.99.

Similarly, to find the biggest 1 digit decimal less than one, its one and only digit should be high as possible.

We can only select the numbers 1 to 9 for the decimal digit, and the highest number that we can select in the range is 9, so the biggest one digit decimal less than one is 0.9

To get the product of the biggest 2 digits and biggest 1 digit decimal less than 1, multiply 0.99 and 0.9.

Solving,

[tex]0.99 \times 0.9=\boxed{0.891}[/tex]

Therefore, the product of the biggest 2 digit and biggest 1 digit decimals is 0.891.
