27. An airplane flies from Manila to Singapore with an average velocity of 195 km/h. How long will it take to reach Singapore if it is 1213.56 km from Manila. Given: Find: Formula: Solution: I need it right now pls


Answer:

Given:

Average velocity of the airplane = 195 km/h

Distance from Manila to Singapore = 1213.56 km

Find:

Time it will take to reach Singapore

Formula:

Time = Distance / Velocity

Solution:

Using the formula above, we can solve for the time it will take to reach Singapore:

Time = Distance / Velocity

Time = 1213.56 km / 195 km/h

Time = 6.22 hours

Therefore, it will take approximately 6.22 hours for the airplane to reach Singapore from Manila with an average velocity of 195 km/h.
