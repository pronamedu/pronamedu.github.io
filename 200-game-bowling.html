29. richard bowled 3 games and got scores, 139, 153, and 128. What was his average scxore for the three games?


Answer:

The Average is 140

Step-by-step explanation:

The average of the numbers can also be called Mean.

We have two steps in finding Mean.

First, add the numbers.

139+153+128=420

Next is divide the sum by how many numbers (we have 3 numbers here)

420/3=140

The mean is 140.
