23. marites took an examination in mathematics and was able to solve 9 problems out of 12.a. what fraction of the problems did she solve correctly? b. what decimal part of the problem did she solve correctly? c. what was her grade in percent? Please give the solution.


A. Fraction= 3/4 because she solved correctly 9. So 9 will be the numerator and 12 will be the denominator because it's the number of the items of the test. Summing it all up, it will become 9/12, reduce to lowest terms, 3/4.

B.The fraction is 3/4 already. Just convert 3/4 into decimal by dividing numerator by the denominator which results 0.75

C. Since it is 0.75 (the decimal part) convert 0.75 into percent by moving the decimal point to the left 2 times which results 75%.
