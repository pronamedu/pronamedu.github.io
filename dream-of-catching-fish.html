16. 3How many fish can a fisherman catch in 8 hours if he can catch 40 fish in 5 hours.​


Answer:

64 fishes

Step-by-step explanation:

Before answering the question, we need to know how much fish would a fisherman catch in an hour.

Let us now divide the number of fishes caught by the number of hours spent.

40 fish / 5 hours

So basically, 40 divided by 5 is 8.

Therefore, he can get 8 fishes per hour.

Now, let us multiply the number of fishes caught per hour by the number of hours in the question.

8 fishes × 8 hours

So basically, 8 multiplied by 8 is 64.

Therefore, he can get 64 fishes within 8 hours.
