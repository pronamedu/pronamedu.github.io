1. Two third of the number is 72.


Are you asking to find the value of the number?

Let x be the number.

[tex] \frac{2}{3} x = 72 \\ \\ [/tex]

Solution:

[tex] \frac{2}{3} x = 72 \\ \frac{2x}{3} = 72 \\ \cancel3( \frac{2x}{ \cancel3} ) = 3(72) \\ 2x = 216 \\ \frac{ \cancel2x}{ \cancel2} = \frac{216}{2} \\ \huge\boxed{ \blue{ x = 108}} \\ \\ [/tex]

Check by substitution:

[tex] \frac{2}{3} x = 72 \\ \frac{2}{3} (108) = 72 \\ \frac{216}{3} = 72 \\ 72 \check= 72 \\ \\ [/tex]

Therefore, the number is 108.

2. Two-third of the number is 72​


QUESTION:

Two-third of the number is 72.

To find the number, divide 72 by two-third.

[tex]72 \div \frac{2}{3} = \frac{72}{1} \div \frac{2}{3} \\ \\ \frac{72}{1} \div \frac{3}{2 } = \frac{216}{2} \div 2 = \boxed {108}[/tex]

Two-third of 108 is 72.

To check, multiply 108 by two-third.

[tex]108 \times \frac{2}{3} = \frac{108}{1} \times \frac{2}{3} \\ \\ \frac{108}{1} \times \frac{2}{3} = \frac{216}{3} \div 3 = \boxed {72}[/tex]
