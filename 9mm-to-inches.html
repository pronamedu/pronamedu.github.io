Answer:

To find the circumference of the ring, we can use the formula:

circumference = pi x diameter

where pi is a mathematical constant approximately equal to 3.14.

Given that the diameter of the ring is 9mm, we can plug it into the formula:

circumference = pi x 9mm

30. What is the area of the circle 9mm?​radius.


Step-by-step explanation:

Sana Maka tulong nayan sayo

Answer:

The formula for determining the area of a circle is A = πr^2, where A is the area, π is 3.14, and r is the radius.

plugging in the radius of 9mm, we get:

A = π(9mm)^2

A = π*81mm^2

A = 254.47mm^2

So, the area of a circle with a radius of 9mm is approximately 254.47mm^2. This is equivalent to 0.0025447 square meters.
