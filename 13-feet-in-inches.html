15. A flagpole is 20 feet tall. Its shadow is 13 ft long. A boy is standing next to the flagpole. He is 5 feet 6 inches tall. About how long is his shadow?


Ratio ⇒  height : shadow

Flagpole height to its shadow ⇒ 20 ft : 13 ft

Let the shadow of the boy be x:

Boy's height to his shadow ⇒ 5 feet 6 inches : x ft

Proportion is the ratio of same unit. Convert the boy's height to feet.

1 foot = 12 inches   ⇒   6 inches = 1/2 foot or 0.5 foot

5 feet 6 inches = 5.5 feet

Direct proportion equation:

20 : 13 = 5.5 : x

20x = (13)(5.5)

20x/20 = 71.5/20

x = 3.575 feet  or  3 feet and 23/40 inches

ANSWER: The boy's shadow is 3 feet and 23/40 inches
