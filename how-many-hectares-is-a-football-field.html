16.5 hectares→ land

[tex] \frac{8}{10} [/tex]→ rice field

So, 

[tex]16.5* \frac{8}{10} \\ \\ =16 \frac{1}{2} * \frac{8}{10} \\ \\ = \frac{33}{2} * \frac{8}{10} \\ \\ = \frac{33}{1} * \frac{4}{10} \\ \\ = \frac{132}{10}= 13 \frac{1}{5} [/tex]
