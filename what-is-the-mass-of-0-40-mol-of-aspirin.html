10. Instructions: Use the periodic table to answer the questions. Present a detailed solutionfor credit.A.) Aspirin has the molecular formula C9H8O4.1. What is the % C in aspirin by mass in aspirin?2. What is the % 0 in aspirin by mass in aspirin?​


Solution (1):

Step 1: Calculate the molar mass of C₉H₈O₄.

molar mass = (12.01 g/mol × 9) + (1.008 g/mol × 8) + (16.00 g/mol × 4)

molar mass = 180.154 g/mol

Step 2: Calculate the percent of C in aspirin.

General Formula:

[tex]\text{percent composition} = \frac{\text{atomic mass of an element × number of atoms in a compound}}{\text{molar mass of compound}} × 100[/tex]

[tex]\text{\%C} = \frac{\text{12.01 g/mol × 9}}{\text{180.154 g/mol}} × 100[/tex]

[tex]\boxed{\text{\%C = 60.00\%}}[/tex]

Solution (2):

[tex]\text{\%O} = \frac{\text{16.00 g/mol × 4}}{\text{180.154 g/mol}} × 100[/tex]

[tex]\boxed{\text{\%O = 35.52\%}}[/tex]

17. A student was synthesizing aspirin in the laboratory. Using the amount of limiting reactant, she calculated the mass of aspirin that should form as 9.95 g. When she weighed her aspirin product on the balance, its mass was 7.54 g. a. What is the actual yield of aspirin? b. What is the theoretical yield of aspirin? c. Calculate the percent yield for this synthesis.​


a. ANSWER:

Note that the actual yield is the measured mass.

Actual yield is always less than the theoretical yield.

[tex]\boxed{\text{The actual yield of aspirin is 7.54 g.}}[/tex]

-----------------------------------------------------

b. ANSWER:

Note that the theoretical yield is the calculated mass.

Theoretical yield is always greater than the actual yield.

[tex]\boxed{\text{The theoretical yield of aspirin is 9.95 g.}}[/tex]

-----------------------------------------------------

c. SOLUTION:

[tex]\begin{aligned} \text{percent yield} & = \frac{\text{actual yield}}{\text{theoretical yield}} \times 100\% \\ & = \frac{\text{7.54 g}}{\text{9.95 g}} \times 100\% \\ & = 75.779\% \\ & \approx \boxed{75.8\%} \end{aligned}[/tex]

Hence, the percent yield for the synthesis of aspirin is 75.8%.
