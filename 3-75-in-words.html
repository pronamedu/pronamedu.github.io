28. five letters are to be selected from the word ALGORITHM. How many of these choices contain at least 2 vowels? A. 3,B. 20,C.36,D.75​


Answer:

D. 75

Step-by-step explanation:

There are two ways of solving the problem

Method 1:

Total ways of choosing 5 letters out of 9 is 9C5 = 126, this includes cases with 0,1,2 and 3 vowels

Of these, the number of 5-letter combinations containing no vowel is 6C5 = 6 (6C5 is for choosing 5 consonants out of 6)

And the choices containing exactly 1 vowel is 3C1x6C4 = 3x15 =45

So the number of choices, with 2 or 3 vowels is 126-6-45 = 75

Method 2:

Number of choices containing exactly 2 vowels is 3C2x6C3 = 3x20 = 60

Number of choices having exactly 3 vowels is 3C3x6C2 = 1x15

The number of choices, with 2 or 3 vowels is 60+15 = 75
