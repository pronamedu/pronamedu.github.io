WHAT IS PHI-COEFFICIENT?

In statistics, the phi coefficient is a measure of association for two binary variables. Introduced by Karl Pearson, this measure is similar to the Pearson correlation coefficient in its interpretation. In fact, a Pearson correlation coefficient estimated for two binary variables will return the phi coefficient.

Answer:

The electromagnetic radiation with the shortest wavelength and highest energy is known as a gamma ray.A photon is emitted when a nucleus transitions from a high-energy level to a lower-energy level, carrying away the surplus energy; nuclear energy levels correspond to photon wavelengths in the gamma-ray area.

Explain what is the meaning of gamma ray?​

What is the meaning of gamma ray? ​A gamma ray, also understood as gamma radiation, is a penetrating kind of electromagnetic radiation produced by atomic nuclei decaying radioactively. It is made up of electromagnetic waves with the shortest wavelengths, usually shorter than X-rays. It transmits the most photon energy at frequencies exceeding 30 exahertz (301018 Hz). While studying the radiation released by radium, Paul Villard, a French scientist and physicist, discovered gamma radiation in 1900. Ernest Rutherford termed this radiation gamma rays in 1903, based on its comparatively significant penetration of matter; in 1900, he had previously identified two less penetrating forms of decay radiation, alpha rays and beta rays, in ascending order of penetrating strength (found by Henri Becquerel).

Explanation:

Ang Tau Gamma Phi (ΤΓΦ), ay isang socio-economic service fraternity na itinayo sa University of the Philippines Diliman noong Oktubre 4, 1968. Kilala  bilang Triskelions' Grand Fraternity, ito ay iniuugnay sa ginto at itim na kulay at mayroong kaugnay nna internasyunal na konseho kasama ang mga bansang Australia, Canada, Germany, Italy, Spain, Saudi Arabia, at United Kingdom. Ang mga miyembro nito ay tinatawag na Triskelions at ang kaugnay nitong sorority ay ang Tau Gamma Sigma (ΤΓΣ), na tinatawag ring Triskelions' Grand Sorority.

Sa Pilipinas, mayroong mahigit na 4000 Colleges, Universities, at Community Based Chapters. Layunin ng Tau Gamma Phi na magkaroon ng Fraternity System na walang karahasan kaugnay ng malawakang karahasan  sa loob ng university campus sa panahon ng pagkakatag nito, iwasan ang elitismo, at sikaping isiwalat ang mga prinsipyo ng fraternity na Fortis Voluntas Fraternitas (Strength, Freewill and Brotherhood) at isabuhay ito.

Sinisikap ng Fraternity at ng mga miyembro nito na umunlad sa spritwal na aspeto at mag-organisa ng mga sosyo-sibiko na mga aktibidad para sa kapakanan ng mga komunidad. Sa kabila nito, nasangkot rin ang organisasyong ito sa halos dosenang kaso ng hazing mula 2006 kahit na mayroong Republic Act 8049 o anti-hazing law.
