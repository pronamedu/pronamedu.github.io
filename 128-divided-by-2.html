19. Divide 128 chairs among 3rooms in the ratio of 1:3:4​


Answer:

The first room will have 16 chairs, the second room will have 48 chairs, and the third room will have 64 chairs.

Step-by-step explanation:

Add all the ratios: 1 + 3 + 4 = 8

Then divide it by the number of chairs: 128 / 8 = 16

Multiply the quotient with the ratios:

16 * 1 = 16

16 * 3 = 48

16 * 4 = 64

Check if the total is equal to the number of chairs: 16 + 48 + 64 = 128
