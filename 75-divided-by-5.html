5. If 200 is divided in the ratio 1:2:5, what is the middle number? A. 25 B. 50 C. 75 D. 125


Answer:

B. 50 is the middle number

Step-by-step explanation:

Let x be the 1st number

2x be the 2nd number

5x be the 3rd number

And equate the following:

200 = x + 2x +5x

200 = 8x

25 = x

And then the numbers would be:

First number: x = 25

2nd number: 2x = 2(25)

                           = 50

3rd number: 5x = 5(25)

                          = 125

Therefore the middle number is 50.
