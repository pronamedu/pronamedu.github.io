30. Cite some real life examples that depict the concept of sex and gender.​


Answer:

Cite some real life examples that depict the concept of sex and gender.

Explanation:

1. Biological Sex: The medical diagnosis of male or female based on genitalia, chromosomes, and hormones.

Example: A person is born with male genitalia, XY chromosomes, and produces high levels of testosterone, which results in the development of male secondary sex characteristics such as facial hair and a deep voice.

2. Gender Identity: An individual's internal sense of self as male, female, both, or neither.

Example: A person who was assigned female at birth but identifies as a man and uses male pronouns and clothing.

3. Gender Expression: The external display of one's gender identity, often influenced by societal norms and expectations.

Example: A person who identifies as a woman but prefers to wear traditionally masculine clothing and has a short haircut.

4. Gender Roles: Social expectations and norms associated with gender, such as behaviors, attitudes, and responsibilities.

Example: The expectation that women should be caretakers and men should be providers, leading to gender-based inequalities in employment and pay.

5. Intersectionality: The interconnectedness of social identities such as race, gender, sexuality, and ability.

Example: A black trans woman who experiences discrimination and marginalization based on both her gender identity and race.
