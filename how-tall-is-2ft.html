13. a cube of ice is 2ft by 2ft by 2ft. the ice melts until it becomes a cube which is one-half as heavy as the original. find the dimensions of the new cube


Answer:

1.59 ft


Step-by-step explanation:

Volume = Length³

Volume = 2 x 2 x 2 = 8 ft³


Find the new volume:

New Volume = 8 ÷ 2 = 4 ft³


Find the new dimension:

Volume = Length³

Length³ = 4

Length = ∛4 ≈ 1.59 ft


Answer: The new cube is approximately 1.59 ft in length
