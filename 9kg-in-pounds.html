Calculating the Kinetic Energy

what is the kinetic energy of a 9kg book that has a speed of 4m/s?

FORMULA:

[tex] \boxed{ \tt{K.E = \frac{1}{2} mv^{2} }}[/tex]

WHERE :-

m = mass v = velocity

Kinetic Energy is articulated in Kgm²/s²

TO FIND :-

The Kinetic Energy

GIVEN :-

Mass, M = 9kgVelocity, V = 10 m/s

Multiply[tex] \tt \: K.E = \frac{1}{2} \times 9kg(4m/s)^{2} [/tex]

23. a 36 n net force acts on a 9kg rock. what ia the acceleration of the rock?​


SOLUTION:

Step 1: List the given values.

[tex]\begin{aligned} & F = \text{36 N} \\ & m = \text{9 kg} \end{aligned}[/tex]

Step 2: Calculate the acceleration

[tex]\begin{aligned} a & = \frac{F}{m} \\ & = \frac{\text{36 N}}{\text{9 kg}} \\ & = \boxed{\text{4 m/s}^2} \end{aligned}[/tex]

Hence, the acceleration of the rock is 4 m/s².
