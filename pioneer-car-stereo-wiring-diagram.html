3. a car accessory dealer wants to add a 9% mark up to the ₱33,000.00 cost a car stereo. what will be the selling price


Answer:
SP = 35,970

Step-by-step explanation:
SP = Selling Price
CP = Cost Price
P = Profit
SP = [tex]\frac{CP(100+P)}{100}[/tex]

SP = [tex]\frac{33,000(100+9)}{100}[/tex]
SP = 35,970
