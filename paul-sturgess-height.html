1. the average height of peter, paul, and james is 154 cm.if the sum of the heights of peter and paul is 305 cm, how tall is james?


Answer:

157 cm

Step-by-step explanation:

154 x 3 = 462 (Multiply the average height by 3 to get the sum of the heights of peter, paul and james.)

462-305 = 157 (Subtract the heights of peter and paul to 462 to get James' height.)

James is 157 centimeters tall.

Let P - Paul's height Let R - Rachel's height  Since their combined height is 94 inches, we can write it as P + R = 94  And since Rachel is 8 inches taller than Paul, it would be R = P + 8  By means of substitution: P + (P + 8) = 94 2P + 8 = 94 2P = 86 P = 43  Paul is 43 inches tall

Power

P = W / t

mass_1 = 50 kg sack

distance_1 = 0.3 meters

time_1 = 5 seconds

mass_2 = 20 kg sack

distance_2 = 0.5 meters

time_2 = 10 seconds

Work_1 = (50kg)(9.8m/s²)(0.3m)

Work_1 = 147 Nm

Work_2 = (20kg)(9.8m/s²)(0.5m)

Work_2 = 98 Nm

Who exerts more work?
