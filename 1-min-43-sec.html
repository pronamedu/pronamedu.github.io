21. find the result when 28 mins and 48 secs subracted by 12 mins and 44 secs??​


Answer:

16 minutes, 4 seconds

Step-by-step explanation:

Given:

28 minutes, 48 seconds12 minutes, 44 seconds

Change to minutes unit by converting seconds to minute where 1 minute = 60 seconds:

28 minutes, 48/60  minutes = 28  4/512 minutes, 44/60 minutes   =  12  11/15

Subtract:

(28  4/5) - (12  11/15)

Least common denominator (LCD): 15

(28  12/15) - (12  11/15)

= (28 - 12) + (12/15 - 11/15)

= 16 + 1/15

= 16 + 4/60  

= 16 minutes and 4 seconds.
