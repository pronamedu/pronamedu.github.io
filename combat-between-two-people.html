28. As a person of good moral character how can you be able to combat discrimination that is being experience of people particularly those who belong to LGBTQ community?​


Answer:

I can combat discrimination that is being experienced by people particularly those who belong to the LGBTIA+ community by educating myself about their community and by being a good ally to the community.

If I know their struggles, I can understand why there is a need to fight for their rights. In addition, by being a good ally, I can give my support to the members of the community which they need especially those who are not out because they are afraid of being disowned by their own families. I can support them by being there for them, and by fighting against or educating bullies.

In summary, the LGBTQIA+ community is discriminated against because people refused to educate themselves about the community. Hence, if I educate myself and become an ally, I can somehow contribute to their fight for equality and acceptance.
