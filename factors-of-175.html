14. 175° convert to revolution


Note: One revolution is equivalent to three hundred and sixty degrees ( 1 revolution = 360°)

So multiply 175° to 1 revolution/ 360° to get rid of the degree sign and the unit revolution will remain.

175° × 1 revolution/ 360°

= 175 revolution / 360
= 0.486 revolution (in decimal form)
