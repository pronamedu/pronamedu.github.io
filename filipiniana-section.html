a. Library

The library can be formulated as a work unit of an educational institution in the form of a storage place for a collection of library books to support the educational process.

From some of the definitions above, it can be concluded that the library is a place to develop information and knowledge that is managed by an educational institution, as well as an educational tool to help expedite the horizons of educators and students in teaching and learning activities.

In line with the times, the understanding of the library has changed gradually. In the beginning, every time there was a collection of books that were neatly and regularly managed, it was called a library, but due to the development of modern technology in the effort to preserve and develop information, the library collection was not only limited to books, but also various types.

4. what is the description of Filipiniana gown.asap po​


The Filipiniana gown is a formal outfit for women. It was only worn by mestizas during the Spanish colonial period as it combines the elements of both indigenous and Spanish designs. This formal outfit was popular during the Philippine-Spanish colonial era, especially in the 1890s.

Answer:

The Filipiniana gown has come a long way from its traditional and stiff design. Now you can find modern designs that are not only fashionable but also comfortable. This style has become the top choice of a fashion statement for many local personalities because of its classic silhouette and timeless elegance

24. what the filipiniana present​


Answer:

based on the definition by Isagani Medina from "Collection Building: Filipiniana", in his In Developing Special Library Collections, Filipiniana: Proceedings (November 1992), refers to Philippine-related books and non-book materials (such as Culture; figurines, games, fashion and others). The materials may be produced inside or outside the Philippines by Filipino or non-Filipino authors. The product could be literature written in any of the languages and dialects in the Philippines or a foreign language.
