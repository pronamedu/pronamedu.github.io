19. If the travel to Europe was dubbed as the journey to light why was the return to the Philippines was dubbed as the return to twilight


The travel to Europe was dubbed as the journey to light because it was an opportunity for Filipinos to gain knowledge, skills, and experiences that could help them improve their country. On the other hand, the return to the Philippines was dubbed as the return to twilight because of the stark contrast between the developed and progressive European countries and the still-developing and struggling Philippine society. Filipinos who returned from Europe often faced the challenge of bringing their newfound knowledge and skills back home to a society that was still struggling with poverty, corruption, and social injustice. The term "twilight" suggests a sense of uncertainty and darkness, as Filipinos faced the difficult task of trying to effect change and bring their country out of its state of underdevelopment
