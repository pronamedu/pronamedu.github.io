INGREDIENTS60 ml low-sodium soy sauce3 tbsp. honey2 cloves garlic, crushedJuice of 1 lime2 tbsp. sesame oil, divided1 tsp. Sriracha1 tbsp. cornflour450 g boneless skinless chicken breastsSaltFreshly ground black pepperSesame seeds, for garnishSliced spring onions, for garnish

DIRECTIONS

Preheat oven to 180°C. In a medium bowl, whisk together soy sauce, honey, garlic, lime juice, 1 tablespoon sesame oil, Sriracha, and cornflour.Season chicken with salt and pepper.

Bake until chicken is no longer pink, 25 minutes. Heat grill.
