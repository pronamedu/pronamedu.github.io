8. The sum of 110 500g and 3 500g is 114 000g. Since 1 kilogram (kg) is equal to 1000 grams (g), the total is 114 000g / 1000 = 114 kg.

9. Since 1 hectogram (hg) is equal to 100 grams (g), 1200g is equal to 1200g / 100 = 12 hg.

10. Since 1 decagram (dag) is equal to 10 grams (g), 4.20 dag is equal to 4.20 dag * 10 = 42 g. Alternatively, you can convert 4.20 dag to grams by multiplying it by 10 and moving the decimal point one place to the right: 4.20 dag * 10 = 42.0 g.
