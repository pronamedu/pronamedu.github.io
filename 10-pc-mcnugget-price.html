ANSWER

It looks like you are trying to calculate the markup and selling price for a product. Here is how you can do that using the information provided:

Calculate the markup amount: Markup = Purchase cost x Markup percentage = $1,000.00 x 50% = $500.00

Calculate the per item cost: Per item cost = Purchase cost + Markup = $1,000.00 + $500.00 = $1,500.00

Calculate the selling price: Selling price = Per item cost x Quantity = $1,500.00 x 50 pcs = $75,000.00

Calculate the percentage markup (cost): Percentage markup (cost) = Markup / Purchase cost = $500.00 / $1,000.00 = 50%

Calculate the percentage markup (selling price): Percentage markup (selling price) = Markup / Selling price = $500.00 / $75,000.00 = 0.67%

Therefore, the markup on the product is $500.00, the per item cost is $1,500.00, and the selling price is $75,000.00. The percentage markup on the cost is 50%, and the percentage markup on the selling price is 0.67%.

22. Compute the selling price, retail price and profit of the following. use 25% as mark-up price QUANTITY 12 pcs PRODUCT Coffee PRODUCT COST 84.00 Selling price ? Retail price ? Profit ?


Answer:

Selling price of the coffee is 105.00 pesos per piece

The retail price is 1,260.00 pesos for 12 pieces

The profit is 252.00 pesos for 12 pieces.

Step-by-step explanation:

To compute the selling price, retail price, and profit, we can use the following formulas:

Selling Price = Product Cost + (Product Cost x Mark-up Percentage)

Retail Price = Selling Price x Quantity

Profit = (Selling Price - Product Cost) x Quantity

Given:

Quantity = 12 pcs

Product = Coffee

Product Cost = 84.00

Mark-up Percentage = 25%

First, let's compute the mark-up price:

Mark-up Price = Product Cost x Mark-up Percentage

Mark-up Price = 84.00 x 0.25

Mark-up Price = 21.00

Selling Price = Product Cost + Mark-up Price

Selling Price = 84.00 + 21.00

Selling Price = 105.00

Retail Price = Selling Price x Quantity

Retail Price = 105.00 x 12

Retail Price = 1,260.00

Profit = (Selling Price - Product Cost) x Quantity

Profit = (105.00 - 84.00) x 12

Profit = 252.00

Therefore, the selling price of the coffee is 105.00 pesos per piece, the retail price is 1,260.00 pesos for 12 pieces, and the profit is 252.00 pesos for 12 pieces.
