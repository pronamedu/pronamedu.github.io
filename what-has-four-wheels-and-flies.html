14. A wheel has a radius of 20.4 feet what is the circumference of the wheel? (Circumference = 2rr)


CIRCUMFERENCE

==================================

PROBLEM

A wheel has a radius of 20.4 feet what is the circumference of the wheel?

SOLUTION

»You can solve this problem using the circumference formula in which π stands for ( 3.14 ), C stands for circumference itself and r stands for radius.

[tex]\bold{Formula \: || \: C = 2πr}[/tex]

'Now to find the circumference of the wheel where r is the radius of the circle that has a diameter,Substitute 20.4 for r in the formula.

[tex]\tt{C = 2πr}[/tex][tex]\tt{C = 2(3.14)(20.4\:feet)}[/tex][tex]\tt{C = 2(64.056\:feet) }[/tex][tex]\tt\green{C = 128.112}[/tex]

FINAL ANSWER

Thus, the circumference of a wheel that has a radius of 20.4 is 128.112 feet.
