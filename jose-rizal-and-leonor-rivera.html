6. What is your reflection (introduction, body and conclusion) to the poem "Goodbye to Leonor" by Jose rizal​


Answer:

Introduction:

"Goodbye to Leonor" is a poem written by Jose Rizal, a national hero of the Philippines. It tells the story of Rizal's farewell to his childhood sweetheart, Leonor Rivera, before he left for Europe to study. The poem is filled with emotion and longing, as Rizal expresses his love for Leonor and his sadness at having to leave her behind.

Body:

The poem "Goodbye to Leonor" is a beautiful expression of Rizal's love for Leonor. He uses vivid imagery and powerful metaphors to convey the depth of his feelings. For example, he compares his love for her to a "bird that sings in the night," expressing the idea that his love is a source of comfort and hope even in the darkest of times. He also uses the image of a ship sailing away to represent his departure, emphasizing the sense of loss and separation that he feels.

Throughout the poem, Rizal expresses his sadness at having to leave Leonor behind. He speaks of the "sorrowful tears" that he sheds, and the "painful sighs" that he lets out. He also expresses his hope that they will one day be reunited, saying that he will wait for her "wherever you are."

Conclusion:

"Goodbye to Leonor" is a poignant and heartfelt poem that captures the intense emotions of love and separation. It is a testament to the enduring power of Rizal's love for Leonor, and his deep longing to be reunited with her. Through his words, we are reminded of the importance of love and the pain of parting, and we are left with a sense of hope and longing for a future reunion.

9. bakit nanlamig si Leonor kay Jose Rizal?​


Answer:

Higit 100 taon na ang nakalilipas pero buhay na buhay pa rin ang mga muebles na ito na pag-aari ni Leonor Rivera sa Camiling, Tarlac.

Ayon sa mga naunang akda at sa mismong kuwento ng kanyang apo, si Leonor Rivera ang childhood sweetheart, ang first love at pinsan ng pambansang bayani na si Jose Rizal.

Si Leonor ang sinasabing inspirasyon ng tauhang si Maria Clara sa Noli Me Tangere.

“Noong si Rizal nag-aral ng kolehiyo sa Sto. Tomas, nakatira siya sa boarding house ng magulang ni Leonor. Noong panahon na iyon, si Leonor doon din nakatira, 13 lang yata siya noon eh. Nagdadalaga na siya noong niligawan siya ni Rizal. Naging sila for 11 years,” sabi ni Michael Charles Kipping, apo ni Leonor Rivera.

Pero alam nga nating hindi nagkatuluyan sina Leonor at Rizal.

Isang dahilan, ayon sa apo ni Leonor, ay ang pagtutol ng kanyang ina sa kanilang relasyon.

Paniwala ng nanay ni Leonor na hindi magiging maganda ang buhay niya sa piling ni Rizal lalo na noong panahong tinutugis na siya ng mga Kastila.

“Sa lahat ng mga sulat na ipinapadala ni Leonor kay Rizal at lahat ng sulat ng ipinapadala ni Rizal kay Leonor, in-intercept niya,” sabi ni Kipping.

Sa huli, nagpakasal si Leonor kay Henry Kipping, isa sa mga inhinyero ng Manila-Dagupan Railway habang si Rizal ay may mga ibang naging pag-ibig bago tuluyang naaresto at pinapatay sa Bagumbayan ng mga Kastila.

Si Leonor naman, namatay matapos manganak sa ikalawang sanggol na sinawing palad ding hindi nabuhay. Kori Quintos, Patrol ng Pilipino
