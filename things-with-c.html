18. it makes things clear how things work and why things happenA. DescriptionB. ExplanationC. Classificationplease answer my question​


B. Explanation

Further explanation:

Explanation Text

Explanation text is a text that contains the process of why and how a natural, scientific, social, cultural, and other event can occur.

Explanation Text makes it clear how something works and why something happens.

The purpose of explanation text is to describe one or more events involving causality and process. Here, the writer must explain the incident as clearly as possible so that the reader can gather important information from what is being explained.

Explanatory text has the following language characteristics, namely:

Focus on general (generic). This text also explains about natural phenomena or social events.

Use more material and relational verbs.

Use the conjunction of time and causal for example if, when, so, before, first, and then.

Use the passive voice.

Using scientific terms.

The language is concise, interesting, and clear.

Explanation of other answers:

A. Description

Descriptive text is a type of writing that explains and describes a certain thing, object and situation. The description can be interpreted as a data processing rule, making it something that can be stated clearly and precisely. To then be easily understood by others.

C. Classification

Classification texts are also often referred to as observation texts, because they contain various types of classifications based on their criteria. The nature of this text is factual, because it is based on facts. The resulting data is stated to be true and false observation reports are not recommended to be used in the preparation of observation texts.
