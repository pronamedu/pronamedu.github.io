Mr. Reyes deposited $80,000 in the bank, if the bank gives him an interest rate of 3% every year. What is the amount of interest he will get after 4 years? 3. A restaurant charges 10% of your bill for service fee. How much will be the service fee of a bill amounting to $1,500? 4. Last year, 50 pupils were enrolled in Grade VI. This year, only 42 pupils enrolled at the said grade. What is the percent of decrease in the enrolment of Grade VI? 5. Nina is a sales agent and she makes money by commission rates, she gets 20% of everything she sells. If she sold 125, 000 worth of items for the month of June, P100,000 for the month of July and 105,000 for the month of August, what is the total of her salary for 3 months​


Answer:

1. Anna bought a washing machine for ℗ 8,000 and a television for ℗ 25,000 for a total of ℗ 8,000 + ℗ 25,000 = ℗ 33,000. She was given a discount of 15% on both items, so she saved 15/100 x ℗ 33,000 = ℗ 4,950. After the discount, she paid ℗ 33,000 - ℗ 4,950 = ℗ 28,050.

2. Mr. Reyes deposited $80,000 in the bank and the bank gives an interest rate of 3% per year. After 4 years, he will earn an interest of 4 x 3/100 x $80,000 = $9,600.

3. A restaurant charges a service fee of 10% of your bill. For a bill amounting to $1,500, the service fee is 10/100 x $1,500 = $150.

4. Last year, 50 pupils were enrolled in Grade VI and this year, only 42 pupils enrolled in the grade. The percent of decrease in enrolment is (50-42)/50 x 100% = 16%.

5. Nina is a sales agent and she makes money by commission, getting 20% of everything she sells. In June, she sold items worth $125,000 and earned $125,000 x 20/100 = $25,000. In July, she sold items worth $100,000 and earned $100,000 x 20/100 = $20,000. In August, she sold items worth $105,000 and earned $105,000 x 20/100 = $21,000. In total, she earned $25,000 + $20,000 + $21,000 = $66,000 for the three months.
