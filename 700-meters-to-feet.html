15. Answer the following routine and non-routine problem. d 49. A submarine was situated 700 feet below sea level. If it ascended 250 feet, what was its new position? ​


Answer:

The new position of the submarine would be at 450 feet below sea level.

To find out, you can start with the initial position of the submarine at 700 feet below sea level, and then add the distance it ascended (250 feet) to find its new position.

700 feet (initial position) + 250 feet (distance ascended) = 450 feet (new position)

26. a world record holder ran 700 meters in 74 seconds. What was his average speed?​


Answer:

To find the average speed of the world record holder, we need to use the formula:

average speed = distance ÷ time

The distance covered by the runner is 700 meters, and the time taken to cover this distance is 74 seconds. Substituting these values in the formula, we get:

average speed = 700 meters ÷ 74 seconds

Simplifying the expression on the right-hand side, we get:

average speed = 9.46 meters per second (rounded to two decimal places)

Therefore, the world record holder's average speed was 9.46 meters per second
