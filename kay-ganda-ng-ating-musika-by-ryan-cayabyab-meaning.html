2. reflection of the song kay ganda ng ating musika by ryan cayabyab​


Answer:

Kay Ganda ng Ating Musika, 1978

Music and lyrics, National Artist Ryan Cayabyab

The song was first performed by Hajji Alejandro in the First Metro Manila Popular Music Festival in 1978 where it won the grand prize. Acclaimed as an opportune piece of musical creation, the song exalts the emergence of Filipino nationalism in music. It describes in metaphorical language, the beautiful experience of discovering the gift of song.
