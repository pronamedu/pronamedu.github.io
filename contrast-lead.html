SIMILARITIES

Both bacteria and protists have cell membranes made of chemicals called phospholipids.

B.DIFFERENCE

FUNGI: Fungi are heterotrophs.

SIMILARITIES

FUNGI: Fungi, singular fungus, are eukaryotes that are characterized by the presence of chitin in the cell wall.
