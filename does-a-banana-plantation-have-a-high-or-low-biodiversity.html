7. which ecosystem have high biodiversity ? low biodiversity


Biodiversity is a measure of how many different species live in an ecosystem. Ecosystems with high biodiversity have different species of organisms and it is said that it makes the ecosystem more stable. While low biodiversity don’t have that many species, it is also said that small changes can cause many species to die; it’s unstable.

Low Biodiversity : desert

High Biodiversity : rain forest
