13. is evaporation good for separating a mixture of coconut oil and water​


Answer:

nope.

Explanation:

You can separate a mixture of coconut oil, salt and water by:-

1. To separate the coconut oil from the salt water you can use the method of sedimentation and decantation as water is denser than oil. So oil will float on the top and then you can separate it.

2. to separate salt from the water you can boil or evaporate the water and the salt will be left behind as a solid. If you want to collect the water, you can use distillation. This works because salt has a much higher boiling point than water.
