Proportion:

3:5 = 15:n

Solve for n:

15/n = 3/5, multiply both sides by n

3n/5 = 15, multiply both sides by 5

3n = 75, divide both sides by 3

n = 25

Answer: If Roy sold 15 newspapers, Nel sold 25 newspapers.
