24. 3. What can you say about the city of Las Piñas​


Answer:

Las Piñas City is a city in Metro Manila in the Philippines. It is bordered to the north and northeast by Parañaque City; to the east and southeast of Muntinlupa City; south of the Municipality of Imus, Cavite; to the southwest and west of the Municipality of Bacoor, Cavite; and southwest of Manila Bay. Half of the land area is residential while the other half is commercial, industrial and institutional. The current physiography of Las Piñas consists of three zones: Manila Bay, Coastal Margin and Guadalupe Plateau. According to the 2020 census, it has a population of 606,293 out of 137,897 households
