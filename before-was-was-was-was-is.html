B. BEFORE CHRIST

Ang natitirang gamit ng BC/AD kumbensyon ay ang Carthaginian bishop Victor ng Tunnuna (namatay noong AD 570). Ginamit din ang BC/AD ng mga britong monghe na tinatawag na "Venerable Bede," pagkatapos ng Victor's death. Ang kumbensyon na BC/AD ay nasimulan noong una at ikalawang sentenaryo ng AD.

4. people before process Customer before policy​


"People before process" and "Customer before policy" are two important principles that emphasize the importance of prioritizing the needs and well-being of individuals over rigid rules and procedures.

"People before process" means that the focus should be on the people involved in a particular situation, rather than on following a set process or procedure. This principle recognizes that each person is unique, and that their individual circumstances and needs must be taken into account in order to provide the best possible outcome.

Similarly, "Customer before policy" emphasizes the importance of prioritizing the needs and satisfaction of customers over strict policies or rules. This principle recognizes that customers are the lifeblood of any business or organization, and that meeting their needs and expectations is essential for long-term success.

Both of these principles prioritize the human element and emphasize the importance of empathy, understanding, and flexibility in dealing with people and situations. By putting people and customers first, organizations can build stronger relationships, foster loyalty, and ultimately achieve greater success.
