9. what kind of oil use for automatic transmission fluid​


Answer:

Commonly recommended manual transmission fluid formulations include SAE 80W, 75W-90, 80W-90 and SAE 90. In some cases, a multi-grade engine oil or automatic transmission fluid may be recommended. However, a manual transmission fluid is typically the most optimal for manual transmissions.

The vast majority of passenger car and light truck transmissions operate automatically, that is without the need to manually shift gears. Automatic transmission fluid is a critical component of any automatic transmission. Also referred to as ATF, this fluid ensures proper operation, performance and protection of the automatic transmission. Critical areas of performance include the following:

Heat resistance and thermal stability, helps prevent deposit and sludge from forming as the result of the high temperature operation of the transmission

Frictional Characteristics enables smooth gear changes by supplying the correct friction to the transmission clutches and bands

Extreme pressure/load carrying performance ensures anti-wear and gear durability

Viscosity stability helps maintain viscosity or proper fluid thickness over a wide temperature range

Low temperature flow ensures effective operation of hydraulics and electronics controls at low temperature

ENVIRONMENTAL AWARENESS AND EDUCATION ACT 2008

Republic Act No. 9512 or the National Environmental Awareness and Education Act of 2008 is a law that discusses the government's policy of defending and advancing the people's right to a healthy and balanced environment in accordance with nature's rhythms and harmony, as well as social progress, and promoting total human liberation and development.

As a result of this law, environmental education must be integrated into school curricula at all levels, whether public or private, including barangay daycare, pre-school and elementary schools, in coordination with the Department of Environment and Natural Resources, the Department of Science and Technology, and other relevant agencies, as well as in the Department of Social Welfare and Development's curricula at the Department of Social Welfare and Development's curricula at all levels, whether public or private.

This course will include both theoretical and practical modules that will cover things like tree planting, waste reduction, segregation, recycling and composting; freshwater and marine conservation; forest management and conservation; relevant livelihood and economic benefits, and other things that will help with the implementation of various environmental laws.

In addition, the law declares November to be "Environmental Awareness Month" in all of the Philippines every year.
