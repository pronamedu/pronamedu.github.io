28. Among a group of boys, 70% like chocolate ice cream, 40% like strawberry ice cream, and 30% like both. If a boy is randomly selected from the group, what is the probability that he likes either chocolate or strawberry ice cream, but not both?​


Answer:

70% like chocolate

40% like strawberry

30% like both

 

Students who like only chocolate = (70-30) = 40%

Students who like only strawberry = (40-30) = 10%

 

Probability that a random student picked likes chocolate or strawberry = 40% + 10% = 50%

30. In other words or meaning = “To a boy his age, the world is strange, hostile, terrifying. He needs a home, he needs you, he needs me– not a huge house like this….”


To a boy his age, the world is strange, hostile, terrifying. He needs a home, he needs you, he needs me– not a huge house like this.

From the context, this shows that the boy being talked about is still a child–young and defenseless from the snares of worldly things and reality.

What he needs are people to guide him and help him grow inorder to face the future. What he needs isn't a big house but people who will love and support him from his growth–a place he can belong to, a home.

The greatest things in life are definitely not things, but people.
