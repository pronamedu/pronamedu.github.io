10. Why are 24k gold, silver, and platinum pieces of jewelry not easily tarnished by sweat?


Answer

It is because oxygen does not have a strong chemical reaction against 24k silver, gold, platinum.

Explanation

A 24-karat precious metal means that the element is pure, meaning, the contents of the platinum, silver, or gold are at 100%.  Pure or 24k silver, gold, or platinum does not easily combine with oxygen, which is a very active element that causes elements like iron to rust. So, instead of rust, only a thin layer of the 24-karat metal develops a patina reaction.

17. factor of 12km³-24k³m²-36k²m⁴​


The factor of that polynomial is: 12km².

To get the greatest common monomial factor of a polynomial, you must get the:

a. GCF of the numerical coefficients

b. Variable/s with the least exponent that appears in each term

Let's start solving!

a. GCF of the numerical coefficients

12 = 1 x 12

      2 x 6

      3 x 4

24 = 1 x 24

       2 x 12

       3 x 8

       4 x 6

36= 1 x 36

      2 x 18

      3 x 12

      4 x 9

      6 x 6

b. Variable/s with the least exponent that appears in each term

km²

c. Product of (a) and (b)

=12km²

So, our GMCF is 12km².
