22. A force og 720 N is needed to accelerate a car from rest to 160km/h 3s second on a level road .what Isa the mass of the car ?​


[tex]\tt{\huge{\blue{Explanation:}}}[/tex]

The force acting on an object is given by

[tex]\boxed{F = ma}[/tex]

where:

F = force

m = mass

a = acceleration

[tex]\tt{\huge{\red{Solution:}}}[/tex]

Listing the given values

vf = 160 km/h = 44.4444 m/s

vi = 0

t = 3 s

F = 720 N

Solving for a

[tex]a = \dfrac{v_{f} - v_{i}}{t}[/tex]

[tex]a = \dfrac{\text{44.4444 m/s} - 0}{\text{3 s}}[/tex]

a = 14.8148 m/s²

Solving for m

F = ma

[tex]\dfrac{F}{a} = \dfrac{ma}{a}[/tex]

[tex]m = \dfrac{F}{a}[/tex]

[tex]m = \dfrac{\text{720 N}}{\text{14.8148 m/}\text{s}^{2}}[/tex]

[tex]\boxed{m = \text{48.60 kg}}[/tex]

Therefore, the mass of the car is 48.60 kg.
