Digit 2 called Hundredths

Digit 5 called Thousandths

125 | 1000

÷ 5 → 25 | 200

÷ 5 → 5 | 40

÷ 5 → 1 | 8

The GCF is 5³ or 125.

Fractions

Fractions represent the parts of a whole. It has two parts, numerator and denominator. The numerator is the number on the top of the line. It tells the number of equal parts taken. Then the denominator is the number below the line. It tells the total number of equal parts. Fractions are expressed with numbers, however, we can also write them in words.

Going back to the given above, we have to write it in fraction. To do that, identify the parts of the fraction first.

"one hundred twenty-five thousandths"

thousandths - denominator
