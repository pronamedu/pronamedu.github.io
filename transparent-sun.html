11. a bag contains 128 marbles some white and some transparent the ratio of white marbles to transparent ones is 3:5 how many transparent marbles are there


Answer:

80 transparent marbles

Step-by-step explanation:

Step 1: Calculate the total from the given ratio: 3+5 =8

Step 2: multiply the given total amount of marbles to the ratio of transparent marbles over the total from the given ratio

[tex]128(\frac{5}{8})[/tex] = 80 transparent marbles

therefore, the remaining are white marbles: 128 -80 = 48

to check: get the lowest term of the number of white marbles over transparent marbles and check if it is the same with the given ratio

48/80= 3/5 ↔3:5

30. wood isn't transparent or transparent or transparent​


Answer:

Wood is not transparent, it is opaque. Light cannot pass through it so it is opaque.

Additional Info:

Transparent - Light can easily pass through in a transparent materials.

Translucent - Light can pass through in a translucent object but not all of the light can pass through it.

Opaque - Light cannot pass through an opaque materials.
