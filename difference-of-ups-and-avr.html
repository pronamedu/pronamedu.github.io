1. give the difference of avr and ups​


Answer:

Both are entirely different things. An automatic voltage regulator also called AVR is used on an AC generator to keep its voltage constant. A UPS is a system to supply electricity to an office or home when there is no supply from grid. A UPS ( un interrupted power supply) consists of batteries and an inverter.

4. why is there a need for AVR and UPS in the computer setup?​


Answer:

What is AVR?

AVR means Automated Voltage Regulator. AVR is a hardware device used to maintain a voltage to electronic devices.

What is UPS?

UPS means Uninterruptible power supply. It provides emergency power to a load when the input power source or mains power fails.

Example:

When there's no electricity or brownout, UPS can save you from PC being shutdown.

11. function like an AVR​


An automatic voltage regulator (AVR) is a solid state electronic device for automatically maintaining generator output terminal voltage at a set value. It will try and do this as the generator load or operating temperature changes. The AVR is part of the alternators excitation system.
