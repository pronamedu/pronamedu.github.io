30. George is 8 years more than Christopher and Ford is 2 years younger than Christopher. The sum of their ages is 60. Find the ages of George, Christopher and Ford.


Answer:

Christopher (x) = 18 years old

Ford (x - 2) = 16 years old

George (x + 8) = 26 years old

Step-by-step explanation:

Let x, x - 2, x + 8 be their ages

x + (x - 2) + (x + 8) = 60

Combine like terms

3x + 6 = 60

Transpose 6 and change its sign

3x = 60 - 6

Subtract

3x = 54

Divide both sides by 3

3x/3 = 54/3

x = 18

Christopher is 18 years old

x - 2

18 - 2 = 16

Ford is 16 years old

x + 8

18 + 8 = 26

George is 26 years old

Checking:

x + (x - 2) + (x + 8) = 60

18 + (18 - 2) + (18 + 8) = 60

18 + 16 + 26 = 60

60 = 60

TRUE
