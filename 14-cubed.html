1. Every cube has 6 faces. 14 similar cubes have a total surface area of 756 cm². Find the total volume of these 14 cubes.


Answer: 378 Cubic CM


Step-by-step explanation:

1. Solving for the TSA of each cube:

756cm^2 ÷ 14 = 54cm^2


2. Area of each faces:

54cm^2 ÷ 6 = 9cm^2


3. Get the side of the cube:

√9cm^2 = 3cm


4. Find the volume of 1 cube:

3cm^3 (Volume formula for cube: S^3)

27 cubic cm


5. Find the total volume of 14 cubes:

27 cubic cm × 14

Answer is 378 cubic cm

8. A uniform pressure of 7.0 × 105 n/m2 is applied to all six sides of a copper cube. what is the percentage change in volume of the cube? (for copper, b = 14 × 1010 n/m2 )


BULK MODULUS

If a uniform pressure of 7.0 x [tex]10^5[/tex] N/[tex]m^2[/tex] is applied to all six sides of a copper cube, the percentage change in volume of the cube if the bulk modulus for copper is 14 x [tex]10^{10}[/tex] N/[tex]m^2[/tex] is 0.0005%

How do we calculate it?

First, we are given the value of the bulk modulus and the uniform pressure applied to the copper cube.If we think of the formula: B = -V [tex]\frac{dP}{dV}[/tex], we can say that the fractional change in volume is equal to the pressure applied to the object over the bulk modulus.

Solving for

% change in volume = [tex]\frac{P}{B}[/tex] = [tex]\frac{7 x 10^5}{14x10^{10}}[/tex] = 5 x [tex]10^6[/tex] (100%) = 0.0005%
