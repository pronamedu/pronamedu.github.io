26. R={month/s of the year starts in letter J​


Answer:

R={January, June, July}

Answer and step-by-step explanation:

The given is in set-builder notation. It is describing the element inside the set.

Then let's convert the given into a roster notation. We will put the elements inside the curly brackets and separate them with commas.

The condition "month/s of the year starts in letter J" should be followed so we have: January, June and July.

Therefore, the roster notation is:

R = {January, June, July}
