12. Find the 85th term in the number sequence. 2, 6, 10, 14, 18,​


Answer:

The general (nth) term for 2, 6, 10, 14, 18, 22, … is 4 and the first term is 2. If we let d=4 this becomes an=a1+(n−1)d. The nth or general term of an arithmetic sequence is given by an=a1+(n−1)d. So in our example a1=2 and d=4 so an=2+(n−1)4=2+4n−4=4n−2.

29. Which of the following is equivalent to the lower quartile?A.1st quartileB.5th decileC.75th percentileD.85th percentile​


Question:

Which of the following is equivalent to the lower quartile?

Choices:

A. 1st quartile

B. 5th decile

C. 75th percentile

D. 85th percentile

Answer:

A. 1st quartile

Explanation:

1st quartile is equivalent to the lower quartile. The first quartile (or lower quartile), Q1, is defined as the value that has an f-value equal to 0.25. This is the same thing as the twenty-fifth percentile. The third quartile (or upper quartile), Q3, has an f-value equal to 0.75. The interquartile range, IQR, is defined as Q3-Q1.
