5. 3. The hypotenuse of a triangle is 51 and the one side is 45, what is the area of the triangle?​


Answer:

540 sq. units

Step-by-step explanation:

Pythagorean Theorem:

c² = a² + b²

Area of triangle:

[tex]A = \frac{1}{2} ab[/tex]

where:

c = hypotenuse

a & b = legs

------------

51² = 45² + b²

b² = 576

b = √576

b = 24

[tex]A = \frac{1}{2} (45)(24)[/tex]

[tex]A = 540 sq. units[/tex]
