Paseo de bicol originates at Oas, Albay. It is commonly known as Paseo de Roxas. Paseo means walking. It is also known as Aurora Festival in Bicol, it is a festival where the adults go to a "capilya" and pray to remove the bad luck in their place, while teenagers dance around the capilya.
