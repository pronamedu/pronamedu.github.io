3. the length of the rectangular basketball court is 6 feet less than twice its width and the area is equal to 165 square feet.​


Answer:

Length = 15.42 ft, width = 10.71 ft

Step-by-step explanation:

Area of a Rectangle

The area of a rectangle can be calculated by the formula:

A = l x w

where l is the length while w is the width.

In this problem, we are given the relationship of the length and the width and the area of the rectangle of 165 square feet. We need to calculate the length and the width of the rectangle.

Step 1: Translate the mathematical statement given into a working mathematical equation.

Statement: The length is 6 feet less than twice its width

Equation 1: l = 2w - 6

Step 2: Substitute the given value of the area and the derived equation 1 to the formula.

A = l x w

165 [tex]ft^{2}[/tex] = (2w - 6) x w

165 [tex]ft^{2}[/tex]  = 2[tex]w^{2}[/tex] - 6w

2[tex]w^{2}[/tex] - 6w - 165 [tex]ft^{2}[/tex] = 0

Step 3: Notice that our resulting equation is in a form of a quadratic expression. Use the quadratic formula to solve for the value of w.

w = [tex]\frac{-b +- \sqrt{b^{2} - 4ac } }{2a}[/tex]

where a = 2, b = -6 and c = -165

Substitute the given constants to the formula.

w = [tex]\frac{-(-6) + \sqrt{(-6)^{2} - (4 x 2 x -165) } }{2 x 2}[/tex]

w = 10.71 ft

w = [tex]\frac{-(-6) - \sqrt{(-6)^{2} - (4 x 2 x -165) } }{2 x 2}[/tex]

w = -7.7

We have two resulting values of w, we will use the positive root.

Step 4: Substitute the value of w to equation 1 to get the value of l.

l = 2w - 6

l = (2 x 10.71) - 6

l = 15.42 ft

Therefore, the length of the rectangle is 15.42 ft while the width is 10.71 ft.

7. The dimensions of the floor of a room is 165 m by 72 m. It is to be covered by tiles of dimen- sion 150 cm by 90 cm. Find how many tiles will be required to cover the entire floor.


Answer:

88 Tiles

Step-by-step explanation:

Let's first get the area of the floor :

A = lw

A = (165)(72)

A = 11880

Area of the floor is 11,880m²

Second let's get the area of the tiles

A = lw

A = (150)(90)

A = 13500

Area of the tiles is 13,500cm²

To get how many tiles will be used Divide the area of the floor by the area of the tiles. let x be the number of tiles.

But before we proceed, the unit of measurement needs to be the same. It's either we convert m to cm or convert cm to m. For the sake of simplicity we'll convert m to cm.

11,880m² = 1,188,000cm²

Now we can divide.

x = 1,188,000/13,500

x = 88

Thus 88 Tiles are needed
