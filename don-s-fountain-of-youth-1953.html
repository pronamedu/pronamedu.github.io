4. the circular fountain in the garden has a diameterof 20m. what is the area of a circular fountain?​


Answer:

Area of a Circle

===========================================

[tex]\blue{{\tt{Answer:}}}[/tex]

314 m²

[tex]\blue{{\tt{Formula:}}}[/tex]

A = πr²

Where,

A = area

π = 3.14

r = radius

[tex]\blue{{\tt{Formula:}}}[/tex]

(note: the given is diameter so we need to change it into radius to find the area of the circle)

given: diameter of 20 m

solve for the radius:

r = d/2

r = 20/2

r = 10 m

solve for the area of the circle:

A = πr²

A = (3.14)(10 m)²

A = (3.14)(100 m²)

A = 314 m²

[tex]\boxed{Area = 314 m²}[/tex]

[tex]\bold{A = ¼πd²}[/tex]

» [tex]\large\rm\purple{Then}[/tex], you know what is the formula and the diameter? Let's move to solving.

[tex]\bold{A = ¼πd²}[/tex][tex]\bold{A = ¼ 3.14 × 20²}[/tex][tex]\bold{A = ¼ 3.14 × 400²}[/tex][tex]\bold{A = ¼ 1,256²}[/tex][tex]\bold{A = 314²}[/tex]
