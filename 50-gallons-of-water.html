26. A gallon of water weighs 4 quarts. How much does 5 gallons of water weigh in quarts?


[tex]\blue{••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••}[/tex]

[tex]\large{ \red{ \underline{ \purple{ \sf{Question:}}}}}[/tex]

A gallon of water weighs 4 quarts. How much does 5 gallons of water weigh in quarts?

[tex]\large{ \red{ \underline{ \pink{ \sf{Answer:}}}}}[/tex]

20 quarts

[tex]\large{ \red{ \underline{ \green{ \sf{Solution:}}}}}[/tex]

» Let N be the missing value of the weigh of the gallons in quarts and solve for the N.

[tex] \frac{1}{4} = \frac{5}{N} [/tex]

» Apply the cross multiplication, multiply 1 by N and multiply 4 by 5.

(1) (N) = (4) (5)N = 20

Therefore, the value of N Is 20, the 5 gallons weigh 20 quarts.
