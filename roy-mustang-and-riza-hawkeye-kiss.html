24. Six years ago, Nilda was five times as old as Riza. In five years, Nilda will be three times as old as Riza. What is the present age of Riza?


Explanation:

6 years ago, Nilda was 5 times as old as Riza. in 5 years, Nilda will be 3 times as old as Riza. what is the present age of Riza?

Equations:

(n-6) = 5(r-6)

(n+5) = 3(r+5)

Rearrange:

n = 5r-30+6

n = 3r+15-5

Substitute for n and solve for "r":

5r-24 = 3r+10

2r = 34

r = 17 (Riza's age now)

solve for "n":

n = 5r-24

n = 5*17-24

n = 85-24

n = 61 (Nilda's age now)
