9. Instructions. List down the distinct feature of the followingFamous Landmarks using the T-Chart. Write it on a short bondpaper.Three Kings parish churchAbucay churchTumauini ChurchSpanish Gate​


Answer:

Tumauiniis an ultra-baroque church known for its extensive use of red bricks on its exterior and interior ornamentations. Brick was used due to lack of good quality stones in the area.

Abucay ChurchThe two-level facade of the church is described as of Renaissance style. Its expanse is divided vertically by single or coupled Doric columns.

Three Kings Parish ChurchIt is one of the oldest Roman Catholic churches in the country, and the oldest and the biggest colonial church in Nueva Ecija. The church has been a pilgrimage site for two patron saints of Gapan and also of Nueva Ecija; the Three Kings, and the Divina Pastora (Divine Shepherdess). On April 26, 1986, the Catholic Bishops Conference of the Philippines declared the church as a National Shrine.

Spanish GateA high wall of locally quarried stone connected it to the south gate that faced the waterfront. In addition to being used as the main entrance and exit to the naval station, the gate was also used as a jail during both the Spanish and the American occupation
