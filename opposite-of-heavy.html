7. a boy and a girl pull the heavy box at the same time in opposite direction with 10units and 5 units of force.what in the net force?​


*The unit of force is Newton, N.

Boy = 10 N

Girl = 5 N

Net force = F1 + F2 = Fn

Opposite direction:

10 N ------> Fn <-------- 5 N

Fn = 10 N + (-5 N) = 5 N, East

or

5 N -------> Fn <------- 10 N

Fn = 5 N + (-10 N) = -5 N, West

**The signs indicate the direction of the force, or which side is grater.

Note:

Net force is unbalanced Force. It can make the object at rest to move or it can make the moving object to stop.

We will deduct 5 N force to the its opposite force which is 10 N and the result will be the net force plus its direction.

Solution:

25. what will be the net force on the object? if the boy and girl pull the heavy crate at the same timer in opposite directions with 10 units and 5 units of force


Given:
            10 units of force in a direction
             5 units of force applied in a direction opposite to the previous 10 units of force

Asked:   What will be the net force on the object?
Solution:
               Greater amount of force - lesser amount of force = net force with direction
            10 units - 5 units = 5 units of force in the direction of the greater force
Warning: ( if you will only answer 5 units of force, it is considered wrong, there should always be a direction, if it is to the left or south, it has a negative sign, otherwise, positive sign)

Answer:
           5 units of force in the direction of the greater force

26. what if a boy and girl pull a heavy crate at the same time in opposite direction with 10 N and 5 N respectively ? what will be the net force on the object ?​


Answer:

The net force of the object will be 5 units to the direction of the greater force. Yes, the object will move.

Net force = 5 and move towards the boy

Further explanation

Net force: combination/sum of all forces acting on an object

If two or more forces are in the same direction, then you add the forces

If two or more forces in the opposite direction, then you subtract the forces

The boy and girl pull the heavy crate in opposite directions with 10 units and 5 units

Net force = 10 - 5 = 5 units

Because unbalanced forces occur, the object will move in accordance with the direction of motion of a larger force that is the direction of motion of the boy (has a force greater than the girl)

The object will not move if forces are balanced (net force = 0)
