16. The needs to know the short and long vowel sounds?


What determines whether a vowel is short or long?

It is a brief sound when a vowel creates the sound of a certain letter. The vowel, on the other hand, generates a lengthy sound when it sounds like the letter's name. The vowel's sound is determined by its placement in the word and the letters that surround it.

Answer:
