16. invention of syringes​


Answer:

SYRINGE

A syringe is a simple reciprocating pump consisting of a plunger that fits tightly within a cylindrical tube called a barrel. The plunger can be linearly pulled and pushed along the inside of the tube, allowing the syringe to take in and expel liquid or gas through a discharge orifice at the front end of the tube.

WHEN WAS THE SYRINGE IVENTED?

1853

1853The first devices recognizable as hypodermic syringes were independently invented virtually simultaneously in 1853 by Scottish physician Alexander Wood and French surgeon Charles Gabriel Pravaz. These were first used to inject morphine as a painkiller.

23. A gas syringe at 20°C contains 20.0 mL of CO2 gas. The pressure of the gas in the syringe is 1.0 atm. What is the pressure in the syringe at 20°C if the plunger is depressed to 10.0 mL?


Answer:

2atm

Explanation:

Data obtained from the question include:

T1=20°C=20 + 273= 293k

V1=20.0 ML

P1=1atm

T2=20°C= 20 + 273= 293k

V2=10 ML

P2= ?

Combined Gas LawP1V1/T1 = P2V2/T2

where in, P1 = first pressure

V1 = first volume

T1 = first temperature

P2 = second pressure

V2 = second volume

T2 = second temperature

Given: P1 = 1.0atm

V1 = 20.0mL

T1 = 293.15K

V2 = 10.0mL

T2 = 293.15K

Note: Dealing with temperature from each of the gas laws must always be in Kelvin(K), that’s why conversion is a must.

°C → K

20°C + 273.15K = 293.15K

Unknown: P2 = ?

Formula: P2 = P1V1T2 / V2T1

Note: To get the needed formula, apply cross-multiplication, division and cancellation of like terms using the original formula of Combined Gas Law.

• P1V1/T1 = P2V2/T2 ← cross multiply

• P1V1T2 = P2V2T1 ← divide both sides

by V2T1

• P1V1T2/V2T1 = P2V2T1/V2T1 ← cancel

like terms

• P1V1T2/V2T1 = P2 ← final formula

Solution:

P2 = P1V1T2 / V2T1

= (1atm)(20mL)(293.15K) / (10mL)

(293.15K)

= 5863atm•mL•K / 2931.5mL•K

= 2atm

Note: Unit for pressure is atmosphere(atm) (sometimes Pascal or Kilopascal etc.) therefore unnecessary units are cancelled out.

Tip: Cancellation of like terms or units is applicable when the other same term or unit is on the denominator.

Final Answer: P2 = 2atm
