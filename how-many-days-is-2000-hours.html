21. A certain machine can print 8000 leaflets in an hour. At that rate how many minutes would it take to print 2000 leaflets?


Answer:

15 minutes

Step-by-step explanation:

Equation: W = rt

W = workr = ratet = time

Given that:

W = 8000 leaflets , r = ? , t = 1 hour or 60 mins

First , we will solve for the rate

W = rt8,000 = r(60)8,000 ÷ 60 = r400/3 = r

Then , we will solve for the time

W = 2,000 , r = 400/3 , t = ?

W = rt2,000 = 400/3(t)2,000 ÷ 400/3 = t2,000 × 3/400 = t15 = t

Therefore , it will took 15 minutes
