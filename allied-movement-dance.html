9. Allied arts in filmmaking


Answer

Allied arts or Combined arts is the term used when referring to different Art forms joined together to create another Art form. For FIlm making, these are the field of Arts involved:

Visual Art - This refers to Cinematography in which the Director artistically envisions how the film should be shown. It involves compositing of visual effects, costumes and makeup, arrangements of visual elements to be shown on the camera, and other artistic elements that are visible to the camera.

Performance Art - This refers to acting for the camera, which is very different from acting for an audience in a theatre. Theatre acting involves exaggerated facial expressions and gestures because the performance was on stage. There was no support of cameras to see acting and what emotion was being portrayed. While in movie acting, the composition of the film affects the actor's gesture and facial expression.

Literature Art - This refers to written works of art like the story, scripts, storyboard, and screenplay.

Audio Art - This refers to Musical Art that involves Background music, Ambient Music, Sound Effects, Dialogue, and Composed Music.

13. allied health assisstants


Answer:

Allied Health Assistants pertain to health professionals who work under the direct supervision of health care professionals such as doctors, nurses, and dentists. Basically, they help health care professionals to provide therapeutic and program-related support to patients. They can be in: occupational therapy, physiotherapy, podiatry, speech pathology, nutrition and dietetics, and community rehabilitation.

Below are some of the professions which are classified as Allied Health:

Audiologist Chiropractor Dietitian Exercise Physiologist Medical Imaging Professionals (e.g. radiographers, sonographers, radiologists) Occupational Therapist Osteopath Orthotist Perfusionist Pharmacist Physiotherapist Podiatrist Psychologist Social Worker Speech Pathologist

These allied health professionals work in different settings such as hospitals, community health centers, mental health centers, private practice, care homes, universities, schools etc.

Whether health care professionals or allied health assistants, they are all important in promoting the overall well-being of the people.

21. What directly led to the German armistice? German and Allied forces fought to a stalemate. Allied armies made significant advances into Germany. Allied forces were weakened by countries breaking apart. German forces were strengthened by more soldiers from allies.


Answer:

The correct answer is "German and Allied forces fought to a stalemate."

The German Armistice of November 11, 1918, which effectively ended World War I, was the result of a stalemate between the German and Allied forces. After four years of war, neither side was able to achieve a decisive victory. The German army had suffered significant losses, both in terms of manpower and resources, and was unable to continue fighting. At the same time, the Allied forces had also suffered heavy losses and were unable to make significant progress towards victory. The German government, led by Chancellor Max von Baden, sought an end to the war and negotiated the armistice with the Allies. The armistice effectively ended the fighting and led to the eventual signing of the Treaty of Versailles, which officially ended the war.
