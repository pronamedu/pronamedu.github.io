7. What is the velocity of a 500kg stuck that has 400000j of energy?


Velocity is 40 m/s

Explanation:

Step 1: Enumerate the given values.

Given:

m = 500 kg

KE = 400,000 J

Step 2: Identify what is being asked.

Required:

Velocity

Step 3: Solve for Velocity

Solution:

The formula for Kinetic Energy is given by:

KE = ½ mv²

where: KE - Kinetic Energy

m - mass

v - velocity

Substituting the value of m and v in the formula,

400,000 J = (½)(500 kg)(v)²

v² = (400,000 J)(2)/(500 kg)

v² = 1600

v = 40 m/s
