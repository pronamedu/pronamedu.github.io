18. Joanna has 2/5 cup of walnuts and 7/5 cups of dried cashew nuts. How many cups of the nuts does she have in all?


Answer and step-by-step explanation:

To find the total of cups of the nuts Joanna have, add the 2/5 cups of walnuts to 7/5 cups of dried cashew nuts.

Add both numerators and copy the denominator.

[tex] \\ \frac{2}{5} + \frac{7}{5} \\ \\ = \frac{2 + 7}{5 } \\ \\ = \frac{9}{5} [/tex]

Joanna have 9/5 cups of nuts in all.

[tex]7x + 9y = 35.50 \\ 7x + 9(x - 2) = 35.50 \\ 7x + 9x - 18 = 35.50\\ 16x = 35.50 + 18 \\ 16x = 53.5 \\ \frac{16x}{16} = \frac{53.5}{16} \\ x = 3.34[/tex]
